Baltic Mill and other musings....

From: mindizney@aol.com | Date: 2004-01-12 22:01:18 UTC
Had a great but COLD weekend out boxing! On Sat. I was able to visit
Butterfly and hear all about her wonderful trip to Utah! I also got to partake in
some goodies brought back from Utah, and her wonderful veg. soup!
YUMMMMMMMMMM!!!!! I also found a couple of mystery boxes that I had been missing, planted
one (I hope they allow it! I have a note in to the manager! Its an indoor
box, which I have a feeling that we will need a lot of this year!), and helped
Bluebird plant four others!

On Sunday, Bluebird and I ventured out to the Baltic Mill (it was still too
cold for Butterfly... but we hope that she'll come with us soon!), and we still
couldn't find the box, since the GPS coordinates given on the informational
site that we used to find W 9 were somewhat off! We gave up the search and
found Salt Rock, Spignesi WMA, Steve's Birthday box, and In the Line of Duty all
in good order. We then decided to head towards home, but found Lions, Tigers
and Bear, Life's Lessons #1, Cats and Dogs, and Tell Tale Apples all in good
order.

I got SO frustrated over Baltic Mill that I decided to get more information
from that informational site and am glad to report that it worked! Today after
school, I ventured out and found the Baltic Mill LB and I Love Lucy! Woo
Hoo! Now, if there HADN'T been any snow, I would have tried for Compass Rose
again, but with the three inches of snow, I don't think I would have seen any
flat rocks...!!!

Thanks for the great boxes everyone!
