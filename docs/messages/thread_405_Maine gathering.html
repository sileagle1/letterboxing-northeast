Maine gathering

From: Phyto | Date: 2004-03-22 15:43:16 UTC
Thanks to all those who made the trek to (southern) maine for the "Letterboxing at
Laudholm" event this past weekend. I'm happy to say that we had around 40 people
attend though we were still missing a few from the north country. Alas, though the
weather was typically new england, I think it remained nice throughout the day on
Sunday.

Thank you to Casco Bay Painter for her creative contributions to the game in southern
maine, thank you to Merenwen and Olwe for their fantastic pouches (putting mine to
shame!!!) and their innovative approaches, and thanks to Deb Blanchard for coming
through on her presentation despite some of the technical challenges. Thank you
AmyMisha for organizing the pre-event dinner and Nautilus and Culex for your
gracious hospitality throughout the weekend :-)

And finally THANK YOU to those who attended making all our efforts worthwhile. It is
so nice to know that others enjoy our wonderful boxes and trails that are so special
to us in Maine. I hope that this will encourage many of you to come up in the summer
and try Deb's "Lakes Region Letterboxing Trail" when it is put out for the season.

For those of you who have digital images please post pictures and/or contact
us when they are ready - I'd love to have some copies.

-Phyto
