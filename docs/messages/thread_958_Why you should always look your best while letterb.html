Why you should always look your best while letterboxing

From: Janet | Date: 2004-07-11 20:25:40 UTC
Yesterday Music Woman & I were out & about RI & CT boxing. I wanted
to sleep in so didn't take a shower before we went out (my thought
was why bother you are going to get get sweating & dirty, no one sees
you in the woods anyway, & I'd rather sleep the extra 20 minutes.)
Well, I was proven wrong.

When we came out from Father's Day flowers & headed for Mt Misery in
Pachaug State Forest we met another couple hiking. The man had a lot
of expensive camera equipment hanging around his neck. As we turned
off the dirt road & started walking up the Mt Misery trail, the man
introduced himself as a reporter from the Norwich Bulletin doing an
article on the Mt Misery trail. He said he would like to follow us up
the trail and take some pictures. I tucked my sweat drenched hair
under my hat & thought, oh my God, why didn't I take a shower this
morning! He spent about a half hour hiking with us snapping away
while I inwardly cringed and tried not to look so much like a wet
dish rag.

He had never heard about letterboxing so we explained its history &
what it was all about. He snapped more pictures of us stamping in
and assured us that letterboxing would be mentioned in the article.

The article will appear in the Norwich Bulletin Sunday paper on
August 8.

So now I know why you should always look your best while letterboxing.

Moonstone
