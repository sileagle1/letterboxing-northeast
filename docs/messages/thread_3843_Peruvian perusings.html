Peruvian perusings

From: Drew Family | Date: 2008-07-09 15:18:44 UTC
Hey, kids, Ned and I are back from our trip to Peru and loving life
here. Working among the poor there really puts things in perspective. I
was in Starbucks this morning (ah, Starbucks!!!) and now I am chomping
on a fresh apple. Mmmm, how I missed those two things.

Ned and I were in Cusco, where we spent about 15 days doing volunteer
construction work bringing in running water and building a bathroom for
a small school up in the mountains above town. It was pretty eye
opening, and we had some great bonding. Of course all the young
Peruvian chicas were in love with my little man.

We also had time to enjoy Lima, Macchu Picu, and the Sacred Valley.
Peru is a must-visit.

No boxing, unfortunately...guess we'll have to try to make the next
cruise for that.

xox, Jay in CT
