more letterboxing fame

From: Janet | Date: 2004-12-31 02:37:12 UTC
Over the past few years, articles about letterboxing have been
written in many local newspapers and national magazines. There have
been articles written in the Providence Journal, Norwich Bulletin,
Hartford Courant, New London Day, Westerly Sun, Time Magazine, US
Airways' Award winning Attache magazine, and I am sure many, other
periodicals. (Who can forget the April 1st a few years ago when
Martha Steward wanted to interview a well known letterboxer causing
such a stir on the talk group). But none of this can compare to the
recent publication.

This month, the Rhode Island AAA magazine, Horizons, contains an
article about letterboxing titled "Treasure Hunting Near Home"
complete with pictures of letterboxers letterboxing. It is a nicely
written article which basically says the same things all the other
articles said, but none the less is nice. The thing I like most
about it is that it is under the heading of "Historic Adventure"
which I think is pretty neat.

Also in this month's AAA magazine, in an unrelated article
(coinsidence ?) in which our own Christopher Martin is quoted for his
interest in movies filmed in Rhode Island. (I agree, Chris, it is
fun to spot RI places on the screen.) People may remember there was
a Providence Journal article written not that long ago that featured
Chris & Rusty Puff and Wanda & Pete.

So hats off to AAA for publishing some interesting articles.

moonstone
