Rocky Neck Letterbox

From: bobsandy98 | Date: 2006-03-12 15:30:56 UTC
While we were geocaching in Rocky Neck March 10th we came across a
letterbox that was sitting in the open near "Tonys Nose"-it looks like
it was part of a series, the box only had an inkpad & an empty
drawstring bag-thought whoever planted it should know that it's
probably not where it should be & without a stamp. We've letterboxed
for years so we feel badly when we see something like this & want
whoever who planted it to know about it. We did come across the final
of that series also by accident (near another cache) but that was well
hidden & in great shape.
