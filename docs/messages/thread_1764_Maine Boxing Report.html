From: weathergleams | Date: 2005-01-19 02:43:48 UTC
The Weathergleams, taking advantage of the fine weather, made a
quick run through the balmy Midcoast this weekend and can report the
following boxes alive and well:

Mount Battie (with ice -- now that's fun)
Andre the Seal (more ice -- another 'gleam goes down)
Wyeth Center (look -- no injuries! The log is full, and the placer
has been notified)
Tropical Maine (sure, why not?)

No luck with Spellcaster (the snow didn't help -- neither did the
point bruise from Battie), and had to save St. George for another
day.

Further south, and reaching back to the dog days of December:

Pettingill Farm (great box!)
Winslow Park (free in winter!)
Skipping Christmas (gone but not forgotten!)

Better box now; pretty soon it might get cold.

'gleams

For the Maine folks on this list....be warned:

Hannaford has LOCK & LOCK plastic locking containers for sale in the .8cup (6oz)
size. I used one of these for the Standpipe here in Bangor, and it's held up very well.
They're 50 cents each right now, so if you're in Maine and you're desperate for micro-
boxes (hmmmm....like nobody I know - ha!) then I suggest you check out your supply
of plastic at Hannaford. They tend to display these on an endcap near the edge of the
isle.
