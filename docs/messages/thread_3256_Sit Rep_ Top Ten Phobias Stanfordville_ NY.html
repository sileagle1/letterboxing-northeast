Sit Rep: Top Ten Phobias Stanfordville, NY

From: AC | Date: 2006-10-09 21:19:51 UTC
What a beautiful fall day! I walked the 5 miles of trail and had a 50% success rate. NOT mind you
because the boxes are missing per se, but due to limited time and, different foliage/time of year,
my senior moments, and near the end exhaustion, I can only at the moment account for 5 of the 10.



Of the 5 unknown, I'm reasonably sure 4 are in place, but due to time constraints, I just didn't put
100% into it. Those rock walls look a lot more alike now then they did a year ago!! There is one
that was originally planted on an old un-maintained trail that has since been rehabbed. It could
still be there, but it was near the end of my day and I just didn't have it in me to give the steep
uphill trek a shot. Not to mention in my infinite wisdom, I hiked the blasted thing backwards.
better on the knees, less so for the sense of placement LOL



SOOOOO all that being said, I'm willing to go back, I believe with more manpower we could find them
with no problem. If anyone is willing to either walk it with me or walk it on their own, I'm
offering a custom carved stamp in their honor. Any Takers????? Otherwise, they'll have to wait
till spring, I'm beat!
