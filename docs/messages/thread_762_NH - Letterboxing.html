Over the long weekend, Brian & I decided to drive up to North Conway,
NH. We did meet Elaine Cat Lover/Teacher/Gardner and her husband
while we there. It really is a small world.

I'd like to get in touch with the Yachtsman & Mary Manatee
as well as the Waterwizards if anyone has their e-mail adresses.
The e-mail for Waterwizards on this group didn't work for me.
So - if you're out there give me a shout.

Early morning on Sunday while looking for a waterwizard's box,
we were blessed by a deer sighting. He was crossing the road.
Afterwards, while driving on a back road, a chicken ran right out in
front of us. We swerved to avoid hitting it and when we looked back
to make sure it was alright, it was running in the other direction.
So, we haven't killed any wild life recently.

Later that day, driving on route 16 towards North Conway, from
Wildcat Mtn, a moose ran right out into the road. He didn't stop to
look both ways but luckily they have a lot of signs in NH that read
"Stop for Moose, it may save your life". Seemed like a good idea at
the time. Anyway, he made it across the street safely before I had
time to get my camera out.

On Monday, we took advantage of the good weather to do a round of
golf. I was going for my three, lined up my shot, swung and didn't
the ball hit a poor little goose right in the leg. I felt so bad.
The little guy was fine, he shook it off and went right back to
eating the grass or whatever it is that they eat but it messed up my
three and I got a five.
