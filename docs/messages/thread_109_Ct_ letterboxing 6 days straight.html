First of all, cogratulations to Swamp Yankee and Sunshine (and Toad
Dog) upon your 500th find! Congrats to Dan, Melissa and Max for the
momentous milestone of 1000!!

These past 6 days, I've met up with people, found hitchhikers and
done box repairs. It has been glorious. I found a number of
Mansfield boxes (new and old), visited Haddam Meadows and Gillette's
Castle in E. Haddam, found Chuck and Molly ;-) in Hebron, along with
a few boxes at Holbrook pond. Visited Pomfret (Bafflin Bird Sanctuary
area) and found Teach and Preach's "We the People" l.b. It was out
in open but is now safely hidden again. I found lots of those
wonderful NeeDeep wildlife series boxes too. Today Hurd Park in East
Hampton was on the itinerary. Found 2 hitchhikers today, 1 yesterday,
plus Lenny and Kathy were kind enough to pass one to me when we met
at Wolf Rock on Dec. 29. It was a pleasure to share the hike with
them to "In Search of Skunk Ape" and to share the honor of being the
first to stamp in. So, from no hitchhikers on Dec. 28, I now have 3,
having re-released one I found just today. I met some new people and
caught up with familiar folks and just plain got out there enjoying
the incredible weather. Wishing you all health and happiness in 2004!
