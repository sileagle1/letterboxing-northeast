Re: [LbNE] Re: URGENT MESSAGE TO CT!!!!!!

From: Pam | Date: 2007-07-23 12:06:51 UTC
Go to Member Services and click on Letterboxes Planted. You will get to a list of your letterboxes. Then for each box, click archive. It will send you to a page where it will say "Yes-archive?" Then press the button.

The reason why you should archive rather than delete is so that 1) All of us who have already found the boxes can keep our logged finds, and 2) You can always unarchive them (doing the same procedure) without having to re-imput the clues.
