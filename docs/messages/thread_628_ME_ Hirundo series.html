Phyto and Isosceles hit the trails today!

I'm happy to say that there have indeed been some finders of my HIrundo series.
This is a 7 stamp series with 6 film canister microboxes and 1 regular sized box.
Sad to report that 1 of the microboxes is missing (Lac d'Or - "Lake of Gold")
however, all the rest are in place and survived the change of season.

While we were visiting this unique area we came across several vernal pools that
were teaming with clusters of frog and perhaps salamander eggs!!! This is very
exciting indeed since I missed "big night" all over the state. While also out
walking the trails we saw evidence of last fall's fungi - a "hens nest" mushroom
cluster as well as 2 butterflies already! Spring has definitely sprung in Maine :-)

Finally, please note that I will be revising the Hirundo clues to make them more
user-friendly. Lac d'Or will be carved and replaced most likely this week.

For anyone that still wishes to torture themselves with the old difficult clue
directions here is the link:
http://home.gwi.net/%7Emoxie1/Clues/HirundoSeries.htm

Cheers!
