From: Kstar | Date: 2004-01-19 03:48:44 UTC
Well, I went out x-country skiing for the afternoon and picked up a
LB in the process. I intentionally avoided all those "bike it or hike
it" locales all summer for days exactly like today!
I'll will never get used to how stunnngly beautiful the woods can be
during a snowfall. Those evergreen hemlocks overhead and the snow and
ice covered brook running alongside the trail...a new england
postcard if I've ever seen one.. but then, you all know what I mean
because you've seen it too:) LB'ing is SO COOL!!
Anyways, afterwards I stopped in to check out the place I thought I
knew but didn't. I had the name wrong, it's called Tweeds. It's a
great place and I hope you'll all enjoy it. Great menu with tons of
seafood, all kinds of sandwichs, 5 different salads, plenty of wraps
and steaks..even veggie burgers.. whatever you like. And the prices
are reasonable too. They'll even hold a function room just for us:)
It's on Rt 9 exactly 1.1 miles west of the Rt135 intersection, and
just before the Rt20 intersection. I've seen about 20 people state
they'd be interested in dinner, so I told her 25 people. I hope that
was a good guess. I'm looking forward to seeing everyone!
