Choi--

As we talked about last night a bit, I find it a bit confusing that when
you click on MA/CT/RI from the big map on the site, you end up at this page:

http://www.letterboxing.org/BoxStateRegion.php?state=mrc

Then, when you click on the state, you go to the state page. However, the
primary image on the page remains the same, it's just that the selected
state is highlighted in the darker shade of light green. But since it's
such a subtle change, I always hesitate and wonder why the new page didn't
load.

I would be happy to split up the three states into separate maps if
everyone thinks that makes sense. Then once you click on MA/CT/RI from the
map, and then click on your chosen state, only that state will appear on
the map.

For CT, my thought is to break it down into the eight counties, and skip
the 4 regions altogether. 1590 letterboxes spread over 9 pages (8 counties
+ mysteries) instead of 5 pages might make them a bit easier to navigate,
especially as the number of boxes in the state continues to grow.

Any objections or better suggestions?

Doug
