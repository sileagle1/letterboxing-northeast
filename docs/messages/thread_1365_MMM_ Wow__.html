MMM: Wow!!

From: cpascott | Date: 2004-10-01 18:28:17 UTC
Wow! I just finished plotting all of the MMM boxes on a map of the
Town of Mansfield. Boy, it is ever loaded!

I was thinking of perhaps adding the locations of the permanent
boxes in the area as well, but as there are literally hundreds of
them, the task was just too daunting.

We were trying to decide whether we should attempt to hunt any
permanent boxes this weekend. We realize that while hunting the MMM
boxes we'll be walking right past some permanent ones, so why not
stop and get them as well, right?

Well, because if we do, we'll be in the same spot for hours!

So, we've made the decision that we will not go after any permanent
boxes unless we accidently stumble upon one. As those boxes will
continue to be there past October 31, and because we couldn't
possibly get most of them in addition to the MMM boxes, our focus
will be solely on the MMM boxes. We'll be lucky to get even a
portion of the MMM boxes anyway!

But I'm curious, for those of you going to the MMM tomorrow (or
rather those going who haven't left yet!):

1. Will you be hunting only MMM boxes or both MMM and permanent
boxes?
2. How many boxes do you anticipate getting this weekend? We're
hoping to take the stamp images from at least 25 boxes this weekend -
- propelling us over the 100 Found mark! (We're at 80 as of today).

See ya there!

CPAScott
