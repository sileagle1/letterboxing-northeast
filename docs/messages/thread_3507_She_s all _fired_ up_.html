Saving TheConnecticutLetterBoxingClub from its spammy demise

From: Silent Doug | Date: 2007-06-01 12:53:07 UTC
As you may know, the old TheConnecticutLetterBoxingClub on Yahoo was
abandoned by its owner and has been taken over by spammers. Since the
owner can't be reached, I've worked with Yahoo to get them to agree
to assign a new moderator for the group so that we can save the
historical archives and messages. In order for that to happen, the
group must elect a new moderator, so there is now a poll going on at
the site for that purpose. We've had a couple of volunteers step up
from the remaining members.

If anyone is a member of the group, please vote here before June 11:
http://groups.yahoo.com/group/TheConnecticutLetterBoxingClub/surveys?id=12585592

If you're not a member, or were a member and left, you can rejoin the
group and cast your ballot to help us out:
http://groups.yahoo.com/group/TheConnecticutLetterBoxingClub/

Thanks.

SD
