Lebanon Boxing Drive details

From: davyschris@aol.com | Date: 2005-02-20 23:01:50 UTC
Hello, all! Here are the long-awaited details of the Lebanon drive. :)

We can convene at the parking lot for Bartlett Brook, where the Sunny Days
series can be found. We'll kick off our event with this series, unless it's
really really snowy. I know it's a series of nine, but it's very quick... don't
even bother to put your stuff away in between. ;)

After this, here is the order:

Pioneers in Lebanon
Lebanon Lions
Baby D turns 1
Hidden Cemetery/Baby D turns 2
Gray Goose in the Town Pound
Red Brick Meetinghouse
Lebanon Loop 1
Tymfurkoffi
Local Heroes
My First Love
Pot O' Gold
Lebanon Loop 2
Lebanon Loop 3
Lebanon Loop 4/Bear Tracks
Castle Scovell
Gray of Liberty Hill

Some of the may look a little "out of order," but there is a method to the
madness. ;)

The parking lot of Bartlett Brook is not paved, so if the weather takes a
turn for the worst, we may have to get together somewhere else. Please keep an
eye on this list (and/or Mark's site) for updates on that.

Questions? Comments? Please don't hesitate to email me.
Davyschris@.... I'd also be glad to give you my phone number if you have anything you want
to discuss, but of course I don't want to post that to this list for the world
to see. ;)

Who's excited?
