Letterboxing Central memorabilia

From: Der Mad Stamper | Date: 2008-08-11 04:25:13 UTC
This is a plea to any of you who have been letterboxing for six years
or more.

My friends and I are putting together some fantastic educational
displays for the "We Live and Breathe Letterboxing" event. Many of
them will relate to the history of letterboxing in America.

One such display will be a collection of letterboxing patches. It was
my intention to keep this somewhat of a secret so it would be a
surprise to those of you attending the event. But we REALLY need your
help, so I'm going to have to let the cat out of the bag...

Although a number of people, including myself, have personally
donated quite a few patches to this collection that are no longer
available, there are still a couple of patches from around 2001 that
we need in order to properly represent the history of our hobby. They
came from your part of the country, so I figured I would just come
right out and ask if anyone has them and would be willing to donate
them to the cause. This collection will debut at the Live & Breathe
event, but it is our intention to make it available for other
gatherings in the future.

The patches in question were from a website called "Letterboxing
Central". We are looking for any patches associated with this site,
but the two we know existed were called "Witches Hollow" and "Pooch
Patch". We've contacted the former owners of the site, and various
other individuals, but have been unable to locate examples of these
patches so far.

If you have these patches, or know someone who might, please contact
me by emailing DERMADSTAMPER at AOL dot COM. This display will help
preserve the legacy of our past, and will include an acknowledgement
of the people who donated items to help make it possible.

Thanks!
Mitch Klink (Der Mad Stamper)
