Saturday's mini-gather

From: Northstar | Date: 2004-03-14 06:09:16 UTC
On 3-13-04 Phynstar and I(Northstar)met up in the parking lot a
little early and hit the trail to collect the Collis P. Huntinton 4
box series before the mini. Glad to report all boxes found and
accounted for & in good shape! What a rough hike, took longer than we
expected! As we approached the 4th box, we met up with the rest of
the gang who were headed in the opposite direction.In attendance were
Letterboxere2002 leading the group, a couple of people from his clss,
(i can't recall their names)Nannybell & The columbian bugs,Just Beth
w/children and last but not least- Team Green Dragon all the way from
Mass!! I didn't expect to see them at all. WE all joined up and
tackled the Peanuts series. Sorry to say, we couldn't find Sally and
Pigpen has been missing for some time now. Of course it was a great
day no matter what. I can't wait to tackle the giant on the 21st.
Wishing everybody great letterboxing days --
Northstar P.S.-- Just a quick note to all
letterboxers-- if you place a box, draw a map so you don't forget
where it is later. HA HA HA(sorry Alan, I had to say something)
