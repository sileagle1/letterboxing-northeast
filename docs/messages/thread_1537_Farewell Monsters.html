Farewell Monsters

From: MayEve | Date: 2004-11-01 01:13:19 UTC
Well, we all knew the Monsters would be with us only a short while,
but the time did fly and now The End is here. It was such fun
dashing out to Mansfield at every chance during October when the
falling leaves welcomed us and the Harvest Moon warned us to get out
of the woods before dark! How exciting to meet up with fellow
boxers on the trails for a quick exchange, a little "Monster Chat"
and then back to Mashing! Now as the Monsters leave the woods of
Mansfield and head towards home, all we have left are their memories
in out logbooks. Good enough for me!! (Although a little sad to see
it all end). THANK YOU, Chuck for a wondrous boxing month!
Till next time.... MayEve
