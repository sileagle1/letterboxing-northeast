Good news to all searchers.
Dave was home on a pass this weekend and we put a new stamp and inkpad in the box.
We really need to educate Geocachers about Letterboxes. A heart magnet was apparently left in place of the somewhat moldy store-bought leaf stamp and well used inkpad. Doesn't seem much of a trade! ;)
But, then from what I've seen of Geocache boxes - there wouldn't be much to tempt me away from letterboxing and the wonderful stamp images I've been able to collect over the years.
By the way, we found the box and it's contents open and strewn about in the middle of the path. It is now well hidden in it's appropriate spot.
Three will have to be the magic number when it comes to von der Insel letterboxes, If this box goes missing again, we will have to call it a 'party spot' and find it a new location at the Sanctuary. It's such a wonderful spot to walk.
