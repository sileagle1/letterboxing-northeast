NY: Taughannock Falls box retired

From: TurtleMcQ | Date: 2005-08-25 16:33:59 UTC
(Cross-posted due to recent discussion of this location/hiking fatality on
various lists)

In light of the hiking death in another section of the same park over the
weekend, I've decided to permanently retire the Taughannock Falls box near
Ithaca. While the box itself is not in a dangerous location, there's a good
possibility that the park rangers will now frown on visitors travelling to
the other side of the stream bed to find the box and I'd rather not
encourage any sort of activity that might get a finder in trouble.

Thanks to all who have visited the box over the past three years -
Taughannock Falls is a truly special place.

TurtleMcQ
