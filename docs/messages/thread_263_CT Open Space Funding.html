From: Steve and Heidi | Date: 2004-02-23 01:20:22 UTC
Added into the files section is a document talking about lobbying
your legislators to push for funding for open space preservation. I
received the document from West Farms Land Trust in Waterford...as
part of this funding WFLT was able to purchase about 1/2 of the
property up at the Avery Tract (where I happen to have quite a few
boxes!).

If anyone likes to politically instigate, or even if you just want
more land purchased to letterbox on, you may want to take a look at
it and make some calls. I'll guess the governor's office is a little
busy given the current "Rowland-gate" investigation...however voices
starting at a local level might get funding secured.
