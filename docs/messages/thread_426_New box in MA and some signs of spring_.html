New box in MA and some signs of spring!

From: Jay and Amy | Date: 2004-03-26 20:40:55 UTC
I just placed the Grouchy Cat Letterbox in Plainville MA. If anyone
is heading to do that letterbox, the Pink Flower Letterbox is in the
same general area.

I saw some definate signs of spring today. While checking on the
Pink Flower LB, I heard the wonderful sound of wood
frogs "quacking". The observation platform along the trail was the
perfect place to listen and spot these harbingers of spring (also
means that there is a vernal pool there...time to dust off my vernal
pool certification stuff, its that time of the year again!!!).

And then, while placing the Grouchy Cat LB, I spotted a garter snake
basking on the rocks at the first stream crossing. I grabbed him
right behind the head to get a closer look but then he did something
that most garter snakes do when bothered...peed. Yuck! I hate that
smell! I quickly let him go and rinsed my hands in the stream.
Aaahhhh, the wonders of nature!

Get out there, box, and enjoy the spring weather!
