Hello,
I have gotten a few e-mails about how much snow Maine has gotten and what
boxes are good to get.
Well the last storm did leave a chunck of snow and the cold weather has
been, well cold. So here are the best boxes that I think you may have some
luck with but there are no garenties.

Carpe Diem - this one might want to be chacked on the morning before the
gathering. :)

Dizzy - I think that this one might be capable of getting at this time with the
snow.

Smiling Dragon - I am not sure, but it seems to be worth a shot

River of Our Love - I have not checked on this box this winter but it is not to
bad of a walk.

Deering Oaks Rose Circle - Is worth a shot with the snow

CBD's Origins Coffee Bar - Is for sure another good idea

There will also be specail boxes planted on the day of the gathering for the
Gorham area. Can't wait to see you all NEXT WEEK!!!!!

Merenwen
