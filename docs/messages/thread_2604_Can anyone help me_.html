From: Kim | Date: 2005-11-28 23:49:46 UTC
My story: A few months ago I made an unplanned stop at the McLean
Game Refuge in CT to find a series of boxes. I had the clues
conveniently filed in a box that I had printed on an earlier date.
(how much earlier?? I have no idea) Well the clues led me through
several beautiful forested miles, past the pond with a small shelter,
and along corn fields, and even to an OLD piece of abandoned farm
equipment. It was a lovely walk, despite my 100% failure to find a
single box! As I was exiting the refuge I passed another couple
heading into the woods, paper in hand:) Clearly letterboxers. I
wasn't sure if I should say anything about my misfortune (and
possibly put a damper their hike plans and boxing hopes) so I decided
to silently walk on.. Anyways I went home , thinking, thinking...
about that couple and their clues.. and so I looked at the clues
online, and egads! they were all different!! Obviously they had been
rewritten since my print date. grrr. I made my mind up that I would
have to go back and do the series, especially since I enjoyed the
hike for it's own sake, so much. Well here I am, ready to get out
there this week, and I can't find the clues. I can't even remember
the name of the series. So, easily, I could be looking in the wrong
place. Can someone please tell me the name of the series? Are the
clues still listed? someone please help me =S K*
