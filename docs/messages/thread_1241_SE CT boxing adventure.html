SE CT boxing adventure

From: thelionandsanddollar | Date: 2004-09-07 01:20:07 UTC
We spent a lovely labor day boxing. We hit the 3rd box in the Thames
Light Series(already had the 1st 2), the Local Islands Series, Pine
Point School, and Drawbridge. All were intact and no problems, other
tahn all being at busy locations. However, at the drawbridge,
apparently, you(someone, may be private?) can keep your kayak behind
where the box is. While we were busy with the box and congratulating
ourselves on how slick we were being, considering how busy it was,
someone went through the other door and ended up washing out his
kayak right behind the spot for the letterbox. That was a little
disconcerting, but not as much as when the water all of a sudden came
out from underneath while he was washing it and we had the box open
on the boardwalk! Everything ended up dry, only the box itself got
wet, but it was a hoot! Also, while we were in the middle of it, a
couple came up to us and asked us to take their picture!! Then, when
we were at the Ram Island box of the local island series, a young man
wrecked his motorcycle not 10 feet from the box(he was fine, but the
bike, I'm afraid, didn't make it). At the Block Island box, there
was a couple parked right in front of the box in camp chairs
reading! Ah the joys of boxing on holiday weekends.
Thanks to all the planters- the boxes were wonderful. It is
great to have so many boxes in the area. Boxing with a 7 week old
baby is indeed a challenge.

ampm(and tm)
Patrick & Amy(and Thomas)
