MA: Quabbin Reservoir Updates

From: Bonnie Sennott | Date: 2004-09-19 17:35:34 UTC
Someone emailed me that the Goodnough Dike letterbox at the Quabbin
Reservoir in Ware, MA, was missing, so I checked on it today. I am happy
to say that reports of its demise are greatly exaggerated. The box is
where it should be, safe and dry, and has been getting visitors regularly.

I can't say exactly why, but I highly recommend that a group of 2 or more
letterboxers visit this box ASAP. You'll be glad you did.

I also checked in on the Find-Me Frog and Misplaced Marbles boxes, at
another spot along the Quabbin in Belchertown, MA. They are both fine, but
the logbook for the frog was soggy, so I replaced it with a new one (now
double bagged).

Happy letterboxing!
Bonnie
