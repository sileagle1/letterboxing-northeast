CT - The Dawnlanders MMM Summary

From: T Prindle | Date: 2004-11-01 16:09:31 UTC
Thank you!!

It's been a Monstrously Memorable Month of letterboxing for The
Dawnlanders. A great thank-you to all the people who put so much
energy into creating, organizing, carving and placing. We think we
got them all (except the missing Grist Mill box) as of yesterday
afternoon, we found 129 boxes. The three bonus boxes were a great
surprise and the two event mystery boxes were still there at the end
of the month! 134 boxes in all if counting the 3 Great Pumpkin
boxes, Phynstar's Halloween Surprise and the Mapline's Coffin event
boxes! Some thirty (?!) miles and one month later we have two log
books filled and lots of great memories! Thanks to all who visited
our Champ and Wendigo boxes (and braved the stream at Sunny Acres 
We saved it for our last!!!).

Rush Gatherer and PowWow Dancer
