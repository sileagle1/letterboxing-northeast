Hi all!

I just spent a beautiful two days at the Chatham Bars Inn as part of
an annual off-site meeting/training. Now, I couldn't go all the way
out there and not take advantage of some of the letterboxes in the
area, could I?

I was restricted to how much letterboxing I could do as my days were
fairly full with all that business stuff and the nights were booked
with pre-planned social events and dinners. (Lobster bakes and open
bars on the company or letterboxing -- hmmm... I like letterboxing,
but, well, you understand!)

Anyway, I did pick up a few, including a collection of hitchhikers.

In good shape:
* Mumma & Bunny Boy's Cape Cod Baseball League Series - Brewster,
Harwich and Chatham (the only three I went after)
* Sadie & Russ' Cape Cod Lifesavers (great walk!)
* Mumma & Bunny Boy's Pleasant Lake HH Hostel -- this hostel actually
contained three HHs:
** Cape Cod Letterboxer's Benny the Bull (taken with me)
** Tweety & Mr. Coon's Ugly Froggie (also taken with me)
** Mumma & Bunny Boy's Doc (left in the hostel)

Of course, I left a HH behind in the hostel as well (I won't say which
one!)

I also found Leader of the Pack's Reunion letterbox. Always great to
find older boxes (this one's from 2001). Great clues!
