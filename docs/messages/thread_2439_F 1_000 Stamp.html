Re: F 1000 Stamp/F500 Box

From: wandaandpete | Date: 2005-10-14 21:04:19 UTC
Well, we'd heard about Mapsurfer's F100 box getting "tied up" by a
few people who were trying to reach F100, while literally hundreds of
letterboxers all around the country who had already reached that goal
were waiting for it, and could have been stamping in! Hopefully,
that box is now well on its way again!

The box that we have been really curious about, though, is
Mapsurfer's F500 box. He had sent it to us, with some pass-along
clues, asking that we try to keep it in chronological order and have
people stamp in their 500th box stamp, if possible. We actually
turned it into a photo album of all the people who had by then
reached F500, along with the date they attained that goal, got most
of them to stamp in at a athering at KJ's in Mansfield,CT, and then
left it in the mailbox at Dan and Melissa's house in Preston,CT (and
we still have the "thank-you" that they e-mailed us for getting it to
them, too!)

Anyway, no one seems to have heard anything about that letterbox
for almost two years now, and quite a few more letterboxers would be
eligible to stamp in by now, too! If anyone cares to do the research
or follow-up to get that Mapsurfer box back in motion, that
would "prolly" be a good thing to do!!!Cheers,

Wanda and Pete in soggy RI
