The orange is great if you are looking for something firm that will hold
VERY small detail. I know I am no where near taking full advantage of
what can be done with it.

I think the white is comparable in texture to the "pink stuff". I get
great image transfers on it. That and the price make it my preferred
block right now. I highly recommend at least trying it. I would be
willing to use the pink stuff if I could buy it locally, but I can't, so
I "buy locally" from another letterboxer and shop at Stampeaz.
