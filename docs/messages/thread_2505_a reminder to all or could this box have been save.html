a reminder to all or could this box have been saved?

From: uneksia | Date: 2005-10-25 17:19:13 UTC
fibrohugs and i have just returned from a search and rescue mission of a few
new hampshire boxes.

stone arch bridge has been recovered. yes, the bridge is damaged and unsafe.
fortunately knowing where the box was i was able to rescue it safely.

franklin pierce was floating in 2" of water. have brought it home also.

wildcat could have been saved if and i repeat IF the last boxer had made
sure it was sealed. the box was found in it's hiding place floating in a
foot of water, the lid off and the stamps ziplock not sealed so the stamp
and inkpad were soaked. the log book was no where to be found.

i can not stress enough.... if you letterbox have respect for the boxes!
have care for that which others left behind for you to find. have courtesy
for the person that will come after you. make sure those boxes are sealed
well and hidden well.

a slightly ticked off
