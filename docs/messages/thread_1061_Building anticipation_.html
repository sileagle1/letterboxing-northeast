Building anticipation!

From: cpascott | Date: 2004-07-29 14:07:25 UTC
Woo hoo! We are ready to plant our first box ... and are planning
on doing so tonight! A second box is in the works, and will be
planted soon as well (if time permits to finish the stamp, it too
may be planted tonight!)

At least one of the boxes will be a mystery box, and we're toying
with the idea of making them both mystery boxes. If not, the one
non-mystery box will have listed in the clues the name of the town
you need to travel to, but will not specifically direct you further
than that -- you'll have to figure out where in the town yourself.
Again, we haven't decided on whether we're going to tell you the
town or not, so be prepared to do some work!

We've decided that our clues will not be straightforward, nor will
they be overly cryptic, but rather somewhere in-between. You'll
have to do a little research before heading out.

Our intention is to place the box (or boxes) and then test the
clues. We haven't decided if we want to find someone else to hunt
for the box before publishing the clues (a beta test) yet or not.

So, that's all for now ... be on the lookout!
