From: ajaomom@aol.com | Date: 2004-08-08 00:26:00 UTC
Hi Everyone,
I just thought i would share the boxes we did this weekend so far, the
successes, and the not so successful.

1. Zodiac #3....Success!!!!

2. Khnum and the Lotus Blossom....Failure on both

3. Zodiac #9....Success!!!!

4. Mountain Park....Failure on all four, although we didn't even try for
all of them, the place seemed very dangerous, what with broken glass, rusty
everything, and very overgrown. #1, we didn't see a gazebo at all, #2 missing,
#3 couldn't find where they were talking about, #4, probably missing, to much
unsafe material, leaves, clothing and trash to even look much. I was kinda
creeped out being up there, the kids loved looking around though.

5. Chickadee....Failure....we couldn't find it, not to mean it wasn't
there, i am not sure we went right at the right fork. oh well, we will try again.

6. Golden Fawn....we had found this on a different trip, and checked while
we were there, it was there, along with a few thousand mosquitos, we didn't
sign in just verified it was there and all was good.

7. The Horse Escape....Success!!!!.........and there was only maybe 1
thousand skeetos at this one, lol. Thanks for the treat!!!!

8. Hampton Ponds....Failure, my clue sheet was old, and i checked when we
got home and found that it was confirmed as missing.

I think that's all of them, if anyone has any info on our failures, if they
didn't find them either, or our successes if they know they are there and we
just have to look better. We had a nice 2 days of boxing!!!

Aj and Munchkins
"The Letterbugs"
