From: LundyandVickster@aol.com | Date: 2006-04-12 00:21:22 UTC
Jayme,

We found the following boxes (apparently last finders) before the winter:

Susie Sells Seashells - Good Condition with a cute story behind it when you
find it.

Treaty of Portsmouth - Orion said in an earlier email that it got buried
under a landslide and will be replaced elsewhere in the future. It's too bad
because it is a very nice walk in a beautiful area.

Live Free or Die - Good Condition and I think it is one of the most visited.
It is in a very cool area. The logbook is full. Bring an index card or
something.

If you have time and energy there are a few in Kittery, ME that are in great
areas and are worth seeking.

Happy Hunting!

Larry
Lundy and Vickster
North Shore, Massachusetts
