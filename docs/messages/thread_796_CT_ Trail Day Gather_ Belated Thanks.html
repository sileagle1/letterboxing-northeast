CT: Trail Day Gather: Belated Thanks

From: mindizney@aol.com | Date: 2004-06-09 01:21:16 UTC
I would like to thank Alan for putting together the great Trail Day Gather in
Danbury this past weekend! Though I came fashionably late (and yes, I was
fashionable this time too... I was in an oriental shirt and pant ensemble due to
a school concert earlier in the day) and missed most of the activities, it
was great to camp with many great old friends and some new ones as well! I
thoroughly enjoyed the many event stamps that came around the camp fire, along
with the letterboxing stories that came along with them! The "Things That Go
Bump In The Night" series, that we did by head lamp, was awesome! If I could be
assured that I wouldn't get arrested, I'd go night boxing more often!

Also, surprisingly enough, it didn't rain much (finally started to pour, as
we sat around the camp fire, at about midnight, so we called it a day), only
raining during the night. I'm almost thinking about doing some camping this
summer (for those who really know me, you know that I usually DON'T like to
camp!). It was actually kind of fun!

Thanks Alan and EMS for a great day!
