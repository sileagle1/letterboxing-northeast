Hitchhiker Hostels in New England

From: cpascott | Date: 2006-06-14 18:28:05 UTC
Hey all ...

Saw on the new boxes thread that a new hitchhiker hostel, the
Blackstone Valley HH Hostel, was recently placed
(http://letterboxing.org/BoxView.php?
boxnum=22928&boxname=Blackstone_Valley_Hithhiker_Hostel).

The placer indicates that the idea came from seeing HH Hostels in
other states but not in MA.

Just to clarify, for those who may not know, including the above MA
has 3 active HH hostels, as follows:

* Oldhounder & Siamese's "Picking Apples with Sarah" (mystery
location)
* Songbird & Eagle Eye's "T-Stop" (Ashland, MA)

* the Blackstone Valley HH Hostel listed above.

2 other HH hostels did exist, but are now either missing or retired:

* Mumma & Bunny Boy's "Pleasant Lake" (Harwich, MA)
* Twinamals' "Christmas Spirit" (N. Billerica, MA)


Additionally, this is a list off all published HH Hostels in New
England:

CT
* J. Peter's "Knight Templar" (Norwalk, CT)
* Susan's "The Spot" (Enfield, CT)
* Fish-or-Man's "Camping Out Under the Stars" (Killingworth, CT)
* JBean's "Cozy Cavy" (Portland, CT)
* Leader of the Pack's "The Inn of Winter" (Mansfield, CT)

RI
* Wanda & Pete's "New Beginning" (Hope Valley, RI)
* Wanda and Pete's "The Inn of the End" (Charlestown, RI)

ME
* YahYah's "Mid Coast" (Bowdoinham, ME)
* Nautilus & Culex's "Transportation" (Wells, ME)

There are no HH hostels that I know of in NH or VT.



CPAScott

An update:

Note that "Christmas Spirit" was a winter-only hostel & no
hitchhikers were lost when it went missing.

The "Dyslexic Queen" hostel nearby is part of the "Octopus" placement
by Morgun. ATM you don't have to leave a replacement, since the two
HHs there are due to move. When they are replaced, the hostel has a
one HH minimum. This hostel is hard to find (as are the clues to
Sarah), but I would be happy to help anyone who has a problem
locating it. When you finally find it, you can return fairly easily.
Morgun (most Morgun boxes are now listed under "Tiny Turtle" "by
Morgun")
