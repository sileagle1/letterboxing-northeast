Jellybean,
Are you the one who emailed me about the Chatfield Trail?
(accidentally deleted it!). Trails at Chatfield Hollow State Park
would be better. Each has some rocks/climbs, but I used to take
my second/third grade scouts there. Since the gate is closed at
this time of year, it makes any hike longer. There is a nice nature
trail, but again, some ups and downs and rocks.
The Chatfield Trail's last box is missing; I just got its
replacement made, but it is not out yet. One hiker wrote in the
now-missing logbook that they saw a ratttler up there, and there
is a lot of climbing over and through rocks up there!
Just come to the gather at Chatfield Hollow that Lou and I are
doing on Nov. 13. Everyone brings a food item to share; I will
have a big pot of stew and one of vegetable soup. There are
already lots of boxes there, but there will be a new treasure trail
with patch "treasure", plus some just for the day, and an event
stamp. See you then!
