From: rospa10 | Date: 2004-07-27 01:09:00 UTC
Thanks to the encouragement of a fellow poster I went back for
the "Pine For You" letterbox and found it this time! As for the newly
replaced "Opening Day"..that's another story. Where is that darned
fallen tree?? I shall return!! I spent some time at Wolf Rock,
stamping into a few boxes right behind Mama Fox. Too bad we did not
meet on the trail. "Inky Fingers" is really cute, and a clever idea
to dedicate a box to this common side affect of l.b.ing. On to my
very first find in the fine new Peanuts series by the Maplines.
Thanks for the background info. on our favorite comic
series. "Schroeder" was playing a fine classic piano piece as I
opened the box. Outer bag already has a busted zipper, so if anyone
plans to visit soon perhaps you could bring a large sized ziplock
bag? My repair kit did not have a suitable sized one. On to "Wild
Black Bagels". Curses, foiled again!! A very pleasant young couple
were busily setting up their gear to go rockclimbing off the side of
Wolf Rock. I hung for a few minutes asking about their hobby then
returned to my car. To repeat, I shall return!!
