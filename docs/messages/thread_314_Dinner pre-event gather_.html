From: Mark Pepe | Date: 2004-03-03 03:22:47 UTC
Hi Nautilus & Phyto:

When I started this dinner thing, I never meant to add to your
workload! You two probably have enough to do. I called it a "pre-
gathering" dinner only for lack of another word - never meaning a
gathering sanctioned event! It all started out as an informal event -
but as you both know, gathering fever is contagious!

There are 10 of us already willing and able to go to dinner - all of
us staying at the Comfort Suites in Biddeford - 2 Exits up from the
event!! ;)

So far, we are: TeamGreenDragon, Solar & Binx, RustyPuff & CSCM,
Amymisha & CameraObscura and Sue and myself. Does that sound like a
party or what? Think we'll be talkin' about the Red Sox's chances at
the pennant this year?

Again, don't feel you both have to go crazy making arrangements. Some
or most of us are planning on letterboxing our way up from the Boston
area Saturday morning and are not quite sure where we may end up. I
would suggest the Biddeford/Saco area also for dinner so that we can
check into our hotel, clean up and have time to make the dinner - but
not so early as to cut into our boxing time!!

Have fun and just get out there & box!!! [And meet us for dinner
wherever!]

Mark Pepe

http://pepeanddavidow.blogspot.com/
