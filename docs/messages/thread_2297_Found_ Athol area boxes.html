From: cpascott | Date: 2005-08-15 13:45:31 UTC
Hi all ...

Great Saturday spent 'boxing! Thanks to Choi who accompanied me on a
hunt for a box I can't mention here (sorry -- but don't you love
mysteries).

After Choi and I parted company, I hiked up Tully Mountain (Orange,
MA) and got the letterbox on the peak -- in fine shape, but the clues
are a bit tough -- "look for the double trunked oak growing next to a
rock" -- uh, there were about 20 of them ... LOL. Persistence paid
off, though! Nice views from up there.

I then went after lazy letterboxer's Lightening My Load series in
Athol. All 7 boxes intact. Along the way I ran into Sue from Pelham,
MA. I don't think she has a trail name other than that but she said
she's been 'boxing for 4 years now. This was actually my first
encounter meeting another letterboxer on the trail outside of
gatherings (the others were on the trails in Mansfield last October
which isn't qutie the same). She was with what I presumed to be her
husband, and we finished the series together.

I told her she should come to a gathering, but she won't be able to
make it to Moose Hill this weekend -- she's quite pregant and due this
week!! She may be able to make it to the gathering on October 1st though.
