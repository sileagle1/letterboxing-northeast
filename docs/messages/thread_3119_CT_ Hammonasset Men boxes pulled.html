From: sadie_russ | Date: 2006-08-07 22:14:27 UTC
With sadness we announce the retirement of the Hammonasset Men boxes.
These were our first plants and Sadie's first carvings.

Maine Man was becoming increasing difficult to find due to the foliage
growth in the area and now Marco Man's hiding spot has been
compromised.

Thanks to Gingerlbd for alerting us to the situation and doing her
best to hide Marco Man out of sight. Both boxes are safely home now.

Thanks to all who visited over the past three and a half years.
We might plant something else out that way!

Sadie & Russ
