Re: [LbNE] Re: Ice Scream Gathering....Weather

From: Team Randalstik | Date: 2006-04-22 21:27:50 UTC
ooo. That weather report doesn't look good.

Well, I will just say this. I was over in the next town over
(letterboxing of course, looking for our 100th) and it was suppose to
rain in the afternoon. Well, it was afternoon and there wasn't a drop
to be seen. In fact there were even breaks in the clouds and the sun
shone.

Of course, tomorrow the reports seem to say it is going pour. So will
it pour or just drizzle? Sadly, I have seen the weather reports be so
wrong sometimes. Pouring rain is predicted, and instead it is
actually a sunny day. We shall see. Ahh, New England.

In either case, I am excited about putting more faces to trail names
and eating LOTS of ice cream. I think I need to wear my silk long
underwear so that I stay warm so I can eat even more ice cream
without temperature concerns. I'll be the one in the corner of the
barn with a wool hat and mittens with hot fudge and carmel sauce
dribbling down my chin. Also, you will be able to recognize my stamp
in your logbook because I'll simply stamp with the Martha Stewart
Praline sauce that someone is bringing.

Team Randalstik
