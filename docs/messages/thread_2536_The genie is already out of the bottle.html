The genie is already out of the bottle

From: Warrior Woman | Date: 2005-11-04 00:54:49 UTC
The genie has already been out of the bottle for a long long time
regarding letterboxing and publicity. Articles are published with or
without anyone's permission, from AAA to The Boston Globe to Time
Magazine.

I've been planting letterboxes in three states since 2001, and I have
yet to see any vandalism done to any of my boxes since the influx of
publicity.

I've got my boxes listed on both LbNA and AQ. I have never pulled my
clues and I don't plan to, either. I'm of the mindset that I want my
boxes found, and I deliberately plant them so that parents with
children can find them with undue hardship. I plant for *everyone*.

I do, however, applaud the creative letterboxers who have taken this
hobby to a different, underground level. I've even been selected to
receive some very very *very* special WOM clues, for which I feel
quite honored.

This hobby is continually evolving, and we should celebrate each
new "twist" as it comes about. Celebrate diversity in letterboxing -
from plain old traditional LbNA clues to mystery boxes to AQ to
private websites to WOM boxes. There's room for it all!

Warrior Woman
Equal Opportunity Letterboxer
