From: mainekokopellian | Date: 2005-10-05 19:16:10 UTC
AS the time grows near for another gathering in the State of Maine,
the organizers would like to announce that at the event there will
be 2 costume contests...

One for the Kids, and one for the adults

Both contests will begin at 2 PM. This is to allow people to be able
to change into costumes & back to regular wear for boxing & other
surprises.

For the kids the Grand prize will be a Build a Bear specially themed
for Halloween. There will be other prizes as well!!!

For the adults we have just added another category for the contest.
A special prize will be given to the costume MOST resembling a
LETTERBOXING TRAIL NAME ( hopefully other than your own)! This is
meant as a fun creative twist on traditional costume contests...

Remember that costumes can make great hiding spots for boxes:)

Some more surprises may be leaked out shortly. We hope anyone
considering coming will email myself or oceanwytch on yahoo. We also
have RSVP list set up on atlasquest.....
