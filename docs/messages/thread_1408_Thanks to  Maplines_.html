Tatanka and I went for the remote, rougher terrain boxes on the
Friday before and the Sunday after the Event. We didn't run into
anyone at *all* on the trails. In fact, we were the first finders on
a lot of these boxes (Who Let The Dogs Out, in particular, which we
did first in honor of Tatanka's dog, Shane).

As for the boxes at the event (The Great Pumpkin, The 10 Monsters),
there was *NO* attempt at discretion! Huge hordes of folks went out
in packs. It was hysterical!

Our F count isn't nearly as high as the rest of the bunch because we
did have Shane with us, and we deferred to his liking (long long
hikes instead of in-and-out-of-the-truck). He was one tired dog at
the end of the day!

Warrior Woman
(boxing as Lakota that day)
