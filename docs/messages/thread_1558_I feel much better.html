From: Dave and Elaine | Date: 2004-11-06 14:53:33 UTC
Here at Thunderhooves World Headquarters, we've been following this
thread with great interest, and I've noticed that no two
letterboxers seem to approach counting in quite the same way. The
Thunderhooves approach is simple -- LIE! Rainy day, slept late,
can't find your shoes? No problem, just say you went out anyway.
You can letterbox 24 hours a day, 365.25 days a year, in any
weather, from the privacy of your own home. The monetary savings in
logs, ink, stamps, etc. are astounding. If you want to start lying
about geocaching, the savings are even greater.
