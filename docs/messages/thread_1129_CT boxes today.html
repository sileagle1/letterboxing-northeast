I plan to return to the park to do "Ivy League" soon! (especially since my son just got over a nasty case of poison ivy) The kids weren't up to it that day. Beatiful trails at that park! The kids played on the bridge and my son tried to catch a few frogs.

How does one get their hands on these unpublished mystery clues? This letterboxing has so many facets to it!

My 2 year old daughter cracked me up on Saturday. While walking along the trail she came to a large rock on the side of the trail and stopped and said, "Box mommmy!" while trying to lift the rock (which was almost as big as she was). I told her that the directions said to keep going, but she was mad, insisting there should be a box under that rock! Maybe I'll have her pick the spot for our first plant!
