Here are our suggestions. You can use these in conjunction with the
clues to the current DEP box and the series to (hopefully) find all
the Nepaug Series boxes! The DEP box used to be much closer to the
parking lot and was moved when they updated it with the new stamp.

- Follow the clue for the DEP Nepaug State Forest box, but stop
right before the power line cut -- box #2 is on the left side of the
trail before the cut. (Note: The trail here is the Tipping Rock Loop
of the Tunxis Trail -- the CT Forest & Parks Association was to add
colored dots to the light blue blazes on the side trails in Nepaug in
preparation for the release of the new CT Walk Book, but they haven't
yet done this trail when we were there, so I can't say what color the
dot may be!)

- Continue to the DEP box.

- Continue to box #3.

- Now, return the way you came, back on the trail to the road, and
turn right. (This is the blue-blazed Tunxis Trail.) Follow the trail
blazes on the road and go left at the first intersection. Continue to
follow the blue blazes along that road to the next intersection. This
is the "Y in the road" in the clue. The tree just to the left of the
rocks rocks now has a dark blue blaze on it, and there is another
trail to the left of the tree/rocks, you need to take the trail
between the two rocks to the right of the tree. This trail was easy
to see and follow up the hill now, but that may change in the summer
when the grass grows up.

- After finding box #1, just continue on that trail -- there are
now no downed trees blocking the path. When you get to the forest
road, turn right and follow it down to the parking area.

We did email this note to the planter but they never responded. If
you have any updates to these notes, let me know, and I'll see if I
can't get a note added to the clue on the letterboxing.org web site.

Cheers!

SD
