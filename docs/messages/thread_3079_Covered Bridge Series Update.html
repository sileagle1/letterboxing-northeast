Covered Bridge Series Update

From: Jayme Duggan | Date: 2006-07-22 01:50:14 UTC
Just wanted everyone to know that the Covered Bridge Series, by Son of Sailor, is in the process of being retired. The Mystery Bridge, Cilleyville and Kenniston were already MIA. Blair Bridge (original log book missing, kindly replaced by a visitor) and Squam Bridge were picked up/retired today. Tannery Hill will most likely be retired on Monday. We thank all of you who visited the bridges and sent us comments. The clues will be updated to reflect that they have been retired. It may be Monday before that happens.

Thanks again and happy boxing.

until we meet again

From: Josef Carlson | Date: 2006-07-22 02:25:59 UTC
I hope that all of you have been have a terrific summer! In spite of the ticks, mosquitos, and poison ivy I'm sure all of us have tried to make the most of the great weather by playing in the woods. Even though I learned about the sport/hobby/pastime/addiction of letterboxing from a student of mine years ago, I still feel the excitement that comes from turning over a stone and finding treasures. Little did I know during my first quest (a dismal rainy October) that my life would never be the same again! I quickly fell in love with the creativity, mystery, and thrill of accomplishment that comes with this fantastic activity. Now I am getting ready to take part in a new opportunity, one that will allow me to live in and explore another region of the world. Though I am very excited about the adventures I will be experiencing, I'll miss family and friends. I know that whatever directions my life's path takes, when I eventually return I'll be able to pick things up where I
left off. I will surely miss the weekly adventures into the unknown and the kind, creative people I've met over the years. You are all amazing! Until then I wish everyone good health, happiness, and continued enjoyment with letterboxing. Take care...and spread the word!
Peace, friendship, and inky hands,
Josef ("hello...from Joe!")
West Bridgford, Nottingham, UK
