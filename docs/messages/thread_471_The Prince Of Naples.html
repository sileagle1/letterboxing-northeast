The Prince Of Naples

From: buddyandpigtographer | Date: 2004-04-04 23:28:14 UTC
The Prince of Naples was out in the rain today!! Despite the muddy
terrain, we found the boxes with TeamGreenDragon - getting there at
about 2:15pm, we were, needless to say - late! Hahaha...it was great
to see a lot of the more familiar NE faces (well, familiar to us!) -
Wanda & Pete, TGD, The Pepes, Butterfly, Bluebird, Sadie & Russ,
Northstar, Phynstar, Solor & Binx, Music Woman, Rubaduc,
Letterboxer2002...

...and the person who made an extra special appearance - I'll keep
that under wraps until someone else reveals the identity of this
elusive boxer...

...We also met a few boxers we havn't seen before - Wolfy, Mingo,
Monarch, Fire Lady, D-Ant, Nico and Tiger & BuzzBee.

How many states were being represented today? RI, MA, CT, NC, NY,
any others?

We had a great time!! After walking through the mud, I now know it's
time to get new sneakers, as well as a pair of hiking boots...(is
there an EMS in Rhode Island???)

And I have to now start moving my stamps to another, more sturdy
notebook - my pages are full and are ready to be cut and pasted into
the new one.

Thanks to the Pepes for putting this Mystery Ungathering on!! And it
was great to see everybody - and meet a few new people as well!!

Buddy
