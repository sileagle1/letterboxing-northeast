Fenton-Ruby Park, Willington

From: rospa10 | Date: 2004-03-21 04:12:50 UTC
I had such a fine and productive day in this gorgeous Joshua's
Trust preserve. My intent was to find the series entitled "State
Symbols" placed by Willington Gang, plus pick up a few other boxes
along the way. The hike up (and down) the Ridge trail to box #1 was a
tad slippery, but found box, intact, with a well carved stamp of our
state flower. On to #2 of this series- this box was also in good
shape but the logbook is almost totally full. I stamped on the back
side of a page which found empty when flipping through the logbook.
Stamps in this series are very well carved plus there is historic
info. provided at beginning of logbook. I do hope the placers will
get more boxes out on the trails for us to find, please?
At this point I decided to stamp into other l.b.s off the same
trail I was hiking. "In Search of Phantom Kangaroos", recently placed
by Chuck and Molly is doing fine. NeeDeep's "Delaware Stamp" is also
intact. Both of those stamp-ins add to my tally of gathering the
stamps of favorite series placed placed locally.
At this point I ran out of steam and time before dusk.
I shall return to this gorgeous park to hike on the Ruby Trail and
find boxes 3-5 of the State Symbols series. Hopefully there will be
time to complete the Julia's Trail loop also.
