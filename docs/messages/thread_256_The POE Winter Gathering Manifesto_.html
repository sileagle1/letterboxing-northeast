The POE Winter Gathering Manifesto!

From: Warrior Woman | Date: 2004-02-21 20:48:24 UTC
Winter Gathering 2004
"Peace on Earth"
Westborough, MA

When: Sunday, February 29, 2004
Time: 10 am to 2 pm
Where: "Peace on Earth" Letterbox
Hosted by: Warrior Woman and Farmer Harvey (in absentia)

The Directions:

Follow the directions to the "Peace on Earth" letterbox or
Get to the intersection of Route 495 and the Mass Pike
Take Route 495 North to Route 9 West (exit 23B - Worcester)
Travel on Route 9 West for 3.5 miles
Take Route 9 West to Route 135 East - Westboro
Travel on Route 135 East for 1.5 miles toward the center of Westboro
You will come to a rotary at the intersection of Route 135 and Route
30
Stay on Route 135 East (this time the sign will say "Hopkinton")
From the rotary, travel on Route 135 East for .5 miles
The entrance to Harvey's Farm is on your left, Maple Street, marked
by a green propane tank sign

Main Greeting Table:

The Event Stamp (most excellently carved by Brian of TeamGreenDragon)
The Event Log (please remember to stamp in)
The "Peace on Earth" Letterbox (its hiding place won't withstand the
traffic of an event)
The Hitchhiker Hostel (deposit your HHs here - take one for each one
you leave)
The Name Tags (helpful to put Trail Names to faces)

Food Table:

Two conference tables
Plenty of electrical outlets for crockpots, etc.

What to Bring:

Your letterboxing gear, plus clues to the area letterboxes
Food! for the potluck luncheon table
Any accessories needed for your food (utensils, plates, bowls, cups,
etc)
Your camera!

What Not to Bring:

Please, at the wishes of Farmer Harvey, no pets of any kind. For
this event, your four footed loved ones will need to stay at home.

Where to Stamp:

Outdoor picnic tables
Inside greenhouse picnic tables (in the event of inclement weather)

Other amenities:

One portalet (yes, you heard me right)
One country gift barn (stamp and shop!)

Any questions? Email me at warrioringilead@...
