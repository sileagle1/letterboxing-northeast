Pulling the box because it was not rehidden punishes the next boxer,
not the last boxer.

Here is what I did:
I was bringing out a box that was taken in for the winter, and
noticed that a box near it was in plain sight - no rehidding
whatever.

Instead of posting my clues, I posted comments about rehiding & a
link to the rehiding paragraph on LbNA, and asked people to email me
with their trail name for the clues. Predictably - no one emailed
me - no problems.

If someone has done 1/2 the boxes in a park without rehiding, then
making the clues hard to get for the rest of the boxes sends a clear
message.
