Sit Rep ... White Mountains and north (NH)

From: cpascott | Date: 2005-09-07 13:23:10 UTC
Hey all ...

Just came back from a long weekend camping in the White Mountains.
Temperatures got a little cold at night (into the 40s), but it was a
nice weekend none-the-less.

I was hoping to do a bit more boxing than I did, but I was with
non-letterboxers and forewent some boxing in lieu of other things. Ah
well, the mountians will certainly still be there and hopefully so
will the letterboxes!

What I did get follows.

* Seal of Berlin (Wanda and Pete)
* The Flower Fairy (Letourneau Letterbox) -- this box was located on
private land. It was nice to meet the people who ran the greenhouse
where the box was located. They recognized me instantly as a
letterboxer and before our visit was up had sent me on my way with a
box full of tomatoes and peppers!
* Diana's Baths (Tweety and Mr. Coon) -- this box is all but ruined.
The logbook is soaked beyond repair, and the box itself is damaged and
in great need of replacement or repair.
* Diana's Bath Too (Mud Bowl Gang) -- met a new letterboxer on the
trail for this box (The Nicol Family). They only had about 8 finds,
but looked to enjoy the hobby. Welcome!
* Fall Foliage (tigquilt)
* Cathedral Ledge series (3) (JP) -- the view from the top of this
ledge is spectacular!
* Swan Ride (MaineKokopellian)
* Humpty Dumpty (Oceanwytch)
* Eastern Slopes Inn Riverwalk (Tweety and Mr. Coon) -- this logbook
is pretty full.

I also went after Thompson Falls Wildcat, but couldn't find it.


Thanks!
