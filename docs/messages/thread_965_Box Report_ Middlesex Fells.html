The three boxes in Middlesex Fells posted on the LbNA website were
found in fine shape yesterday. Also found was Rag Rock in Woburn.
For anyone going into Middlesex Fells and intending on hiking to each
box rather than driving, we recommend a trail map! Without one our
anticipated 3 hour hike turned into 6. We were tired after that one!

Re: Box Report: Middlesex Fells

From: cpascott | Date: 2004-07-13 14:09:30 UTC
Uh huh. The problem is that there are a number of trails that
apparently are now "restricted" due to their proximity to the
reservoir. My guess is that they reruited some of the major trails
to stay outside of the restricted area. I suppose we could have
simply stayed on the couple of blazed trails rather than travelling
on the other trails, but what fun would that be?

If I had any suggestion for the reservation, though, I'd encourage
the use of intersection references -- similar to the Blue Hills.
That would help ...

It's a nice reservation, just be aware of the trail challenges. A
map would do wonders! Apparently the Department of Conservation and
Recreation (which manages the Reservation) doesn't sell trail maps
anymore, though.

CPAScott
