We all have -someone- to thank for putting us on to letterboxing. Very few of us (okay, so Diane's brother) just stumbled upon a letterbox without help or encouragement.
"As the twig is bent, so grows the tree" - if people discover letterboxing and the courtesies of it early on, then we can hope that they will be more respectful than some boozing youngsters who stumble upon a box and make mischief.
Besides, it's in the holiday spirit to share good things...
