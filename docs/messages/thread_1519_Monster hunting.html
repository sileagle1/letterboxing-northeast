Monster hunting

From: lord_ladybug | Date: 2004-10-28 13:34:22 UTC
We are relative newbies (2 months) at letterboxing. We went down to
Mansfield (from western MA) early this week to hunt monsters before
they all disappeared forever. We had a wonderful time, and wanted
to thank all the organizers and monster makers for doing such a
phenomenal job. It is impressive to see how much effort and
creativity went into making the Monster Mash the success it has
been.

Because we live in an area with few letterboxers (apparently), we
have never met another boxer on the trail. What a difference in
Mansfield! We want to tell everyone we met on the trails and in the
General Store how much we enjoyed meeting and exchanging with you.
It's fun to put faces to names you know so well from the chat
board. And everyone was so nice!

All in all, a terrific visit. Thanks, Everyone.

Lord and Ladybug
