**2/8/10
 
It seems the trail clearing work has begun in some parts of the state during the middle of Winter. While out walking the pups yesterday, my husband and I were amazed at the clearing going on at this particular mystery location.
 
We tried to find the correct tree, but it had been cut down and chipped. We found the remnants of the box-baggies, plastic, paper towels etc. Then we noticed the log book had been stuck in the crook of another tree.
 
We were about to give up on the stamp when our letterboxing dog, with a nose for all things plastic and rubber, came across it. Thanks Doodles!!!**
 
This box has been retired.
