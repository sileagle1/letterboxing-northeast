You can do it by a) look for the directions to my "Peace On Earth"
letterbox or b) follow the directions below:

Since we have folks coming in from all directions, I'll try to make
this fairly universal:

From your starting point, you need to get to Route 495 in Westborough.

If you are coming in from the west or east, you can take the Mass
Pike to Route 495 North. If you are coming in from the north or
south you can take Route 95 to Route 495.

Once on Route 495, take the Route 9 West exit. Follow Route 9 to
the intersection of Route 135 in Westborough. I will have to double
check if this is an EW or NS route.

You will want to head toward Westborough center on Route 135. Be
careful of the rotary - stay on 135! Harvey Farm is on Route 135 in
Westborough, less than a mile from that blasted rotary. The actual
entrance to the parking lot is on Maple Street, which is on the left.

I hope this is helpful for everyone! Can't wait to see everyone!
