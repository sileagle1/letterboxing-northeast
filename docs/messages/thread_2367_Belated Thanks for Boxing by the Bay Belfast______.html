Very Very Belated Thanks for Boxing by the Bay Belfast..........

From: Danelle Baldwin | Date: 2005-09-12 16:00:52 UTC
We have to agree wholeheartedly with all of the kudos
and thankyous for Boxing by the Bay. The clues were
amazing and our little spider still has "his" treasure
map and woe betide anyone who tries to move it. :)

We, alas, were also plagued with car trouble. We
spent most of the day in the parking lot at AutoZone
fixing the brakelines of our car. We are forever
indebted to the good people there for going way past
above and beyond to help us poor travelers. With
their help we were able to eventually stroll into the
park to see our boxing friends.

Although, we missed many, we were welcomed and
befriended by those still there. Our day truly got a
lot brighter and we had a wonderful time boxing into
the evening. A huge THANKYOU to Phyto and Iscoceles
for helping to turn a horrible day into a good one.
The boxing and the friends old and new were wonderful
as always.

We finished out the rest of our Maine vacation without
mishap and returned home with many new boxes and
stories to go with them. We look forward to boxing in
Maine, again.

Thanks again!!!!
Danelle
