For the benefit of CPAscott,my esteemed Haunted Hayride compatriot,
who raised the question, and others who are wondering.. Cereal
Monsters was created as a series for the Frightnight Gathering. Due
to planting space ( at the picnic area) and time constraits, they
were not planted in separate boxes. The intent in their new location
is to have one logbook and separate box locations along a central
route. In much the same way some urban series have 1 logbook and
several microboxes,(One prime example is nearby in Portland), this
is the intention of the creator (myself) of this series.

With the number of boxes hidden in the area around the gathering I
chose to put the stamps into one letterbox...
One can choose to log it accordingly. The series will be planted in
Maine and in separate boxes BUT 1 logbook.....

I thank my esteemed colleague, CPAscott for posting this question to
the board. Anyone with other questions about the Frightnight boxes
please email me directly....
Your Humble Mainekokopellian
