MayEve and I are thinking of organizing a Drive for April 23rd or
30th in Hartford County. I am told the first drive was great fun,
and the second drive in Lebanon was a blast!!!

Krusty Krab (my daughter and MayEve's niece/godchild) turns six on
April 27, and MayEve and I are thinking of planting a 7 or 8 box
series -- The House Of Mouse -- in Hartford County, and debuting it
for the April Drive in honor of Krusty Krab's birthday. There may
also be a few other surprise new boxes on the agenda, as well as
some that were not sought in the First Drive.

If there is interest in doing it MayEve and I will get to carving
and organizing!!! Please let me/us know by posting, or by personal
email. I think it would be a great time. Thanks. ---Wild Rover

Re: Anybody interested in an April Boxing Drive ??? -- Update

From: wildrover351 | Date: 2005-03-25 16:51:56 UTC
As luck would have it, I knew there was a May Day Gathering planned,
and for some reason my brain just did not realize it was on May
Day!!! By the time I was informed I had gotten many responses
expressing interest in an April Drive, both on the LBNE message
board and privately, and the overwhelming majority favored
03/30/05. As I said in a private e-mail to Donutz, which was sent
to Momma Fox, I am hopeful that all are of the opinion that there
can never be too many boxing events, but if the organizers of the
MaY Day Gathering do not give their blessing for the April Drive
going forward on 03/30 I will respectfully alter my plans, re-group
and plan for 03/23 instead.

As for progress with the April Drive, be it on 03/30 or 03/23,
Chrissy has offered her unparalleled experience in organizing
these "Drives" to assist our efforts. Also, Music Woman has
graciously offered to help, which is phenominal especially because
I think the Drive will center in Glastonbury (after starting in the
Capitol City for the unveiling of a new series by MayEve, RTRW and
Wild Rover), and Music Woman is second-to-none in Glastonbury
boxing. I also received many offers to carve an event stamp, for
which I am grateful, but I believe RTRW will be carving the event
stamp. MayEve is re-planting her "Good Grief" series in time for
the event, and I am planting the "House Of Mouse" series in
Glastonbury to debut for the Drive [with the new series in Hartford
and the "House Of Mouse" series there will be 12+ never-before seen
boxes debuting at the Drive]. Music Woman and Chrissy will be
helping to formulate a plan from there.

I hope to have a chronological list of planned boxes along with
directions and my cell number available in advance so anybody
wishing to join en route can do so. Thank you to all who have
replied supportively!!! Once I have heard from Momma Fox I will
determine the date to be 03/30 or 03/23, and further details will
follow. I apologize to Momma Fox for my mistake and for the
resulting date issue. As Mark says, I just wanna "Get out there and
box!!!" Thanks again to all. ---Wild Rover
