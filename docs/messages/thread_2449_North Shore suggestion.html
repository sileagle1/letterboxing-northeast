From: Team Randalstik | Date: 2005-10-08 01:53:39 UTC
THE NORTH SHORE?!!! That's WHERE WE LIVE!!!!

HAPPY BIRTHDAY!!!

We have done almost every North Shore Letterbox.

The ones in Salem are drivebys. The tricky part of the Playground series is
the discreetness. They are in pretty open spaces, and if there are kids
playing it may almost be impossible. BUT lucky or unlucky for you gals, it
will be raining Saturday and Sunday. Give them a try.

There is the Alien Limited Time letterbox on Winter Island. This has some
nice views of the ocean (ignore the power plant behind the park) but it can
be a 30 minute walk on the road if you don't want to pay the $15 parking.

The Good and Bad Series is only the Bad now. But an easy drive by. How do I
know this? We planted these.

Sadly, the Witch Trails/Bridget letterbox has been long missing.


THAN there's Rockport. I would go to Rockport more than Gloucester. A bit
farther but beautiful views of the ocean. Halibut Point has three. These are
a bit more of a walk, but only an hour and a half tops, and that's getting
three different stamps from different people, without enjoying the views.
BUT THE OCEAN! Even in the rain!

PLUS near downtown Rockport, Frodo's Voyage, Pirate something, Frog Pond.
ALL worth a good visit. Spend a day in Rockport and you have got all these
boxes plus Legolas which is a bit farther down the road past downtown.

Than there''s Lynn. One in Marblehead.

Maybe this will keep you people busy.

If you finish those, there's always Newburyport!

ENJOY!!
