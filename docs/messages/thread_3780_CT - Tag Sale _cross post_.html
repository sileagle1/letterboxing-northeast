CT - Tag Sale (cross post)

From: letterboxer2002 | Date: 2008-04-10 01:30:30 UTC
Letterboxer2002, Sunflowergirl, and MT1 are having a Tag Sale on
Saturday and Sunday from 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM. We thought we would
extend the invitation to all of our letterboxing friends and offer up
a "Tag Sale" letterbox in the process. I have been known to
have "Munchkins" and you never know who will be "Under The Table and
Dreaming". Moreover we can exchange personals including MT1's first
personal of Thomas The Tank Engine.

We are selling all sorts of furniture including:

1. Complete dark pine finish bedroom set (included, full bed, with
matress and boxspring, dressers with mirror, nightstand and dresser)
2. 3 piece light pine cocktail tables including a sofa table
3. Full futon with matress and cover
4. Dining room table and chairs
5. Patio table, and chairs
6. Giant brand bike
7. Computer items
8. storage containers (all types)
9. WEBBER grill

These are the main items, but we will have lots of other items too.

If you want to stop by, here are the directions:

From New Haven:
Take 91 North
Get off exit 15
Take a left off the exit onto RT 68
At the third light, take left onto Leguis Rd
At end, take a right onto Grieb Rd
At third left, take the left onto Fieldstone farm BLVD.
At end, take left onto Broad Meadow Rd
We are the 4th house on the right, #24

From Hartford,
Take 91 South
Get off exit 15
Take a right off the exit onto RT 68
At the third light, take left onto Leguis Rd
At end, take a right onto Grieb Rd
At third left, take the left onto Fieldstone farm BLVD.
At end, take left onto Broad Meadow Rd
We are the 4th house on the right, #24

There will be neon green signs as well! Hope to see you this
weekend :)

Safe Letterboxing!
Letterboxer2002 (Alan), Sunflowergirl (Amy), and MT1 (Matthew Thomas)
