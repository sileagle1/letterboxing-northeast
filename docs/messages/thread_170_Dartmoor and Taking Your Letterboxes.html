Dartmoor and Taking Your Letterboxes

From: markpepe | Date: 2004-01-15 19:06:18 UTC
Hi boxers:


It looks like our Dartmoor trip in September is actually coming to
fruition. Sue and I will be joined by 9 others [may increase to 12]on
our trip to England in mid September!

I was wondering if there were any letterboxers who would like to have
one of their letterboxes or hitchhikers taken with us and planted in
England. You can email me offlist if you have an interest. You will
just have to prepare it as you normally would and mail it or give it
to me if you plan to be at one of the upcoming gatherings. Our group
will plant it on the moors and take a picture of it's final resting
place that we can post on our website. In addition, we are planning a
gathering in Dartmoor with fellow British letterboxers and will be
sure they also stamp into your logbooks. If you want your hitchhiker
returned to the US after our trip, we can do that also.


Have fun and just get out there & box!!!


Mark Pepe

http://www.pepeanddavidow.blogspot.com/
