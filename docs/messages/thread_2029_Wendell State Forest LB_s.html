Hi all,
My letterboxing buddy and I spent a day at Wendell State Forest
recently and we hunted up the 5 letterboxes planted by Bonnie Sennott.
All five are there and intact and doing well. Although one in
particular has not been 'found' since 2003! We wanted to let everyone
know that all is well with the five boxes. Close attention to the
clues will help you find the elusive second box on the MM Trail as
well as some beautiful trilliums in bloom along the trail. ;-)
