From: cpascott | Date: 2005-08-29 01:54:20 UTC
Hey! Thanks to Brandy You're a Fine Girl for boxing with me
yesterday! After a long day on the trails in Eastford, I'm happy to
report we found all but one! Combing the Eastford boxes with a the
Fairy Tales series in Chaplin, a couple in Mansfield, and one in
Ashford (Ashford Oak), I added 36 boxes to my F count yesterday -- a
single day record!

We hit the trails at 8:30 in the morning, and didn't stop until we
needed the head lamp to get us out of the woods at 8:00 at night --
and then still stopped to pick up some drive by boxes before calling
it an official night around 11:00pm. Wow!

I won't have a chance to update my website until Thursday, but for
those of you with boxes in the area who'd like to see that they were
all in good shape, you can check out the list of finds on then!
