From: Mary Nelson | Date: 2008-08-02 10:53:41 UTC
Cape Point is a popular choice among visiting letterboxers.

http://www.capepointhotel.com/

It is located in mid-Cape in West Yarmouth, near lots of boxes in both directions. September is the best time to enjoy the Cape. Be prepared to spray and wear light colored clothing to defend yourselves against deer ticks.
