Hey all ...

In addition to letterboxing, I spend a fair amount of time editing
Wikipedia, the free online encyclopedia.

I've engaged myself in two related projects lately -- one, to
catalog and create pages for all lands managed by Massachusetts'
Department of Conservation and Recreation, and two, to do the same
for lands owned by The Trustees of Reservations.

Once those are complete, I'll likely move on to other noteworthy
land conservation and historic preservation groups such as the
Massachusetts Audubon Society or Historic New England.

The project is far from complete, and most of the information on
Wikipedia is sourced from the respective organization's websites,
but you might find it a useful reference. There'll be more progress
in the next couple of weeks.

Anyway, I've updated the TTOR page, but have not yet added or
revised pages for their holdings. Conversely, I've added or revised
a number of pages for DCR holdings, but have not yet updated the DCR
page itself.

If you're interested in taking a look or tracking the progress, the
top articles are below:
