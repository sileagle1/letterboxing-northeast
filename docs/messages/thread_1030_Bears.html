We have a bear in our neighborhood. It seems to make regular visits.
Others have seen it (them?), but as yet, I have not. The discussion of
wearing bells reminds me of the talk a park ranger was having with a
group of tourist before they left on a hike. Most of the tourists were
decked out with bells to let the bears know they were in the woods. He
explained there were two types of bears in the woods; black bears and
brown bears. The ranger told them that if they came upon bear poop they
could identify it by its contents. Black bears eat berries, seeds, and
nuts. Brown bears were carnivorous. To tell the difference he said black
bears' poop contains seeds. Brown bears' poop contained little bells.

Luckily, we have black bears here in CT.
