This box is in terrible shape, totally flooded with water as earlier
letterboxers had noted in the somewhat damp logbook. I had to throw
out the green inkpad. Stamp is fine-those thick white erasers
Drewclan uses are practically indestructible. Like Pease l.b., this
box was planted in 3/2000 and also needs the same size box as what it
was in. I dried box as best I could (ice inside), double-bagged the
logbook and placed the whole box in a ziplock.
There are a number of other boxes at this park (Camp Moween State
Park) on Cedar Lake. I ran out of time for seeking out Fritz's l.B.,
Special Moments of Recreational Delight l.b. and the A' Boxing We
Will Go series. Perhaps I'll get back there soon and can fix up
Granny's Tav. l.b.
