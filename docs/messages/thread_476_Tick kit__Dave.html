LB'ers

Tick protection is a must. Just because the mosquitoes are not out
doesn't mean you should pass up the DEET. I've always used the simple
bottle of OFF. On Sat. I sprayed up but my boyfriend did not. Two
hours later I found 8 ticks on him; I was thankfully tick free. Deer
were plentiful of course: we spotted a herd of 5. We were in the
Charles River valley in Dover, MA (Peters Reservation). I dunno if
there is a complete list of boxes in this town and its neighbors.

Brenda

> What do you put in your tick kit?

Sharp tweezers for plucking and alcohol swabs for rubbing the area
are good things to have for when they bite you.

Bug spray is a definite.

If you don't mind looking silly, you should tuck your pants into your
boots and pull your socks up over them.

And make sure that you know what a tick looks like before you go out.
