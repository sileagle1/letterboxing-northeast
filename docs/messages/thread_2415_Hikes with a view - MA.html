From: LundyandVickster@aol.com | Date: 2005-09-29 15:11:20 UTC
Donutz716,

The best view I found so far was not much of a hike. It is in New Salem, MA and is called Quabbin View. This is little more than a drive-by. It overlooks the Quabbin Resevoir and you get a great view of Monadnock to the north. To get an alternate perspective of the Quabbin try Ye Ole Fishing Hole. It brings you very near water lavel. While in that area there are a number of other boxes in and around the Shutesbury/Pelham/Amherst area and some of those can stretch your legs a bit.

Larry
Lundy and Vickster
