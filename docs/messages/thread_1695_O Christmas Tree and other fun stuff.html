O Christmas Tree and other fun stuff

From: jbean1164 | Date: 2004-12-23 17:54:41 UTC
Hello!

m & m and I were so excited to take off this morning intent on
finding the O Christmas Tree series before its gone (even though I
SHOULD have been home cleaning and baking and wrapping and cooking,
but as I told my husband...m & m can't sit around all day watching
me do these chores...she needs a diversion!)

Well we hit two milestones today..

we did our FIRST exchanges! We have exchanged with a few friends,
but have never run into another boxer while out. Today we met the
Maydaylillies, QueenBee and the Bookworm!

AND

we hit F100!!

Thanks to Queen Bee for going home to get her stamp and bringing
back a couple quick drive by clues for us! We didn't get to all of
them, but got enough to reach our 100! Local Heros and The Meeting
House.

It was great meeting you all and thanks again!

Love the O Christmas Tree series by the way! Very cute and festive!

Ho Ho Ho to all!

JBean and m & m

Glad we could be part of those milestones with you.
