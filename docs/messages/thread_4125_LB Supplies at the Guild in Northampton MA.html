LB Supplies at the Guild in Northampton MA

From: Cathy | Date: 2010-06-17 18:01:31 UTC
I just wanted to give a shout out to the locally owned arts store in my area. The Guild in Northampton MA has a great selection of papers, logbooks, inks/pens, carving materials & knives. The staff is kind & goes out of their way to help. Just this morning I was in looking for a #1 blade. They didn't happen to have one in stock, but the staff offered to bust open a complete set of blades and sell me just the blade I needed. (They will make it a complete set again when the #1 blades get back in stock.) So awesome! So if you happen to be in the area, please do check them out!
