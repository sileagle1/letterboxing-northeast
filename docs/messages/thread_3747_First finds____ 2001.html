I just had a frantic search to find my first log book. I was looking
for info on a box I had found in 2006. Much to my surprise, that
first log book not only has my finds from when my family started
boxing in 2005, but on the very first page I have my first finds from
2001!!

In 2001 I learned about boxing and took my best friend on a surprise
trip. I told her to bring a stamp and a book.

We found 3 boxes that first day.
Bay of Bristol #1 and #2 in Bristol, RI
Fort Butt in Portsmouth, RI

After that I went boxing with my husband 9 days later finding
Island Sanctuary in Jamestown, RI

Then life took over and boxing got put aside until Nov 2005. My son
was then 3 and we started boxing in earnest.

I was so excited about finding my original log book that I just had to
share!

RIFamily
