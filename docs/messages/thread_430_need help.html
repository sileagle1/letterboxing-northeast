From: connfederate | Date: 2006-10-08 16:15:50 UTC
Hi Butterfly,
My first plant faced the same problem: planted in one county but
more easily accessed, and located nearest to a town in another
county.

If the clue is posted at LbNA:

Go to "Member Services", and left-click the link.

Find "Manage Your Letterboxes", go to "Your Letterboxes (#)", and
click on that link. You should see a list of all the letterboxes
you have planted and listed on LbNA. Note the links under your
planted boxes that will allow you to Edit or Archive your boxes.

Go to the box for which you want to change the county listed in the
clue, and click on "Edit".
-Find the "County" listing, clicking on the county name or the
little blue arrow to the right of it will trigger a drop-down menu.
-Select the county you want to display in your clue.
-Scroll to the very bottom of the page, and click "Submit changes".

The correct county should now be displayed in your clue.

Sorry the post is so long, I hope it helps solve your problem.
Connfederate
