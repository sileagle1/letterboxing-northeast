Some days wonderful things happen

From: rubaduc2001 | Date: 2009-03-12 21:21:32 UTC
Just after I planted The Linwood Tree (Colchester, CT.) in 2005, I got an email from some very nice (aren't we all?) boxers who had been "caught" by the cemetery caretaker as they were stamping in. Not knowing what else to do they explained the process to him and he seemed quite all right with it, even offering to "watch" over it. Now four years later some other very nice boxers reported that the namesake tree was missing as well as the box (the box was at the end of a stonewall under a bush). Checking in on it today, the tree appears to be gone although there's another just behind the original which will do as a landmark. A new stone wall has been added to the left of the first one and going behind this we found a perfectly square niche with the box in it. My thanks to a wonderful caring caretaker.
Rubaduc
