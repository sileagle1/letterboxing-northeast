RI Finds

From: Don | Date: 2004-04-10 19:21:19 UTC
Hi

Just got back from a great day hiking in Burriville, RI. During my
trek around Buck Hill I found 5 Letterboxes and one Hitchhiker. The
Tri-state letterboxes are all fine. During my hike for them I saw my
first Wood Duck for 2004, a deer and several wild turkeys. While
stamping in at the North/South Trail LB for my 100th find, I got a
surprize,The Dragonfly Hitchhiker. A great sunny spring day in the
high 50's,lots of wildlife, my 100th find and a Hitchhiker, it
doesn't get any better than this.

Trail Turtle
