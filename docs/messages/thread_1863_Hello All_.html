Hi!

I just wanted to write a quick post saying hello to all of you. I
have been away from letterboxing for a while and was very
disappointed to see all the hate being thrown around on the main
talklist. I am glad there is a place to see whats going on in the
letterboxing world without the slander.

My wife and I just got back from a weeks vacation in England,
Ireland, and Wales. It was a great time. At one point we were so
close to Dartmoor. I almost convinced my companions to travel the
extra distance so I could say a quick hello to some of my favorite
tors. Alas we decided to visit Bath and Stonehenge instead ;(

I hope all is well in "letterboxing-land". Perhaps with the big
melt to come, I will take Mark's advise and "get out and box".
Until then, I hope you all stay safe and have a great time
letterboxing!

Best Wishes-
Alan (Letterboxer2002)
