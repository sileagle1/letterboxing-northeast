From: dave & diane | Date: 2004-04-03 04:12:15 UTC
Early in the week, our weather (especially Monday morning) is supposed to be sleety and crappy. If so, you can always letterbox in the Jamestown or West Warwick libraries (shameless plug!). HOWEVER - do try to make it to the Just Bass letterbox! The stamp is just so gorgeous! I wish I'd seen the Porgy stamp that used to go with it. There's so many great LBs out there, I wish I knew where to start advising you. Faces of Wickford is also very well carved, but I think the fourth one is still missing. Anybody have an update?

Dave
