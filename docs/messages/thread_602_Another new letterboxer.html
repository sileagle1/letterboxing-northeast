Another new letterboxer

From: lb_moongriffin | Date: 2004-04-26 22:50:45 UTC
Hey all:

I thought I would say hello and introduce myself. I am pretty new to
letterboxing -- read about it for the past five years in various
Globe articles, finally got out and did two letterboxes with
boyfriend and niece last summer (Wompatuck and Webb in MA). Got all
excited, began to brood on stamps to carve, delayed going out til
niece could come along again .... and embarrassingly, here it is the
almost end of April without ever having LB'ed again.

But this year I am going all the way! I found some pink stuff, got
some linoleum cutters (perhaps better named, finger stabbers),
plunged myself into the Abyss of Carving Humility by checking out
the artistry of TGD, Chuck and Molly, et al., and am ready this week
to carve sig stamp, traveller/exchange/friendship stamp, plus I am
way psyched to plant some boxes, like a Bridge Troll series, inter
alia.

Still trying to get various beloveds as equally pumped -- but so
far, the boyfriend is less than enthused -- all he will consider for
a team name is Team Honda (he is a motorcycle fanatic ...). *sigh*

Anyway, I work right near the Arnold Arboretum (JP area/Boston), so
as soon as I get the stamps carved, I will be dashing out at lunch
to do the two (maybe more?) boxes there and posting the breathlessly
dorky box updates that are the hallmark of we endorphin-drunk
newbies.

Luna <-- my trail name! (Oh, I am dorking out here!)
