Yes, that is correct. There are those that are lost to the old ways.
Primarily because the switchover to the "new" site involved removing graphic links
for clues. You'll find some particularly creative clues on the "old" site.

>If so, how does one distinguish between those that still exist and those that do
> not?

Well, speaking from my personal experience - the ones in maine - ARE THERE. I
highly encourage new boxers to really take some time and research the history in
those old clues and boxes. That is only speaking for the ones in Maine placed by a
certain sleight of hand man. Those clues are worth every ounce of effort you'll go
through to find them - hunt them down! I say, the harder the clues are to find - the
better the boxes, and the greater the chance that the box actually survives in the
long run.
