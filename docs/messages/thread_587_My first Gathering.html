Hi Fish-or-man! Since this is your first gather, I'll let you in on
something. YOU WILL BE TOTALLY OVERWHELMED BY THE EXPERIENCE! When you get
there, you want to exchange with everyone you meet! Also, bring as many
personal traveler clues as you can! It seems as though personal travelers
are becoming very common and make sure you have them in some sort of order
(alphabetical or something) so you can thumb through them easily when you
find who you're looking for. Or, write on a separate piece of paper, the
people who have personal travelers and check them off as you find them.

Expect to have anywhere between 50-100 exchanges by the end of the day. I
believe this will be a large gather, so just be prepared to be totally
overwhelmed. Sometimes people also bring hitchhikers to the event for
others to stamp in. Bluebird and Butterfly usually don't stamp in, and
rather stamp in on the trail, however, sometimes I find a couple that I
really want in my book and stamp in (being that I've stamped into over 130
hitchhikers, I usually have most of them).

Also, bring food to share, if you can ;-) and drinks for yourself, unless
they are being provided. And, lastly, HAVE A GREAT TIME! I will see you
there!

Music Woman

Hate to go on a posting binge, but I forgot to add:

A personal traveler is a stamp that is carried around by letterboxers
that is usually handcarved. In addition to exchanging with them with
their trail stamp, many letterboxers carry a personal traveler
(a.k.a. P.T.) and you usually have to either recite a line, sing a
song, or whisper a secret answer in their ear and you can recieve
their traveler...different letterboxers have different tasks for you
to do in order to get their P.T. - and some people may have more than
one!
