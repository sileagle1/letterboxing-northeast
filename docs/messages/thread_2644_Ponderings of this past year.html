Ponderings of this past year

From: chunna21 | Date: 2006-01-01 21:25:24 UTC
As I follow this trail I have embarked on for over a year, I have
found that Every artist was at first an amateur, and that People
only see what they are prepared to see. There is a neat little book
I have found in a local library that has a good grasp for words and
shows me that There is creative writing as well as creative
reading... Some books leave us free and some books make us free
Nothing great was ever achieved without enthusiasm! The miracles of
genius always rest on profound convictions which refuse to be
analyzed, Don't be too timid and squeamish about your actions! I
have learned that All life is an experiment! Trust your instinct to
the end, though you can render no reason. Everything in Nature
contains all the powers of Nature. Everything is made of hidden
stuff The reward of a thing well done is having done it!

To me, Letterboxing embodies all these assets.

Happy New Year to All

Chunna
