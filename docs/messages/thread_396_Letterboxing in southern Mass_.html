Hi
Today I spent my 1st anniversary of letterboxing hiking in southern
Massachusetts, what else; letterboxing with my daughter Talia and our
dogs Oak and Grady. Our first stop was Happy Birthday Baby LB. at a
nice park in Walpole. The box was full of water but the journal &
stamp were dry. After drying it out the box in fine.

Next we stopped at the Water Series in F. Gilbert State Forest,
Foxboro, Mass. all three boxes our fine and the Water Drop Letterbox
is Ice Free now. While stamping in at the Water Lilly Letterbox we
found the Rambler Hitchhiker resting. He'll be continuing on his
rambling ways soon.

From the Turtle Clan we would like to wish Jay a Happy 28th Birthday
and a long life in letterboxing.
