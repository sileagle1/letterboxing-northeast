Message to Webmasters,

From: Anonymous LbNA User:

Message:
I am writing you regarding a disturbing trend whereby various Geocachers have
been trespassing private property in their zeal to find their clues including my
land which is in Bradford NH. My surveillance cameras caught two Geocachers on
Sept. 26, with GPS devices, hatchet, book and cellphones on their belt, breaking
through my barriers by a bridge and trespassing my property with blatant
disregard for the POSTED and NO TRESPASSING SIGNS clearly marked and on display
like Christmas lights.�

I don't know if they were from your organization but so far Geocache.com had
denied it although they admitted that one of their caches was within a couple
hundred feet. The local police thinks it was one of theirs but a Geocache
official is strongly denying it and has recommended that I look into several
other Geocache organizations. If you know of any caches that are on West Meadow
road in Bradford, please let me know and you should also immediately delete any
coordinates that are toward the south end of that road. The trespassers not only
committed criminal trespass, they also chopped down part of a small tree to
notch an obvious sign for future geocachers but proceeded to wander more than
200' into my land and were there at least a half hour making mocking gestures to
my camera as they left.

I am not saying these people were from your Opencaching organization but I do
want to know if you have any caches in that area of Bradford NH? Additionally,
you could be of help by clearly stating that your organization strictly
prohibits the trespass of any private property marked with POSTED or NO
TRESPASSING signs regardless of whether they think there is a cache there or
not.�

Thanks very much,

J
