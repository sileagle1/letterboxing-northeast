A Gathering Traveller Stamp (Look for Me)

Thoughts of mountains that you've climbed
While your at a gathering time

Look for this box when you rest
In the grass may be best

Now move the box away from here
To a spot you'll have to define where

Write it down then pass the clues to another
Maybe a sister, brother, father, or mother

In the end I'll be the last
I hope to find it somewhat fast

With lots of stamps and fun clues
Provided by all of you!
