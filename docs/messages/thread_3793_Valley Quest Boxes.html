Remember that a lot of people exclusively use letterboxing.org so if it's
not listed there that group of boxers won't find your clues.

Also, many people do not log their finds on AQ. When you log on and check
your boxes on AQ you can look at how many hits the clue has gotten, that may
be an interesting number for you to look at as well.
