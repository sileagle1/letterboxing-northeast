Cape Cod weekend

From: rospa10 | Date: 2004-04-27 02:58:25 UTC
The highlight of our trip was a whale watch. I firmly believe that
everyone should try to seize the opportunity to view these fellow
mammals in their natural habitat. It is awe inspiring to put it
mildly.
Now, on to the topic at hand!! I found "Stonecroft"..well, let me
restate that, I found brand new letterboxers, "The Vt. 2" stamping
into Stonecroft and joined them. This box was their third. On Sat. we
checked on my "Great Island" letterbox. It was so nice to see the
sign-ins of visiters. On Sun. I found "Tandem Mates", part of a
series on the Cape Cod Rail Trail for bikers and hikers. I had gotten
one other stamp from the series last year. Now, have found all th
eWellfleet area boxes except "Long Pond" one. Driving directions to
the site are not working out as spelled out. Would have been great to
run into Camera Obsura at Nickerson. Clues were printed out but we
chose to linger at the beach before heading back to CT.
