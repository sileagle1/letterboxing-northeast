From: Chuck & Amy | Date: 2008-10-21 03:14:45 UTC
Hello Mark, Yes I know the stamp. It's from a letterbox. It's an oldie. Planted 8-2-02 by Leader of the Pack and Pink Trotters. Part of a series. The series was freshened up in 2005 by volunteers fixing Carolyn's boxes. Anyway, It is listed on LBNA under Mansfield Center, planter is Carolyn, name is Kettle Hole Series and it is box #2. The person should be able to get a hold of Carolyn through contact the placer.  If that don't work, I would be happy to pass along a message. I doubt she needs the stamp back. It can be remade quickly. It's just the word ESKER as a negative carve into speedy-cut. Glad I could be of some help...Chuck of WWW
