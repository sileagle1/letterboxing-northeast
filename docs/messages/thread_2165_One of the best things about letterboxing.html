One of the best things about letterboxing

From: Team Safari | Date: 2005-06-12 03:04:49 UTC
..is being introduced to places you'd probably never have gone to otherwise. And then, you go back, time and time again even after you find the box, either to watch a friend find it, or to check on it because you heard it was missing, or because the place was so nice or such a pleasant surprise you just have to go back or show it to someone.

Big Ol' Sun is alive and well in Creeee-anstin, RI. I just had to find that little spot from memory (as IF, my friend must have thought).
