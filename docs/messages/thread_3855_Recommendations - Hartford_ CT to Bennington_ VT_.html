Recommendations - Hartford, CT to Bennington, VT?

From: Wisconsin Hiker | Date: 2008-07-18 15:48:16 UTC
Hi!

Martini Man and I will be in a portion of the NE area from AUG 9 - 17.
We'll be flying in/out of Harford, CT and looping up into the
Bennington, VT area. So we'll be in northwest CT, western MA and
southwest Vermont, and may possibly cross over into NY. We're hoping to
get recommendations for some good hikes that include letterboxes. The
main focus of our trip will be hiking & letterboxing, so longer treks
are welcome! We haven't set an exact route yet, but will let the
hiking opportunities guide us in selecting where we might want to make
hotel reservations.

If anyone can also provide info on any of your favorite restaurants in
this general area, we'd love to hear about them. If by any chance any
of you live in this area, let us know if you'd like to meet up to
hike/box with us or get together for a meal.

Wisconsin Hiker

Re: Recommendations - Hartford, CT to Bennington, VT?

From: C D Moskey | Date: 2008-07-19 19:25:05 UTC
You can do a search by highway (I-91 from Hartford to Brattleboro) on Atlas
Quest to find boxes near your route.

You might also benefit from visiting Mark and Sue Pepe's blogspot,
http://markandsuepepe.blogspot.com They have a number of boxes in the area
of Wilmington, VT, a lovely little town that you'll travel through if you
take route 9 from Brattleboro to Wilmington.
