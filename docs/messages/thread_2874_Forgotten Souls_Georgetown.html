From: Memlili | Date: 2006-04-30 18:30:21 UTC
Thanks for the compliment on the letterbox! I am delighted to echo
the comment for placers NOT to give up on a missing box. This box
was "gone missing" for several months. Even the friend who watches
over the box for me could not find it ~ and he knew where it was
supposed to be! Someone ~ and I apologize for not remembering exactly
who first contacted me ~ found it, followed by at least one or two
other people. I got detailed descriptions from them of where they had
found it. My friend went back one more time and, with that added
information, found the box. It had been moved a fair distance away ~
20 paces from a maple tree but the wrong maple tree! This box is now
back where it started and my friend says that, while well hidden, it
will be obvious to a letterboxer.

My thanks to everyone who wrote to let me know it was still there and
shared details. This is one of my favorite boxes and has sentimental
value to me. I am SO pleased it is not gone.

~ Memlili
