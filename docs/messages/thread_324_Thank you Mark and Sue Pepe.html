Thank you Mark and Sue Pepe

From: Phyto | Date: 2004-03-05 00:44:46 UTC
I would just like to personally (and publicly) thank Mark and Sue Pepe
for their contribution(s) to letterboxing. The blogspot that Mark
maintains is not only entertaining but very informative and helpful
for us afficionados - I mean, addicts (!) and for those who might
happen to stumble across such a page, what a wealth of information!
THANKS!

I'd also like to thank Mark for his efforts in organizing a Dartmoor
Trip. Although Phyto and Isosceles cannot afford such an endavour, we
will certainly be able to at some point in our lives, lets hope that
at that point we would be blessed with the opportunity to box with
Mark and many others.

To complete my gushing I'd like to add that I love his weekly polls. I
love knowing that I can vote anonymously (well, sort of thanks to
internet technology!) and still have fun getting out there and boxing!!!

THANKS MARK and SUE PEPE!!!
