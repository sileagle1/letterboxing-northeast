Great Pumpkin & Touring Turtle HH'ers found on Sunday

From: Janet | Date: 2004-10-18 12:01:26 UTC
I know why Linus couldn't find the Great Pumpkin HH in the pumpkin
patch... it had crossed over into the Twilight Zone! Yesterday
morning I found the Great Pumpkin HH hanging out with that nasty
monster "To Serve Man" in the Twilight Zone. I brought the Great
Pumpkin HH back into this dimension. The Great Pumpkin HH is now
happily hanging out with another monster in Mansfield.

I no sooner got the Great Pumpkin HH situated in her new home when I
found the Touring Turtle HH lurking about the Bates Hotel (very seedy
Turtle.) I convinced the turtle to leave the Bates Motel and travel
with me. I dropped the Touring Turtle off at another letterbox in
Mansfield.

All totaled I spent 15 hours hunting monsters on Saturday & Sunday.
That doesn't include the 3 hour round trip drive each day. Boy am I
tired! Met a lot of great people out on the trail (& in the Country
Store) and had a chance to box with people from all over New
England. As Nov 1 rapidly approaches I worry that I may not be able
to finish all the Monster Mash boxes (I still have 45 left to find!)
and my motto is "No monster will be left behind." So, I continue to
pray for good weather on the weekends (This last weekend's weather
was a "10" in my book.) and hope that my aching joints hold up.

Happy Monster Hunting to all,
