ME+NH: Boxing weekend

From: oceanwytch | Date: 2005-05-15 17:26:01 UTC
We had a wonderful 2 days boxing in Maine and New Hampshire. Here's
a list of boxes we found:

Red Bench (Box was exposed. The person who adopted the trail found
the box, but was really happy it was there. We re-hid it in the
correct spot.)

Elephant Head (Logbook almost full...not many pages left)

Glen Ellis Falls

Eastern Slope Inn Riverbank Walk

Diana's Bath (Box was cracked and contents were soaked. Unfortunetly
we couldn't do much to help since we're in Maine)

Diana's Bath Too

Cathedral Ledge Series

Fall Foliage

Rainbow Chameleon

Strongbad

Smooth Sailing

Crossed Arrows

Brownfield Bog Cattails (Box was completely filled with water. We
took it home to dry it out and will replace it very soon.)

The boxes that don't have a comment next to them were dry and in
good condition. We had a wonderful time with all of these.

~Oceanwytch and MaineKokopellian~
