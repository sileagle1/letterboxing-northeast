New box! "Lizzie" ... featuring artwork by Steph of Pointer Party

From: cpascott | Date: 2006-07-09 17:57:39 UTC
This has been posted to LbNA and AQ and is included in the e-mail update.

"Lizzie", a theme box based on the infamous Borden murders in Fall
River, has been placed! The box is located on the grounds of the
former Borden home (now a bed & breakfast) at 92 Second Street in Fall
River, MA. The owner was very gracious in allowing its placement, so
hunters please be respectful of this property's primary use.

The stamp was carved by Steph of Pointer Party ... thanks Steph!!

Clues to the box are on my website at
http://home.comcast.net/~scott.e.hall/clues/Lizzie.html

Also ... be on the look-out for another box, "Purgatory", to be placed
next weekend. This box will be located at Purgatory Chasm in Sutton,
MA and is placed in collaboration with SapphireBerry (host of the
Greek Myth Gathering for those who may not know her), who was
intending on placing a similar box and has generously offered to
combine efforts with me!

A number of other boxes are in the works, including a series at Mount
Auburn Cemetery in Cambridge. The first stamp of this series, carved
by Scarab, has been received and I am considering potentially placing
it as a single box while awaiting receipt of the other stamps.

Happy hunting!

CPAScott
