Re: [LbNE] LBNA Webpage Question

From: Silent Doug | Date: 2005-04-13 20:08:18 UTC
At 10:54 AM 4/13/2005, you wrote:
>I am curious why some boxes have green flags next to them. What does
>it mean? How do I get rid of them?

The green flags mean that those letterboxes have clues that are hosted
somewhere other than on the LbNA web site; click the green flag to record
that you've found that letterbox.

For boxes that don't have a green flag, you can record your find by
clicking the green flag that appears on the top of the clue page itself.
