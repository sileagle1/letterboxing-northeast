I revised the clues to Aqueduct today. When I went to check on the
box with a friend, we discovered the box on the kiosk. There has been
work done in the area where the box had been and a new bridge built. I
was very pleased to see that they had saved my box for me with
everything intact. The ink pad needs replacing and I didn't have one
with me so please bring your own.

My friend is a new letterboxer and is Norwegian. She says that the
word "kiosk" is a Norwegian word and is pronounced "djoosk", as close
as I can phoneticize it. You learn something every day!

BTW, I will be on vacation for the next 2 weeks, so if you have any
questions or comments, I won't be available after Monday until Nov. 1.
And I'll probably forget how to pronounce "kiosk" by then. ;-)
