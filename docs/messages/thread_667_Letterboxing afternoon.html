After fortifying myself with a very late lunch (chili-cheese dog at
McHenry's stand on Jackson St.) I proceded to The Windham Mills
Heritage Park for Thread City Mills letterbox. While teaching at
Windham Middle School our team did a walking tour of "the Bridges of
Windham County as part of a huge interdisciplinary unit, ending with
a picnic lunch at this new park. Too badi its so torn up right now
due to installation of new drains, looks like. The stamp is perfect
and facts on the clue sheet are very interesting. However, retreiving
and replanting the box is a tad dicey...on to Shelt Falls Preserve in
Mansfield for a combo. search for NeeDeeps' new box "Mystical
Legends" and to finally get Cock-A-Doodle-Moo's 4-H series, "Just for
Kicks and Shelter Falls". At "just for Kicks" I found the "Ramblin
rose" h.h.! Next stop: "Mystical". Wow, is all I gotta say..oh, and
I would suggest searchers bring a large sheet of plain white paper.
On to "Falls" which had the replacement stamp plus an dunexplained
one inside it. Ah well, free bonus stamp-works for me! Back towards
home and a quick visit to the "Inn of Winter H.H. Hostel" to leave a
giftie and find a giftie.
Hopefully lots of you got out today too.
