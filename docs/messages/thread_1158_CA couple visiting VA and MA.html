From our house in Needham MA to Woodstock, we take 128 North (same as
I-95)to Rt. 3 North which turns into NH Rt. 3 (Edward Everett
Parkway) to I-89 all the way. Once you cross I-91 in White River
Junction start looking for Rt. 4 (Quechee/Woodstock) exit. It might
even be the next one. Be careful not to take any Rt. 4 exits till
after you cross over I-91. (It must run parallel or something as
there are I think two Rt. 4's before I-91 as well as the one you want
after it).
