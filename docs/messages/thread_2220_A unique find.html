A unique find

From: J. BRIDGMAN | Date: 2005-07-09 22:30:57 UTC
While hiking at McLean Game Refuge this afternoon, after successfully
finding Rising Tall, we were hiking toward the trail to the picnic
grove
when we spotted a bit of a zip-lock bag poking out from the leaves at
the base of a small tree very close to the trail. Upon
investigation, we discovered a letterbox with stamp, ink pad and
logbook, but not one thing was written on the box or the logbook.
The stamp is a puppy dog holding a bone in its mouth - a cute
store-bought stamp that appeared fairly new. Possibly it had been
stamped once or twice. Talk about a box with no name! We brought
the box home with us but will replace it if need be. Was hoping
someone would recognize this and tell us what to do with it.
