"Seafaring Mystery" still unsolved!?!

From: wandaandpete | Date: 2005-06-17 18:20:18 UTC
We recently had an opportunity to check on our "seafaring mystery"
microboxes, and were amazed to find that even after a whole year they had
still not been stamped into! "The Quarry" had, in fact, gone missing, probably
due to a party of teenagers in the area, some evidence of which we saw, so
we recarved and replaced that box in a safer nearby location. Also, the
"Plymouth Rock" box had dropped into a perilous predicament, so Pete
carefully extracted that one and replaced it in a safer spot as well. Updated
clues for both of these (the third one stays the same) are on our web site. So
now, for all you New Englander boxers who claim to love mysteries, all we
can say is "don't miss the boat"! It's really not a very hard mystery compared
to some that are out there, so come on, folks, put on your captain's hats and
come steer the ship!
