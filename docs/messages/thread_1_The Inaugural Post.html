Anyhow.... Team Green Dragon has stepped up offering to take the
reigns of this board--which is certainly more logical than me who's
only stepped foot in the northeast once in my lifetime. (Well, okay,
a million or two steps to be precise, but you know what I mean....)

On a couple of notes:

When I created the board, I set the Links section so moderators could
update it, but members can only view it. Ideally, I see it as a
single source for all webpages related to letterboxing in the
northeast. So if you wake up one morning and think, "Hey, didn't
that Jay Drew guy hide a box in such-and-such place?" But for
whatever reason he didn't list it on the main talk list--probably
because the National Park Service is hot on his track. How do you
find the clue? Ah-ha! Go to Links and there's his website!

I'm kidding about the NPS thing, btw. They're really after me and
Amanda, not Jay. =) But I digress....

I'm not really sure how often it is on the Pacific Northwest board I
created, but I love the feature and use it often when I want to look
up somebody's website. To keep the section from becoming cluttered
with all sorts of unrelated information, though, only the moderator
can update links in the section. I added a few links I knew off the
top of my head or were easy for me to find, but if you know of others
to add, let us know! =)

Oooh, another for-instance: I know Wanda and Pete have a website for
their hitchhikers because I've seen the webpage before. But where is
it? I don't know! How do I find it? I don't know! Guess I'd e-
mail them (or someone else who I knew had the website) to find it.
But if it was in the Links section, it would be easy for anyone to
look up.

So, in a nutshell, it would be great if the Links section could be
the single, best place on the Internet for a list of all Northeast
letterboxer webpages. (There's not one already, is there? Because
I'm assuming there isn't. Not being from the area, I wouldn't know
stuff like that. Another good reason I shouldn't be the owner of
this board!)

Hmm.... That's all I can think of. Hey, Green Dragon--should we
make the offical announcement for the opening of this board? =)
