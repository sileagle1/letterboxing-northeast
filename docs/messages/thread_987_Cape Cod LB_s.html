Re: Cape Cod LB's

From: Jayme | Date: 2004-07-16 16:45:34 UTC
It appeared to be a husband and wife couple, without stamps. I'll
ask my husband if he remembers the name. I think it started with a D
or L (some help, huh?) They did the Nickerson State Park Series, at
least 2 of the 3 we found. We didn't get to the Headless Hunters.
We were staying in Wellfleet and boxed mostly from Provincetown to
Nickerson State Park. When the weather finally turned nice
yesterday we put an end to boxing and headed to the beach. We
headed home today. As for the Kayaker, it was either rehid too well
or we messed up. I thought we had the spot and could see others had
been searching the same spots. I even looked at some spots that
didn't seem correct because it looked like someone had just been
there as well. After not finding the Kayaker, and then not finding
the Yurt (all the directions made me feel like we were in the exact
spot for that one) I was about to call it quits but tried for one
more, and found it. At least we ended with a find.

Jayme

Re: [LbNE] Re: Cape Cod LB's

From: MommaFox | Date: 2004-07-16 22:35:51 UTC
It was probably the people camping across from us, we gave them the clues to that series and one other easy box. I figured if I gave them some easy ones that we had just found then they might stick with it. Believe it or not we never got their names! We did give them our email and asked them to let us know if they found any.

We were sure that we were in the right place for the Yurt also. There were four of us looking and I think we covered a large area and no luck.

When we looked for the Kayaker we originally took the wrong path. When we found it on the right path it was lying out in the open so we had to rehide it. Maybe someone messed with it again! It was there on the 11th.

Anna
