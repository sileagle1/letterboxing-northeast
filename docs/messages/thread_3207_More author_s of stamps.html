Here's a little info that I have...
>
> Who carved the following stamps?
> 3 fingered MacGee- Mainekokopellian
> Blow Me Down - Kreepy Kelli
> Jamaica's Hidden Treasure - n/a
> Dunkin Donuts- Hillel
> Gracey O-Malley - Beirt As Sceiri
> Unmentionable Pain- Team Safari
> Cat and Mouse - n/a

pirate ship in the distance to the right of him. It appears that
there is smoke coming out of his hair on both sides of his head. I
don't have a name for this stamp or author.

There were a couple carvers who used this image of Blackbeard. I have
the one with the ship on the LEFT and Blackbeard underneath. It is by
Tempus Fugit.

Hope some of this helps!
