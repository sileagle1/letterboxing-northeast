Keeper of the Flame (Ashland MA) Recovered!

From: Connie Nicolas | Date: 2006-01-31 14:58:38 UTC
Thanks to a friendly geocacher, this box has been
recovered! It had been marked missing since last
fall's flooding. But a helpful geocacher (who was
searching for a geocache in the same park) found it,
looked up our website, and emailed me with a
description of the box and the location where he had
left it! Oddly enough, it was found upstream from its
original hiding spot. I am very grateful to this Good
Samaritan who took the time to contact me since this
was my very first carving/planted box ever! Keeper of
the Flame will stay out of commission until spring
when it will be replaced. Songbird
