Today I stopped at the Wethersfield, Conn. Ocean State Job Lot on the
Silas Deane Hwy. to browse. For S's & G's I looked at their food
storage boxes. In the words of the great one (Homer): Woo-HOOOOO!!!!!
A hefty supply of "Freshness Keeper" boxes!
These are similar, if not comparable to "Lock'N'Lock" boxes, ie:
microwave/freezer safe, water-tight gasket in the lid-channel, and
locking tabs on x4 sides. The tabs are not as secure as L'N'L but HEY,
THEY'RE CHEAPER! (And less expensive, too :p)--30 oz. @ $1.29 ea., 2.1
qt. @ $1.99 ea. and 4.2 qt. @ $2.99 each. Four of the 30 oz. boxes
cost me $5.47, including Jodi Rell's share! :D

Connfederate
