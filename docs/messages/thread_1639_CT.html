Let me share something that I found this weekend while scouting out a letterbox
location in a favorite spot of mine. I discovered a serene inscription upon the four
sides of a stone monument that read:

"VITA BREVE, ARS LONGO"

For those latin scholars out there, forgive my hideous translation and/or spelling -
however I read this as LIFE IS SHORT ART IS FOREVER. So in those brilliant words, I
think your art will last on long beyond the physical box/location. I appreciate the
efforts of artists and creative thinkers everwhere you are brave enough to put their
ideas out for the public! Here's to you folks!

Cheers!~
