Our Seasonal Friend

From: david | Date: 2007-02-06 17:58:14 UTC
I just wanted to let everyone know in New England, that we have been
visited by a seasonal friend. There is now a snowman across the street
from our house. This seasonal friend also comes with a box. If anyone
would like to go looking for this box, the link is:

http://www.atlasquest.com/lboxes/showboxinfo.html?gBoxId=31163

This box is will be pulled when our seasonal friend picks up and leaves.
hopefully the local kids leave him alone and he doesn't pick up early.
so for any of you looking for a reason to visit New Hampshire, you have
on now. This box is an easy drive-by, no more than 5 minutes out in the
cold.

rockin and rollin in the granite state
david (team new hampshire)
http://teamnewhampshire.blogspot.com
