Viking Ships at Sunrise - The Sequel

From: Warrior Woman | Date: 2003-12-23 00:00:16 UTC
After receiving "not found" reports on Viking Ships at Sunrise for
the second time, we replanted the third box for Trish in November -
only to discover on further inspection that boxes #1 and #2 were
actually there!

After combing through the log books of VSS #1 and #2, there was a
possibility that Palantir and Traveler with a Twist were also buried
under there.

Today, I went up to the location of this letterbox, and tunneled from
one end to the other. There are no missing letterboxes - the only
thing in the location is the VSS #3.

We filled the center of the location with large stones and rocks to
avoid any more boxes falling into the abyss, and the box was
replanted securely at the correct compass location. I was lucky,
though! A HH was inside - all the way from Oregon! From Pandora, to
boot!

Mystery solved, and further problems now avoided.

I also returned the logs and stamps from VSS #1 and #2 to Trish today.
