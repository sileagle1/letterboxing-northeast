From: highpeakhiker | Date: 2004-05-27 05:26:46 UTC
Tenant Falls letterbox is north of Northville, off Rt 30. There's a
Sinclair? Point on Sacandaga also, on the other side, but it is
listed as "Saratoga Springs", where there are two boxes that truly
are in S. Springs. There's a new one, going by the title something
like worst carved stamp or something like that. This new one is
relatively close to Amsterdam, in case you're going north via that
jump off from the Thruway.

Oh, if you're going north of Albany, there's also one fairly close to
the north end of I 787, listed on LBNA as My Birthday Box, really in
North Troy but listed as Lansingburg, NY--which is a "section" of
Troy.

That's about it for the Capital District Area of NYS. I'll be
placing one soon at a little known but spectacular location, but I
have to carve a stamp showing the largest waterfalls east of Niagara
first.
