Must box area for CT !

From: Sara | Date: 2006-04-13 12:55:29 UTC
Hi Everyone,

We went to Barkhamsted, CT yesterday and visited American Legion &
People's State forests. This is a must box for anyone boxing in CT.
There were tons of boxes.. we were there for a few hours and picked
up 11 stamps! Thank you to Music Woman, The Engraver, and los
banditos for some really fun (and challenging) hikes! We must say
Nature of the Forest Parts 1 & 2 are fun to do with a partner
because both follow the same path.. and each person can be in charge
of their own "part".

We think we heard a moose or a bear, but we weren't going to stick
around to see what was making the loud growling/howling noise. Has
anyone seen moose or bear in Northern CT? The DEP LB's clues mention
that both live in the area.

Some of the boxes (especially one placed by the Engraver) were
damaged or out of place.. I have notified those placers, and tried
to stick the box we found out of place back where we think it was
supposed to be. I know that a lot of LBs carry replacement log
books, but what do you do with an old wet one? (I just put it in a
new baggie to keep it from further damage) Should we have taken it,
tried to dry it out, and send it to the placer? I felt so bad
leaving it there.. but I didn't want to risk angering the placer by
taking it.

Team Happily Ever After
