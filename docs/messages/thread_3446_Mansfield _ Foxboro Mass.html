Mansfield / Foxboro Mass

From: Rick from Boca | Date: 2007-04-07 14:29:49 UTC
I'm working up here for a few months, up from New Jersey.

I am so surprised with the numbers of letterboxes in the immediate
vicinity, as compared to the relative dearth in my area of Central NJ.
As our newest homesteaders, Team Green Dragon, can attest, it doesn't
take very long to have visited every letterbox in our area. We also do
have semi-permanent visitors like the the von der Insels to help.

So far I've only gotten to some of Bicycle George's MBTA boxes, loved
the clues. I have a whole bunch of clues printed, and although it is a
bit nippy out, can't wait to grab them after work. So many boxes in
such a small area!
