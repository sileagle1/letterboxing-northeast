You might want to talk with the person that placed the hitchhiker
before moving it to Mexico. They may love the idea, but it's likely
that Mexican letterboxes don't get much traffic and the hitchhiker
could languish for months or years before it's found, which the
creator of the hitchhiker may not like. So it might not be a bad
idea to ask permission before moving it out of the country.

If the hitchhiker is one of mine, I'd love for it to make a trip to
Mexico, though. :)
