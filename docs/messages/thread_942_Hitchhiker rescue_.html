The Green Dragon Tavern is also a real place -- in Boston, MA, not
too far from Quincy Market and Fanieul Hall. It was opened in the
17th century and is supposedly the place where Boston patriots met to
plan the Boston Tea Party. Apparently it's known in historical
circles as the "headquarters of the American Revolution". Today it's
an Irish pub.
