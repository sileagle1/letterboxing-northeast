> I haven't seen any Lock N Lock at the local Stop and Shop. Any Cape

Yes, the local Stop & Shops do carry them. I just walked in the door
from scouting out the last 2 Stop & Shops in the area for a few more
of the .8 Cup size Lock & Locks (only $1 each!). (I have to replace
the boxes I used for 9 of the Oso Pardo series - they're having some
minor leaking).

Yarmouth, Hyannis & Orleans only have big ones.
Harwich has a couple of the smaller sizes, but not a whole lot.
Dennis has the best selection of the smaller sized ones.

Depending on which store you're in, you can find them in the bulk
and/or seasonal isles (in the middle of the store) or on an end cap
(the back of the store, not near the registers).

Mumma & BB =D
