Saturday in Arcadia, RI

From: dave & diane | Date: 2004-08-29 03:11:12 UTC
Diane and I were determined to find Troy - er, Breaking Hearts at Breakheart. New evidence in hand, we headed to the vast Northern Reaches of Arcadia Park. Today, we found the entirety of the Lucky Charms series - all doing well - and the elusive Loch Ness Monster, Nokkon Wud, and the terrible Wendigo of the Legendary Creature Series #1 and #2. Thereabouts, in a lovely wander about the pond, lay our quarry. We got Breaking Hearts!

Huzzah!

A relic of the 20th century, with its *original logbook* intact, dry and full of Ancient Ones!

Thanks to all the incredible folks who allowed us a truly adventurous day:
Team Primate
Moonstone Baby (with Music Woman in a hearty assist!)
Eric, the Primal Placer.

Cheers!
