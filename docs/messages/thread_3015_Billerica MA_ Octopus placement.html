Billerica MA, Octopus placement

From: morgunjp | Date: 2006-06-23 23:28:04 UTC
I was there during the heat this week, and the mini-water park was full
of happy little kids and their Moms. The water park is free, it has
sprays & falling water. parking was free when I was there (probably
free all the time, but I was not there on a weekend).

If you have small children, one of these hot muggy days would be a nice
time to visit.

BTW - this placement is a bit harder than average, if you try it, don't
be afraid to email for help. The two hitchhikers there need to move on
(they seem to be in the hardest box). Don't worry about leaving a
replacement if you want to take one. If a HH has been there less than
a month, please leave at least one in the hostel.
