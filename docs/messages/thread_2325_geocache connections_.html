From: birder579 | Date: 2005-09-03 02:29:36 UTC
Although I only look for geocaches when they are near my
letterboxes, I signed up with geocaching.com so I can use their
website. The free option gives me everything I need. By looking at
the maps and calling up the cache details you can get a link to the
placer's profile. There you can email the placer

I have done this with the caches that were near to my boxes, and
they all responded positively. They were aware of the difference
between caches and boxes.

I also have a GPS receiver and try to separate my boxes 300 ft or
more from the nearest cache.
