From: uneksia | Date: 2007-07-03 18:10:18 UTC
i agree with connfederate on this one. always have your asbestos suit ready.
the flames can actually be quite funny. also, if you do your research you
will find that many flaming each other are actually the same person. the
exception to this is when the flaming starts on a newbie or a principle,
then my o my, they can get their undies in a bunch. it helps if you realize
that many there are tired of the same old questions and the same old debate.
most, not all, of the members of the main list are dedicated boxers with a
lot of good advice if one cares to listen and learn. the waves of publicity
are scaring some and causing a lot to go underground. i can't say that i
blame them. the publicity scares me as well. i just happen to be of a
different belief. teach the newbies, those that are serious will stick
around and those that aren't will fade away. so, yes, i think the main list
is worth it. just don't take everything you read seriously, think before you
respond so that you don't fan the flames, learn what it there under it all,
and enjoy the banter inbetween.

smile
