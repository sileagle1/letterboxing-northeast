Thanks to Rubaduc for a great series at the Harwinton
Conservation area. After placing the "Harwinton Hummingbird"
and realizing that there are 5 boxes in her series, it was nice to
have some others to find! There were some wonderful mosses
and wildflowers out on the trail. Two of the boxes were not re-hid
very well, but in the usual Maine style, I made sure to take care of
that with a few mini-boulders and natural covering! :-)

After finishing the series we (brother and I) went and checked on
the Hummingbird and it was great to see the box still intact and
with many visitors to boot! I'm glad that you folks don't have a
problem finding the clues after my recent move to pulling them
off the LBNA page.

I placed an urban box this weekend and I have another to
accompany it, but 'yall will have to wait on the clues till they're
more thought out. Urban boxing is quite tricky...
