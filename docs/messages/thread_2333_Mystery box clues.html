There are no boxes on the top of Mt. Katahdin...
There are no boxes in Baxter State Park nor are there boxes allowed in
Acadia National Park. ANP because of obvious reasons, and
Baxter...well, it's been something I've thought about since the day I
started boxing. I think it's wonderful that there are no boxes in this
state park. It's as though it is sacred land off limits in a sense.
I'm not sure why, perhaps it is the remote area, it is also mostly
used by locals.

I am happy for that. There are plenty of boxes in Southern Maine for
everyone to enjoy, and not nearly enough people visit the County.
Here's to the Aroostook Sleuth and the other folks in northern parts
of maine for putting that area on the map.

See you folks at the gathering!
phyto
