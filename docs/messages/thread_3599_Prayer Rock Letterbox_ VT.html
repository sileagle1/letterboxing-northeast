Prayer Rock Letterbox, VT

From: Drew Family | Date: 2007-08-03 17:56:41 UTC
We're sorry to report that it looks as though the historic Prayer Rock
Letterbox may have gone missing. For those that don't know, this was
the first letterbox planted in the US after the landmark Smithsonian
article. (Vermont did have Valley Quest letterboxes before the Chris
Granstrom article, but this was the first of the national wave of LbNA
boxes).

Prayer Rock was carved and planted by The Vermont Viking, one of the
founders of LbNA. The boxing community lost Eric to cancer several
years ago, but his wife Susan has continued to be an inspiration and a
resource to letterboxers ever since.

Sue did replace the old logbook this year, so the log is preserved in
her home. But she is worried that the original stamp is likely missing.
I'm sure that she would welcome any offers from talented carvers to
replace the stamp. Any one have the time?

Sorry to bear this sad news, Jay in CT
