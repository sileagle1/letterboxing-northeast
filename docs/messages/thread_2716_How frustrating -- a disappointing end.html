How frustrating -- a disappointing end

From: Gretchen Caldwell | Date: 2006-02-28 00:41:24 UTC
Recently I posted a request for photo-clue websites, because I thought I had
located a letterbox site, but couldn't remember the clues. So today in 0
degree wind chills, I headed to the site (dog in tow) and nosed around for a
bit. Couldn't find anything and headed back to my car. As I was heading
back (and trying desperately to keep the dog off the ice!), I realized where
I had seen the clue. :-( geocaching.com Ugh!!!!!! I passed what
looked like a bird's nest, with some plastic in it, but didn't want to
disturb a nest, so didn't investigate further. Just went to GC.com and
found the clue, and a hint to "keep your head up." So probably the nest
was fake and that was the cache.



I had nosed around GC.com in an effort to not place boxes near caches, in
hopes of preventing people from messing with the boxes (as much as
possible). So that's where I saw the clue. Sigh.



I did however, decide that it was a potential LB site, just need some
inspiration for the stamp for that area. :-)



BostonRott
