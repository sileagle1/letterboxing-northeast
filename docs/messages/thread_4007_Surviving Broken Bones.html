How can I ever thank the letterboxing community enough? For the last five months you've made my life so much better with all the cards, emails, letters, gifts, surprise party (what a shock that was) and especially the boxes planted by Duc's Fanclub (whoever you are :-). They kept me busy when it seemed as though there might not be a light at the end of the tunnel. And yet, yesterday we all gathered and walked to the infamous tree where this all began. To be walking again is truly a miraculous thing that I'll never take for granted again. Thanks to all who were there and who planted boxes along the way.
Thank you one and all for everything. Lets' get out there and box.
