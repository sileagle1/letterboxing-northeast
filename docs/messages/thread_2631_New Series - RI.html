New Series - RI

From: dave & diane | Date: 2005-12-20 13:37:16 UTC
Well, just in time for the holidays - Dave and I finally got his "All Roads..." Series out. He has done a fine job on the stamps and the story. Look for the clues at the main list.
This is the first time we've done a Letterbox series. Be Kind. Happy Boxing!
We're trying something a bit different and hope it works. We have included the usual "First Finder" Certificate and then because there were more boxes we included second, third and fourth finder certificates. So, hopefully the first finder will get the first one, the second ones to find the second box get the second certificate, etc.
Remember to be discreet while boxing, if possible post a watcher. This particular hiking area is well traveled - even on weekdays and in winter.
Diane
the von der Insels
PS - If you want to try to see the seals, bring binoculars.
