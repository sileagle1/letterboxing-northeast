Phyto and Isosceles picked up the Skipping Christmas boxes today. Some of these
boxes will be replaced this spring in more permanent locations. The Robinson Woods
land trust is an excellent location prime for boxes (hint hint) with plenty of wildlife
viewing opportunities in the spring. It looks as though some of the area is abundant
with vernal pools.

Enjoyed a late evening at Giddy's Serial Mystery box... though a little bit of
bushwacking at the end made it very interesting!! This was an awesome walk.

We visited "Missing You" by Holster-Roo but unfortunately the stamp and felt
covering was frozen solid. Perhaps the box had a leak earlier in the winter. The
logbook was bone dry, but won't be when things start to thaw in the spring.

We also snacked while visiting the Smiling Dragon on the way to "Seizing the Day"
which I must say is a most WONDERFUL box to do at this time of year!!

Phyto also checked on DIZZY (Gillespie that is) and he's doing just fine - blowing the
blues away - plenty of visitors - and quite accessible even during the winter I might
add.

Where have all the letterboxers gone?
