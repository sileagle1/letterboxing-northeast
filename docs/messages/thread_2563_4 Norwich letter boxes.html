4 Norwich letter boxes

From: Kim | Date: 2005-11-13 21:06:22 UTC
The following boxes have been pulled.....The Volunteers, Dragonsfire,
Painters Pallet, and the Stone bridge. We have also pulled the Agent
Orange box in Coventry. The reason for this action is due to yet
another box of ours being destroyed, and more of them not rehidden well
so they could be spotted by anyone. One had been removed by a school
child and brought to school. Thankfully the school bus driver and the
school principle handled the situation and the box was returned. Bear
and I are very frustrated and disappointed in that there are people out
there that do not share the respect for others endeavours that most
letter boxers do. I know we make sure to re-hide every box we find as
well or better than the way we found it. If everyone did this there
might not be many boxes destroyed by vandals. Right now we are not sure
if we will re-plant these boxes. They will remain pulled for the winter
and if we re-plant them it will be in the spring of 2006. Thanks to all
who found our boxes and had the respect to put them back as they were.
