Towpath box check

From: J. BRIDGMAN | Date: 2004-08-04 02:49:16 UTC
I was able to get to my Towpath letterbox Monday. I had left a note
before leaving on vacation that the trail had been blocked off. The
orange netting that had blocked off the trail was open. There is
construction going on but there was no equipment at the area near the
red trail where the box is. There was an orange net blocking the
latter part of the trail, but I walked around it without any problem.
I could hear construction equipment but it was in a different area
of the park. You might want to wear long pants for this hike since
the trail is narrow and the vegitation is quite lush. Watch for
healthy poison ivy when you step over a log.
