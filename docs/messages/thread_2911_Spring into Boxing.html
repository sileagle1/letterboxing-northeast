Huge thanks to Wild Rover and MayEve for yet another successful Springtime Event! It was nice to meet new letterboxers and catch up with the others. The rain held back most of the day so a few one-time only boxes were planted on the grounds of the Riverfront Recreational Center, as well as others in the community room and on the patio. Thanks to Music Woman, who held a carving class and MayEve, who taught Japanese Binding. Also, thanks to all who debuted their letterboxes on that day. Mark and Sue included all the information about the event on their website, including a map of Glastonbury and the location of the new boxes. RTRW carved an awesome Event Stamp!

I truly enjoyed the day! Thanks, everyone! I am happy to be part of this letterboxing community.

Enjoy!
