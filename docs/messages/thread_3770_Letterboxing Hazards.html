Letterboxing Hazards

From: donutz716 | Date: 2008-03-31 15:55:08 UTC
Letterboxing hazards in CT are FAR different than those in Florida where I had one of my most memorable hikes in my letterboxing career. Imagine starting at the trail head where a sign is posted with pictures of poisonous and nonpoisonous snakes in full color and additional information about what to look for as well. Imagine walking right next to a small pond and hearing a loud splash like someone just did a cannonball into it. Imagine reaching through cobwebs of some unknown eight legged creature or being frightened by a bunch of squealing feral pigs that were in the brush! Phew - terrifying, but fun!

Enjoy!
