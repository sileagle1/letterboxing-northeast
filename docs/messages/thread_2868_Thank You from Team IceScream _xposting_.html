From: team_icescream | Date: 2006-04-26 01:27:48 UTC
So THAT'S what an event is like!

Back in December when Ice Scream Sunday first came into being,
oldpatterns and I had never been to an event, and Team Randalstik was
the weathered veteran with one under his belt. Saying we really
didn't know what we were getting into was a bit of an understatement.

But when we finally went "public" with the event, we were immediately
overwhelmed by all the positive feedback, helpful advice and requests
to lend a hand. And it just got better.

When the day finally came, it was truly amazing to see so many
letterboxers in one place, ready to stamp, exchange and box their
hearts out, in spite of the fickle weather. I was happy to finally be
able to put some faces to names, and I missed many. The barn was full
of goodwill, and the generosity of the group was clearly apparent with
all the bountiful potluck food and ice-cream paraphernalia brought by
attendees.

What a great hobby.

We'd like to give special thanks to Warrior Woman, Orion,
VickieandLundster, J.E.S.S., CPAScott and the Lazy Letterboxer for all
their help and support.

I'd like to acknowledge oldpatterns for all her hard work coordinating
the event, and Team Randalstik for his amazing work as stamp/Zine boy.

I do have one complaint however - ok, maybe two. Yes, the weather
could have been nicer. And the people attending the event could have
eaten more ice-cream! I now have approximately 2 gallons of cookies
'n cream in my freezer - this is not a good thing.

But otherwise, myself, oldpatterns and Team Randalstick were very
happy to host you all.

Thank you!
Eyze on the Prize
