I day that if you click MA from the big nationwide map you should get
MA at that point, not the three southern NE states. When you click
Nebraska, Kansas doesn't come with it, so why do CT and RI come with
MA (or any similar combination)?

As for dividing the state, you could break up by the Cape, but if you
do, include the islands (Nantucket and Martha's Vineyard) with the Cape.

You could also divide by county (similar to most other states in the
database) Challenge with this is that counties are really used or
known much here in New England.

Dividing Eastern MA into North-Eastern and South-Eastern might be a
good idea.
