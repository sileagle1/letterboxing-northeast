So disappointed....sniff sniff

From: mindizney@aol.com | Date: 2004-06-18 01:48:07 UTC
Bluebird and I went boxing today after school (she was in school, I wasn't).
We decided to meet in Wallingford/North Haven at Wharton Brook SP. I parked
really close to the main gate, hoping that they would only close the inner
gates. We went to a different SF and found boxes there, only to get back to
Wharton Brook at about 8:35 p.m. (it was still light out!). Unfortunately, all of
the gates were closed and my car was locked inside! Well, after checking to
see if both gates are the guard shack were really locked and didn't just
"look" locked, we walked to the house next door which looked like the ranger's
house. As we approached, we noticed that the gate between the house and the state
park was open, so I quickly got my car and drove out the gate (yes, I know it
said "not open to the public", but I REALLY needed my car for work tomorrow
morning!). Some of you may know that Letterboxer2002 had a call on his phone
after boxing at night in a CT State Forest, so I was SO sure that I would have
a call from the DEP or Police on my phone when I got home. Sure enough, the
message light was blinking on the phone. As I listened to the message, it
wasn't the DEP or the police... it was my cousin just saying HI. I was SO
disappointed!!!! Oh well.... just remember.... they really DO mean sunset!

Happy Hunting everyone!
MW
