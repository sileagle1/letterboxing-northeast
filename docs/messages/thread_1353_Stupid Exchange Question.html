OK I am sorry because I know most people will find this dumb but I
just wanted clarification. I always believed that an exchange was you
putting your stamp in someone else's book. However someone recently

Hi- We've been boxing for a year and a half, and as far as I know, an
exchange is when you and another boxer exchange personal stamp images
in each other's logbooks. If you carved your signature stamp, then
so be it, but it is not mandatory that you do so. Be prepared for a
crazed experience of exchanges at this huge MMM Gather!!! Happy
Boxing Everyone-- Sunshine :0)
