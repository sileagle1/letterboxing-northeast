CT: Spring into Boxing Gather

From: Pam | Date: 2006-05-14 14:08:37 UTC
A very special THANK YOU goes out to Wild Rover and May Eve for a great boxing gathering yesterday! The gather was in the brand spankin' new Riverfront Community Center in Glastonbury, which is absolutely beautiful and the conference room was the perfect size for our group! The rain pretty much stayed away for the whole day too, which allowed some of us to get out and box!

I do have to admit.... it was nice to have the one day only boxes close to the venue, and not out on long trails. That way, we had plenty of time to exchange and visit with other boxers. Also, the boxes that were making their debut are permanent boxes, which is nice because we didn't feel pressured to go out and box, and we could really have fun at the gather.

Thanks also to W.R., M.E., Donutz, Enologist and the Pepe's for the great boxes! Lotsa Lincolns were great, as were the celtic and spring symbols that I found thus far!!! Thanks also to MayEve for the Japanese Stab binding tutorial! First coptic stitch, now Japanese Stab binding... I can't wait to make LOTS of logbooks!

I hope everyone had fun! Thanks for a great day!

Music Woman
