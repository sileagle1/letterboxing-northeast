From: solor8008 | Date: 2004-03-11 19:59:19 UTC
Amy,

We also had a heck of a time tracking down the correct people and
the booklet. It seems to be out on the fringe of regular AMC
activities. I finally picked one up in a trinket shop in downtown
Quincy called Abigail's Crossing (they have a website).

Supposedly, the booklets are also available at:
Bookport, 21 Main Street, Hingham
Hingham Public Library, 66 Leavitt St., Hingham
South Shore Natural Science Center, Jacobs Lane, Norwell

Be aware that not all the quests are left out for the entire year.
Most are taken in for the winter. I think a new booklet is published
each year so if you persist in finding the right person by
telephone, you may be able to find out when the 2004 version is out.

We had a lot of fun doing these quests and exploring the south shore
and the patch is nice besides.

See you soon!

Solor
