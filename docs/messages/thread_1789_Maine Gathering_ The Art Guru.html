Gosh, I know that I'm not an official Nor'easter, even though I was born in Boston, but
I feel compelled to make a comment here. I follow Mark Pepe's page religiously and
noticed that there is a carving contest as part of the planned Art Guru festivities. I am
pleased that it isn't humanly possible for me to be there since I'm on the left coast.
With the likes of Legerdemaine, TeamGreenDragon, Jay Drew, Phyto, etc. I would feel
totally outclassed by the potential carving talent in attendance.

I do hope that newbies and those with less time to devote or more modest skills with
their carving tools aren't discouraged by this. I would never want one of my stamps to
be judged against another letterboxer's. I hope that the only person I ever have to
compete against in this arena is myself. As time goes by, my carvings get better and
better but I don't think I'll ever be as masterful as some... Peace, Funhog

Hello Everyone,
I am writing to try to explain the purpose behind this "Contest" If there was a
better word to use I would but I did not want it to be a competition. What it is
all about is a group of people working for an important cause. I know it
sounds silly that acrving a stamp with this theme can have an impact but the
power of postive thought in every carving will make a difference. I was even
hoping that people would plant them in there area after so all the other
letterboxers that go and visit that box will be blessed with that good peace of
mind. When I vote at the gathering I wont be looking for the best carving skills
but the carving that means the most to me.

It was wrong of me to keep the list only open to people coming to the
gathering. My only reasons behind it were that of the postage cost of mailing
everyones art copies back. If you wish to join in the experince and are willing
to mail a few dollars along with your art work I would be happy to mail you
back the collection of art work if you are unable to attend the gathering.

With that all said there is only a few more days to sign up. Please e-mail me
off list if you are interested and for more details go to Mark and Sue Pepes
web page.

Thanks For Your Time,
