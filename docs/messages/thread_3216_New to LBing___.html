From: Lock Wench | Date: 2006-09-22 13:12:41 UTC
Hi Amy! I'm a NY boxer and there are many of us on this list. There is
also a board for the Entire State of NY (we have an identity
problem...lol) and you can find many people in your area there as
well. There are even some gatherings coming up on October 8th near
Albany at Thacher State Park called Wonders of the World and After
Wonders. A GREAT way for your family to quickly find boxing buddies!
You pay the regular park entrance per car fee ($6) and bring a potluck
item. Here's some links for you:

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/LbNY/

www.atlasquest.com (Post on the NY board. found by looking at
"Boards", then "United States Letterboxing", then "New York")

Here's the link for the upcoming event:

http://www.atlasquest.com/events/event.html?gEventId=119

You can find it again on Atlasquest under "Toolbox" and then "Events"

Welcome to the addiction!

LW

Hiya Amy,

Welcome! Here are some handy links that can help you get started.

Letterboxing.org:
http://www.letterboxing.org/GettingStarted/

Silent Doug's site:
http://www.letterboxing.info/
Doug probably has the best info site on Hitchhikers and links to
regional chat lists.

AtlasQuest:
http://www.atlasquest.com/
Ryan has a link set up "Code of Conduct" that is really good for all
boxers to read.

Newboxers group:
http://groups.yahoo.com/group/newboxers/
Where there is no dumb question, and is "the" place for new letterboxers.
