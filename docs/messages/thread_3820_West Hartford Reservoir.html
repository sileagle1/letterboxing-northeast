West Hartford Reservoir

From: bobsandy98 | Date: 2008-06-03 00:58:58 UTC
We found a letterboxing book-pink with a yellow duck on the cover-on a
rock near the back parking lot of the MDC reservoir off Route 4 in West
Hartford. We couldn't tell if it was part of a letterbox or if it was
someones book that they use to stamp in. We didn't want to leave it on
the rock in case it rained or someone took it so we put it under the
eaves of the mapboard that is right next to where you drive in to that
back parking lot. Hope someone reads this who knows of someone missing
a book or a part of a letterbox.
