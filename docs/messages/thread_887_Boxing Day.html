Boxing Day

From: buddyandpigtographer | Date: 2004-06-27 22:31:21 UTC
Pigtographer and I headed out this morning to find boxes in Warwick &
Pawtucket. After heading to City Park in Warwick, we quickly found
Smiling Tree and afterwards, Brushneck Cove. We drove a bit and
later found Low Tide (the geese kept a VERY close eye on us!).

We went to a Paw Sox game and saw them win in the 9th inning, 2 outs
and and RBI brought the score to 4-3. After, we went to Slater Park
and found Fox in the Box. We went off to find Fanny's Footsteps, but
got very confused and gave up after a very long search.

How is everybody? Seems like there's a bunch of new boxers to meet
and exchange with!! Sorry we wern't able to get to the MH Gathering
today...us Rhode Islanders don't like those oh-so-long drives!! I
have a lot of reading on this list to catch up on!! Hope to hear
from a few of you soon.

Buddy

P.S. If anyone would be interested in finding Fanny's Footsteps with
us, e-mail us at buddyandpigtographer@... -- the box is
located in Slater Park, Pawtucket, RI. There's also one other
letterbox (which we found today) in the park.
