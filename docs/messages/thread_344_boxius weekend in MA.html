boxius weekend in MA

From: Amy Barker | Date: 2004-03-08 12:19:40 UTC
Well, I had a fun boxius weekend in Massachusetts this past weekend.
Exhausting, but fun.

On Saturday, Camera Obscura and I found Team Green Dragon's Let It Be
series! It was a long walk, and rainy, but worth every second. Those stamps
are AMAZING! The Forest they are in is really lovely too.

On Sunday we met Solor and Binx in Bolton. This was a lot of fun for me, as
I have never letterboxed with more than one other person, and much of the
time I am by myself. They are super cool and we chit chatted and swapped
gossip and stories all day. We also stopped at the Nashoba Winery, which was
fantastic! We found Gould Letterbox, Powder Hill House, and Welch Pond
Letterbox before I has to leave. I am definitely going back to that town.
Those boxes are fun.

I am so happy that spring is on the way! It's was a gorgeous weekend, and
fun boxing!
