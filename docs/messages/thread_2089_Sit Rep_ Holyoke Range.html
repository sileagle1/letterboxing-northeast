Sit Rep: Holyoke Range

From: Jim Plouffe | Date: 2005-05-21 00:32:57 UTC
We took a wonderful hike up in Amherst for the Holyoke Range boxes.

They were in place, however, the first box was full of water. The log
was dry, but the actual box needs to be replaced due to its condition.

The second box was dry, but the log was really damp. A lot of the prior
stamps had run a bit, but other than that, the box was in fairly good
condition.

A note to anyone else going for these..... Don't make the same mistake
we did by going up the wrong mountain and searching for 20 minutes in
the wrong place, although the work out of climbing two mountains in one
day was a bit exhilarating.

Have a great weekend everyone,
