FYI: TreadLightly training

From: defygravity2001 | Date: 2006-09-11 02:14:52 UTC
There is a TreadLightly training session happening in Connecticut in
November. This is a great educational opportunity for anyone who
mountain bikes or rides any kind of motorized trail vehicle, or who
works with youth or adults who are mechanized or motorized outdoor
recreationists. Courses such as this are offered in the North-Eastern
US only about once in five years or so.
