CT: Blue Moon on Breadloaf Mountain

From: T Prindle | Date: 2004-07-31 22:57:42 UTC
"Once in a Blue Moon", as they say... Tonight marks our second full
moon in one month, the first showing it's full face the eve of July
2nd. And on this night which blends it's way into August, our great
new Breadloaf Mountain box gazes wistfully up at the lonely Blue
Moon, which has dutifully kept it's company until the boxer comes
along. The Blue Moon envelops the sweeping Housatonic valley in it's
cobalt glow and smiles down encouragingly upon the Breadloaf Mountain
box "They will come, they will see, once in a Blue Moon".

Rush Gatherer, and her sister, JPH the LeafLady
