CT State Stamp Series has a Winner - but wait - There's MORE!

From: Mark Pepe | Date: 2004-06-16 02:43:47 UTC
Hi boxers:

Wanda Miner is the winning letterboxer to first complete this 8
county series and we offer her our congratulations for her effort!

In an effort to keep this quest interesting, The Engraver has
suggested that we give you other boxers 1 year to complete this
series from today - 6/15/2005, after which a drawing will be held
from all of the eligible boxers who have completed this series for 10
personalized gifts.

"So 10 lucky letterboxers out of all who finish will get a
personalized gift from The Engraver!" [Thank you, Don Pardo]

I thought we would end this fun series [not that it hasn't hit a few
bumps in the road!! - HH!!] with - what else - a gathering. Look for
details to come for the CT Stamp Series Gathering to take place some
time next summer.

Until then, get all 8 stamps, email me with your scanned images, or
email me for our snail mail address. And if you complete the series,
your name will be added to the CT Stamp Series Hall of Fame page
located on our webpage.

Have fun and just get out there & box!!! [and complete the CT series!]

Mark & Sue Pepe, along with The Engraver
