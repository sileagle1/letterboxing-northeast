Now, as for your questions and comment regarding the state of origin.
Good point!!! Folks on this list sort of take for granted that
everyone knows what state, but even that aside - I have pointed out
that there is a Portland, Connecticut and a Portland, Maine.

And, to kill two birds with the same stone so to speak, I designed the
LBNE patch and there was a poll on this talk list regarding which
states to include. Currently, our patch inclues Maine, New York,
Vermont, New Hampshire, Massachusetts and Rhode Island. Therefore,
when Rubaduc was posting about Camden the other day, she meant Camden,
Maine on the coast.

That doesn't mean that you can't disuss other states across the
country in the "Northeast" talk list, it just means that our
discussions primarily focus on those states. I hardly ever hear from
folks in NJ, but I do know that Martian Maggot is from that area and
she's currently in Unity, Maine as well as TeamGreenDragon who will be
moving to New Jersey soon! So, I'm sure that New Jersey will be on the
map soon :)

Hope that answers some of your questions!
