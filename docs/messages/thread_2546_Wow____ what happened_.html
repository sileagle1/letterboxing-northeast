Wow.... what happened?

From: Steve and Heidi | Date: 2005-11-07 09:34:23 UTC
This list used to be so cordial, friendly and nice....

Its amazing how individuals and little groups can publicly battle like
this. These discussions really should be taken off list and settled
best (perhaps?) by just ignoring each other. This particular list has
interesting dynamics because MANY of us have met face to face and
really know each other better than just "penpals". I find it hard to
believe that neighbors can sleep at night when they are out bashing
each other in front of mutual friends. Remember that there's always 3
sides to a story and that writing to a public forum while you still
have a clenched fist often results with egg on your face.

As a moderator on the big list, I've always steered those who send me
emails to the effect of "Leaving the big list because of the
bitterness" towards these regional lists because they tend to be
kindler and gentler. I'm beginning to rethink that decision and
guidance.

As far as clue removal from the site....go for it! But bring them back
once the storm blows through. There's too many good boxes out there
that need to be shared and enjoyed by all! If you are worried about
vandalism, make your box harder by either clues, tricks, or longer
hikes. Not only will you avoid the "casual" letterboxer who is after
the numbers, but you will make the hike more enjoyable for the rest of
us.

-Steve in Ledyard
