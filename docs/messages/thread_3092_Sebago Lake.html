Sebago Lake

From: phinstarr | Date: 2006-07-29 03:18:42 UTC
After meeting Sojourner at Sebago Lake in Maine, we headed for a
couple of boxes...then for Douglas Mountain. What a great hike and a
fantastic view. Later we jumped in the lake to cool off. The following
day I experienced the beauty of the trails around this lake by finding
Sojourner's plants. There is wild life galore, fungi, blueberries, and
some very large deer hoofs along with a snake or two! Very pleasant
walk with few hills.
