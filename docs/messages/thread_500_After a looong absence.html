I've not been able to box or even read messages for a while.. and
now, after days of catching up it seems I missed quite a bit.

weekend with my scouts. ...which, for those of you who may be

a mapsurfer box and several other oldies (hopefully).

the sun was setting over the water. It was a beautiful evening.
I still have one more HH to liberate which I hope to do tomorrow..
rain or shine. Finally, I've been a bad, bad letterboxer and held
onto Aztec Gold longer than I had any right to. I'll mail it out
Monday FIRST thing. It's nice to be back! cheers:) k*
