After a relaxing afternoon at Scarborough Beach, we stopped at Point
Judith for that letterbox.

The logbook and stamp are fine, but water had gotten to the stamp
pad and made a mess. Unwrapped the stamp from a very blue, very wet
paper towel. We re-bagged items and removed the pad and its bag and
left happy but with blue hands!

So, if you visit this box, be sure to bring a stamp pad. Oh, I would
recommend long pants and shoes, not sandals, if you are in growing
season as the path is quite overgrown. (We managed without but are
now praying there was no poison ivy...)

Take care,
Cavy Lovers
