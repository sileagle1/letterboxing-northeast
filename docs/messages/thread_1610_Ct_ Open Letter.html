This is an open letter to all boxers, but is really for Jay Drew.
Today Lobsta and I took a walk at Milo Light and for old times sake
went to Jay Drew's Milo Light box. This was the first box that Lobsta
and I found and it began our obsession with this 'hobby' The log book
was full and the Lady is charge of the Preserve had left a new log book
with a note saying "Thanks to all who visit." That was so nice, and
since the box was now crowded with both log books, I tucked the first
one in my pocket to give to Jay. What a Treasure !! He planted it in
December of 1999. There are so many stamps and names that are now
familiar and it was great fun to read . I know that Jay will be
pleased to see the enjoyment he has given to so many folks. Now there
is a new log book, and fresh pages waiting , in Mark Pepe's words,
"get out there and box " !
Jay, I will hand this over to you with a big smile.
Butterfly
