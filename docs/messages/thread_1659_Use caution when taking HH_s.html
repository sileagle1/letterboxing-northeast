Use caution when taking HH's

From: swampyankeesunshine | Date: 2004-12-04 00:08:21 UTC
This is to everyone- newbies and oldies alike!! Recently we were at a
box where there was a HH. The name of it was new to us, so we
excitedly opened the bag to check it out. When we opened the
logbook, we were already stamped in as the first finders and we
recognized it as a box we'd been to. We didn't look at the stamp
because we thought the placers had decided to make their permanent
box into a HH. I emailed them when we got home to ask about it and
found out that they hadn't done any such thing!!! Fortunately the
box where we found it was very near our house, so I went back and
retrieved it. The stamp is, indeed, from a HH that we had never
found. I'm meeting the placer tomorrow to get things back in order,
since he went to his box and found the right stamp, but wrong
logbook!! It's just lucky that we don't live very far from each
other, and the home of the permanent box is also close by. This is
not meant to flame ANYONE, but just to remind us all to take an extra
second to make sure you're doing what you mean to do!!! Happy Boxing
Everyone-Sunshine :0)
