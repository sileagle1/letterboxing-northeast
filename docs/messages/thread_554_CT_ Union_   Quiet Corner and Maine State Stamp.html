We tackled the epic hike today. 6 1/2 hours in Bigelow Hollow and
Nipmuck SF. 11 miles, 6 boxes, 1 hitch hiker, and we're exhausted.
What an incredible hike. If you can swing a full day hiking, go for
it! We liked how you came back by the car from time to time, makes
for a nice central area to eat lunch and hit the little hiker's room.

The scenery was incredible. The terrain tough at times. Its a perfect
hike with places to linger, Maddie got lots of swims in, and we just
cannot rave enough about what a fun day we had! All 5 of Jay's boxes
and Lenny /Kathy's Maine State Stamp were in excellent shape. Picked
up the Ebenexer Tuttle HH and will move it south a ways.

Thanks to the placers! What fun!

-Steve, Heidi and Madras
