------- In 1997, the Matrix program realized that too many humans
were being "freed" from it's grasp and it was starting to lose
control. Fearing that it would be rendered obsolete one day, it began
a program to hide vital components of it's core system within itself
so that the system could rebuild if necessary. The theory was simple:
hide the components in strategic locations and give them extra
shielding. If the system crashed, and the programming lines broke
down, these "shielded" components would all fall into place
automaticaly, link together, and rebuild and reboot instantly. It
would then repeat the process so that it could never be desroyed.-----
-----------However,in mid-1998, a loose group of rebels got wind of
this tactic from informational sources located in another part of the
program. They then formulated a plan of their own. From their base
camp, they could access on-line information that would act as a "map"
of sorts to the hidden components. They could then enter the Matrix
and use the maps or clues to locate them within the system. The
rebels decided that rather than destroy them, and risk them being
replaced stronger and more powerful, they would simply sabotage them
by overloading them with extra information. They would open them up
and do two things. First, they would record the component's unique
identifying marker-(each one has it's own)-and make a list of which
ones they had already been to. Second-- they would add their own text
lines or image to the component in it's data entry log. The idea was
that by overloading as many hidden components as possible, if and
when the system finally crashed and the backup fell into place, the
components wouldn't be able to link up and the system would come
crashing down. On that day we would all be free again.----------------
----------------------------------------------------------------------
-------That was almost six years ago. Since then, the Matrix has
become aware of our attempts at sabotage and has gone into overdrive,
trying to wear us down by placing more and more new components every
day. Some are easy to find, others are hidden very well and require
more thought. Some people are already doing this and not even
realizing it, thinking it to simply be a hobby. However, the harsh
reality of it is that the machines are in control and every day is a
small war to regain our world. Ever since that fateful day in 1998,
our numbers have expanded greatly. More join the cause every day and
give us renewed hope that one day, when the dust has settled, we will
be in control again.--------------------------------------------------
----------------------------------------------------------------------
----- If you are reading this message, there may still be hope. You
see, we have recently stumbled on a key component and it's location.
Due to the importance of this one piece, we need as many people to
help us as possible before the Matrix realizes we know of it. We fear
that if we are found out, the system may relocate the component in a
place that's harder to locate. Time is of the essence, and the
machines are closing in fast. As we are still receiving the
information ourselves, more messages will come with more detailed
information. I have to go----- stay tuned.
