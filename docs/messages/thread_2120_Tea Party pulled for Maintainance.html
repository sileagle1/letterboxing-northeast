From: thelionandsanddollar | Date: 2005-05-31 11:09:19 UTC
We spent a loely afternoon in Rocky Neck doing box maintainance
yesterday. All 3 tea party boxes were still there, and the first 2
were in pretty good shape. Alas, the top had cracked on the 3rd box,
and the box was filled up with rain water. We were able to salvage
the stamp, but lost everything else. All 3 boxes are in for
maintainance and repair, and we will post when we put them back out.
Thanks to all who visited these- we have been enjoying reading the
logbooks. These were some of our first boxes, so they are near and
dear to our hearts.
