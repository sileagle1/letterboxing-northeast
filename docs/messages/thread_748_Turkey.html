What a beautiful day to be out letterboxing. We found
18 out of the 21 My Favorite Musicals boxes. When we
returned to our car, there was a wild turkey in the
middle of the road, first standing, then lying down.
Another car had stopped and we tried to shoo the
turkey off the road. When I waved my hat at it, it
came at me and chased me around a bit. He wouldn't
let up! I thought it was going to fly at me at one
point. Finally, a bicyclist got between it and me and
I escaped into the car. I guess I'll let the wildlife
fend for themselves from now on. ;-)
