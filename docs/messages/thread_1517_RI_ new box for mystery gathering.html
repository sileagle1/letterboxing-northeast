Apollo's Power

From the smoke hole in the longhouse, take the path at 320 above the
water's edge. Follow the freshmen left. At the next intersection
take the path at 140. From here, go 135 steps along the trail until
you see a stump on the left. The stump is about 10 steps from the
trailside with another stump 10 steps past that. Look under the
fallen log attached to the first stump at the stump's foot. When
you're finished, continue along the trail in the direction you were
initially heading to make the circuit back to the longhouse.
