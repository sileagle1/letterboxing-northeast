A Little Birdie Told Me and Corn Maiden

From: Team Safari | Date: 2006-07-11 00:14:38 UTC
I'm currently going through years of logbooks trying to log the stamps onto AQ. One of the stamps I have is "A Little Birdie Told Me" and it's a small bird about an inch square with the wings extended upward. It's body has a sort of Aztec or native American look to it... cut like an angular maze within the border of the bird's outline. The name of the box is part of the stamp. We got this image at the Mystery Gathering in RI on Sept. 20, 2003. We didn't make any notation on it as to what kind of letterbox it was or how we got the image. :-( [Not the only one we were remiss in adding important info to our logbook over the years.]

There is a box by this name that is a stationary box in Washington by Wingnut so I don't think it's that box, and I know it's not the HH carved by Green Tortuga because he wrote back to me and the description of his doesn't fit.

Can anybody identify this box for me?

Also, it's on the same page as Corn Maiden, which was planted for that event, IIRC, right near the site, and I can't remember who carved that one. Can anyone enlighten me?

Mary of Team Safari
