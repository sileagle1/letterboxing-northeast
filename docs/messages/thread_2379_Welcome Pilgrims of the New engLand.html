Welcome Pilgrims of the New engLand

From: Tom Noone | Date: 2005-09-16 17:53:17 UTC
Rejoice all ye weary travelers for redemption is at hand. We shall gather
together at the spring to wash away our former selves. Repent! Repent! I
say. Forgo your mindless pursuit of superficial rewards. Lay down your
burdens with your fellow seekers for the day so that you may box anew.

Heed my call! For many years now we have met and we shall meet again just
days before you gather with your family and friends to be thankful for what
has been given to you. That place which is old shall be new again, there
shall our congregation meet.

I seek a host of angels to greet my wayward flock. Let those among you who
can capture their divine image be known to me.

Patience, patience. All will soon be made clear.

Yours in eternal pursuit,
The Right Rev Thomas Noone
