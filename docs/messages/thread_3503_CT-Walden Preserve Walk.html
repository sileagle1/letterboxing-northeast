CT-Walden Preserve Walk

From: Drew Family | Date: 2007-05-29 14:29:54 UTC
The wonderful folks at www.salemlandtrust.org are sponsoring a
letterboxing walk at the Walden Preserve in Salem on Saturday June
9th at 10am. This is not a Gathering, but should be lots of fun for
anyone who would like to go. I recommend bringing a small ($5-10)
donation to the land trust if you come, as we are trying to raise
funds to purchase a new preserve.

Lots of wonderful letterboxes nearby if you work just a little to
find the clues ;)

Hope to see you there, Jay & Margaret
