Re: [LbNE] Blue Diamonds

From: Andrea Roher | Date: 2006-09-29 03:04:09 UTC
It was mentioned on the write up on the front page of Atlas Quest that
they have intentionally limited the overall number of Blue Diamonds.
That way as more people rank, the "good" boxes will lose their Blue
Diamonds leaving only "great" boxes... then only "excellent" boxes,
etc. They also mentioned that with the limited number of ratings to
start with there would be sudden jumps until there was more of a
baseline to average against. Finally, they said that the actual
formula was rather complex and something they were not going to share
for fear of someone trying to "cheat" the system.

Visit http://www.atlasquest.com/help/bluediamond.html for all this
information and more.
