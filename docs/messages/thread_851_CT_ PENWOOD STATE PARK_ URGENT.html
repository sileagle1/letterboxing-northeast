OK... I was up doing many of the Penwood boxes today (though I did not do the
series in question, which is Sneaky, Beaky and Leaky) and met up with two
other letterboxers who notified me that there is a wild cat's den right near the
"Leaky" box. The cat was quite agitated and angry that people were near her
den. (She may have had cubs in the den?) I was asked by the other boxers I
met today NOT to do the third "Leaky" box.

If you are planning on doing the Sneaky, Beaky and Leaky series, please be
very careful and perhaps leave the third box for another time. I've already
e-mailed Rubaduc to notify her, and perhaps either she or I will notify the Parks
service. Since I haven't done the series yet, and I didn't see the cat first
hand, I'm not sure if I have the authority to do so.

Anyway, be VERY careful out there!!!!!
