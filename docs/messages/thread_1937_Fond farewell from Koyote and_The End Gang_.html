Fond farewell from Koyote and"The End Gang"

From: theendgang | Date: 2005-04-07 00:21:21 UTC
We will be going off line starting April 11th while we relocate from
Conn. to Virginia. During our transition, please direct correspondence
regarding "The End Gang" and/or Koyote's boxes to Mark Pepe. I will
stay in touch with him periodically during our move. All of our boxes
will remain in place including "The Four Directions" series. Thanks to
those wonderful people who will look after them from time to time. I
will visit enough to maintain them as well. I will be able to be
contacted on the mid-atlantic site by the end of May.
We wish all our boxing friends safe trails and thank you for
wonderful times at gatherings and on trail. We will miss you.
Koyote and The End Gang
