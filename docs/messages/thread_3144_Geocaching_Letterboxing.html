From: bobsandy98 | Date: 2006-08-17 11:53:31 UTC
We geocache & letterbox, ( 850 letterboxes, 250 geocaches) so maybe we
can offer a little insight on what could have happened to your boxes-
there are always new letterboxers & new geocachers, who aren't really
sure what they are doing when they first start these hobbies. It's very
possible that whoever found the letterbox was a beginner geocacher who
was confused about what they found. There are many, many places where
geocaches & letterboxes are within feet of eachother, and if you don't
know about letterboxing & only starting out, you could find a box &
think it was a geocache. Both are great hobbies & the people we have
met that do either (or both)on the whole are nice people. The woods are
big enough for both hobbies!
