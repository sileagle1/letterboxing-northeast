Flamingo Caper and more

From: Pamela Smith Lenox | Date: 2005-10-03 16:27:23 UTC
Well, my daughter had today off from school, so we made a long weekend
of it and are just now getting home.

First, a big THANK YOU to everyone involved in helping out with this
event. This was only our second gathering, but we had an absolute ball.
All of the actors were great and my daughter enjoyed them so much that
she asked for autographs from those who showed up to the party
afterwards. lol. The Pink Panther kids were awesome! And BOY did our
exchange count go up! It was really fun to meet so many of you.

We stayed one more night at the Super 8 (never again... the manager
tried to overcharge us for the room and was *amazingly* condescending
when I insisted on paying what was on my original sheet...... the one
dark spot on our weekend) then switched to another hotel for last night
and spent Sunday out on the Rail Trail getting the Build Your Own
Burger series, the Dave's Fave's series, and Everybody Loves Ginger.
All stamps and boxes seemed to be in great shape, though a couple of
inkpads may need replacing after a few more finders. My poor old knee
barely made it back to our car, but it was worth it and we're
tantalizingly close to F100 now. :-)

So, the Best Western/Fitchburg was really nice, the rate was as good as
at the Super 8, and it looked to be right near a nice bit of
parkland....... maybe another gathering next fall? :-)

Thanks again to all who helped even a bit with this gathering. We had a
wonderful time.

pezpam (and Miss Buggy)
