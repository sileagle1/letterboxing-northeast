From: Don | Date: 2004-04-26 10:27:00 UTC
To all the letterboxers that showed up to stamp in at my Living the
Scout Law Letterbox Series this weekend during the BVD Earthday
Camporee I thank you. I'm also sorry I missed you. With running the
event I had to take care of a lot of administrative work like loading
the canoes back on the trailer. One thing I didn't give much thought
to when first starting letterboxing was true north VS Magnetic North.
One of my troop adults (Who is Hooked on LBing Now) complained my
azmuths were off. Well his compass was set for True north. The
difference between magnetic and true north on our area is about 14
1/2 degrees N/W. So I'll be adding this little bit of info to my
clues. Also the eight letterbox in the series is planted and I'll
post it tonight.

Not All Who Wander Are Lost
Trail Turtle
Scoutmaster
TRoop 12 Berkeley Ashton, RI
Our 25th year
