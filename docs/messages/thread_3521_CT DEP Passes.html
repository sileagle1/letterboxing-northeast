CT DEP Passes

From: davyschris@aol.com | Date: 2007-06-10 20:57:50 UTC
Everyone,

I just got a job at Wadsworth Falls State Park on weekends. I found out
today that the season passes for all the state parks are $40, and on June 29,
they're going to raise that to $50. If you're planning on doing lots of boxing
this summer, this pass makes sense! If you're planning to buy one, I'd suggest
stopping by a state park near you and picking one up before June 29. :)

Also... those among us who qualify for this probably already know, but if you
don't, here's some more good information: Senior citizens can get in free to
the state parks! You just have to get something called a Charter Oak Pass,
which is available to all seniors by sending in a copy of your CT drivers
lisence to the DEP. Unfortunately, I didn't write the address down, but I'm sure
you could find it, or a good contact phone number, on the DEP website!

Happy boxing...

And keep your eyes peeled for a limited time box you can get by stopping by
the Wadsworth Falls ticket booth on weekends this summer and saying hello to
me! (I look fantastic in khaki and green.) You might also want to work on my
Naxscio mystery box if you're going to be seeing me anytime in the near future.
;)
