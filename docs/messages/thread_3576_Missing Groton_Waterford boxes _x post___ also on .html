I guess we've all heard about this sad situation by now.

As some of you know, I'm a park ranger for the DEP this summer. I talked to
my supervisor, who is a full-time ranger. I asked him if the DEP had changed
its policy as far as letterboxing, and he said no. He said that letterboxes
are welcome in the state parks and forests. He also told me that individual
parks can set their own rules, and that he's not in charge of Bluff Point and
Harkness, so he can't speak for them specifically.

Today, Music Woman talked to a ranger at Bluff Point who told her that
they're still OK with boxes too.

I'm guessing we're dealing with a muggle here. I can't believe that someone
would put THAT much time and energy into pulling people's boxes. The ones at
Avery Point are missing as well, and those are not DEP boxes.

If anyone has boxes in Groton, Waterford and/or the surrounding area and
wants me to pull them for them for safekeeping until this blows over, please email
me. I'm a teacher on summer vacation and can do that for you this week if
you'd like.

Just trying to do my part. This is a sad development.
