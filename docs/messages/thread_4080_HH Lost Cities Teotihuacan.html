<What was your boxing adventure this weekend?>



Mine was at Sleeping Giant. Im trying to get all the boxes there:
http://sheltontrails.blogspot.com/2009/11/back-to-giant.html



I had the worst stealth situation Ive ever encountered. I had just
finished stamping in about 20 feet from the hide, when a group of four
college student came and sat down between me and the hiding spot facing the
hiding spot. They were so rude! There was so much space up there and they
just came in and practically sat on top of me, I mean, I could almost touch
them. They had French accents or something like it. Its like, when an
elevator opens and one person is inside, do you go stand right next to them,
or space yourself out? These people just did not get that. And they would
not leave. I finally pulled out my cell phone and tried to be the obnoxious
cell phone user, but I was getting embarrassed at myself :-D. Then I took
out my camera and started mulling about taking close up pictures in front of
them of grass heads and such like a dork. Finally, after about ½ hour, they
left and I was free to rehide. Phew!
