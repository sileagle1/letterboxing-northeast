CT: Route 2 Tunnel

From: Pam | Date: 2006-08-13 02:25:45 UTC
FYI: For those of you who want to do Evil Queen, Cruella, Lady
Tremaine, Town Line, and My Favorite Winter Things at the same time,
the Route 2 Tunnel's water has receded! (Just make sure that you plant
a car out on the Dickenson Road trailhead for Lady Tremaine!)

On your way to Cruella, you go over the dirt bridge before the Y
intersection.... Look downhill to your right. There's the tunnel.
Rubaduc had fun today going through the tunnel! Just make sure you
wear waterproof boots.... it's not totally out of water! Once you go
through the tunnels for both the eastbound and westbound lanes, search
around... you should be near the last two boxes of Favorite Winter
Things!

Since I haven't done this yet.... I'm looking forward to it! Maybe I
can get PSU Fan to go too......

Music Woman

Re: CT: Route 2 Tunnel

From: rubaduc2001 | Date: 2006-08-13 19:32:06 UTC
-No tube, this tunnel. Full fledged cement double structure of great
height. It was totally awesome in there and because you go under one
side, out into the median, then under the other side...it's not
really too dark but flashlights were good to have.
The west bound structure had a date of 1949 above one of the tunnel
openings. I hope my pictures come out.
There's also a good path just as the pavement ends that goes directly
to it.
Rubaduc
