Witches Hollow Series!

From: jbean1164 | Date: 2004-10-06 21:13:34 UTC
Hey! Yesterday afternoon a friend and I and our 2 daughters 5 & 7
finished the Witches Hollow series at Chatfield Hollow! We started on
Friday afternoon. There are 4 hikes ranging in length from 1/2 mile
to 2 miles. There are a total of 5 boxes in the series, though we
never found the Staircase LB. In 4 of the boxes are 4 clues to a 6th
box (thankfully there wasn't a clue in the one we couldn't find!),
where a treasure awaits, which we were thrilled to find! We were
exhausted, as this is serious hiking but it felt great! I recommend
this series, it was great, although I wouldn't have done it alone
with my daughter. Having another adult to help manuever the course
was extremely helpful! Beautiful scenery on every stretch! Check it
out!
