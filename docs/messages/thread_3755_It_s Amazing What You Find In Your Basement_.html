It's Amazing What You Find In Your Basement!
I decided to organize my letterboxing box equipment in the basement
that hasn't been used since we moved to Wallingford in July of 2006. I
found so many things. I have tons of stamps (Mickey Mouse, Looney
Tunes, Sponge Bob, El Diablo, etc.)that I have carved and never
planted. I found a HitchHiker that was given to me at the Trail Day
Gathering in 2005 that I will put in the Hostel in Meriden later this
week. And ... TWO POSTAL LETTERBOXES! I am so embarassed!!! :(

I found BuzzBee and Tiger's "Snail In The Mail" postal box and
Uneksia's "!@#$%" postal box. If those two letterboxers would email me
with instructions I will do whatever they want with these boxes. I
continue them on their way or send them back to the originator. Just
let me know.

I hope you all are doing well.
Safe Letterboxing!
-Letterboxer2002
(Alan)
