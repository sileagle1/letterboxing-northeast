The Ravine and Beaver Pond Trails - Beaver Pond Removed for Repair

From: nature77girl77 | Date: 2005-05-15 22:06:13 UTC
Today we went in search of The Ravine and Beaver Pond Trails
letterboxes in Pomfret, CT. The Ravine letterbox was fine, but the
Beaver Pond letterbox was completely buried in dirt and the inside of
the box was full of water. Fortunately, the logbook had been
protected by its ZipLoc, but the ZipLoc with the stamp and ink pad
wasn't closed. The stamp and ink pad are comletely soaked and
actually growing mold! (I've seen wet boxes before, but never this
bad!) We have brought the box home to (hopefully) repair it. As soon
as we dry it out and fix it up, we'll return it.

NatureGirl and Viking Brewer

Re: The Ravine and Beaver Pond Trails - Beaver Pond Removed for Repair

From: nature77girl77 | Date: 2005-05-17 22:36:27 UTC
We have replaced the Beaver Pond Trail box! After cleaning LOTS of
mud from the box, I found the reason for so much water inside -
something (a beaver?) had chewed on the box. We put everything in new
ZipLocs and a new Rubbermaid box and it is now ready and waiting for
letterboxers to find!
