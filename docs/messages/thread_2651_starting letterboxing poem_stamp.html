Create something personal whether it be a written dedication, art
that you created or a combination of both. Special doesn't have to be
profound...it is simply expression from your heart. This is sure to
inspire your cousin to get out there and find letterboxes in NYC and
beyond. Always remember, as in life, all the bits and pieces that
make up letterboxing are journeys.
