Gillette Castle Event Dec 2-3

From: swampyankeesunshine | Date: 2006-12-10 00:33:08 UTC
Just want to let everyone know that our weekend was extremely
successful. We had lots of interested families take advantage of the
stamps that were available to use for the day, which had been provided
by D.E.P. Lots of folks stopped by to learn a bit about letterboxing,
and we cheerfully gave them answers to their questions. This was the
9th weekend event as part of D.E P's Great park Pursuit/No Child Left
Inside. There were many other activities available to the families to
do and try, and all free of charge. The Pointer Party were there in
Mideval Armor giving a sword fighting demo. The Kids loved that,
too! Thanks to Sojourner for the loan of her Letterboxing 101
display. It was perfect. There was even an event stamp provided by
D.E. P. Great Day!!
Sunshine, Swamp Yankee, Hez, Grumpy, and Butterfly
