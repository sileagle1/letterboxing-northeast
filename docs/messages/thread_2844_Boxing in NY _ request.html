Boxing in NY & request

From: J. BRIDGMAN | Date: 2006-04-18 02:13:21 UTC
Had a great day boxing in Dutchess County today.
Found in good shape:
Norrie Point #1 (Couldn't find #2)
What Do You See #2 & #3 (#1 appeared missing)
No Onion
L and W, both Alphabet Bandits boxes
On the Way to the Old Schoolhouse
Fly Away Home HH

We would like to request that placers update the clues on LBNA when
boxes are retired or are known to be missing. We spent a lot of time
hunting for the White Rabbit in Red Hook only to find later (on AQ)
that it had been retired. Also had a great hike a couple of days ago
looking for Daydream Believer series in CT only to discover later
that all were reported missing on AQ. We usually print our clues
from LBNA since we like the way they come out. Don't always have
time to check AQ as well. Just some thoughts from a couple of
letterboxing addicts.

Bell Lady & Mr. B
