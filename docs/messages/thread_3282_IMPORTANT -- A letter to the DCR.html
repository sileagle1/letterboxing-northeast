IMPORTANT -- A letter to the DCR

From: cpascott | Date: 2006-10-17 19:36:55 UTC
Letterboxers -- please assist me in crafting a letter to the MA DCR to
determine what their official policy is regarding letterboxing. With
the disapperance of letterboxes at DCR properties and the singificant
increase in letterboxing in general, I think it time to be sure that we
are not on the wrong side of the law.

I'm thinking a letter should be sent that:
1. Explains what letterboxing is and why it's good for the DCR to allow
2. Inquires as to whether any current policies exist and what their
intentions are.

I've made no communications yet.

Please e-mail me and let's discuss further.

Thanks.

CPAScott
