From: cpascott | Date: 2004-08-30 19:28:02 UTC
Alright ... after a two week hiatus, Anjabanja and I finally got
back to 'boxing this weekend as we went after Family from Mass'
Weather series in Callahan State Park. As these are all new boxes,
they were all there, but at least one of the boxes (Sunshine) was in
a spot that was extremely difficult to cover. The box was fairly
exposed when we found it, and, unfortunately due to the nature of
the hiding spot, fairly exposed when we left it. It may not survive
long in its current location.

We also picked up Gray Reservation ... a nice little spot in
Sudbury, MA.

And, FINALLY ... after being the boy who cried wolf around here with
our placed boxes, we've managed to actually place two boxes and have
sent volunteer letterboxers out to test our clues. As soon as they
report back, we'll make the clues public. Next time I won't be so
anxious to announce the placement of a box!
