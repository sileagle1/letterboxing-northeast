Sadly , we're a distance away. But don't even hesitate to ask anything! The only dumb question is the one you don't ask, y'know. Heck - Diane and I found a hitchhiker in our first box and had no idea what it was or what to do with it. I hear that's not unusual. We went to your neck of the woods, and there's a number of nice boxes. It's Fall, so you can even just take a nice tour one day to take in the foliage - say up Route 7, or to upstate New York - and just do a few drive-bys on the way. If you have kids, it helps them to break up the trip with activity.
