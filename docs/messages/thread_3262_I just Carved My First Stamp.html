Hello Everybody out there in L'Boxing Land!

Well -
I did it!
I just carved my first stamp.
I am very excited it came out pretty good.
I carved it on a regular pink eraser 1" by 2 1/2 "
I bought at the dollar store.

Now I want to glue it on to another eraser.
What type of glue should I use???
Some stamp carving sites say rubber cement.
Others use wood glue but I think that is if you mount it on a wooden
base.
I don't want this to fall apart.

I can't believe this stamp came out good.
Much better than I expected.
Can't you tell I'm excited?

Ok, now I can be ready to plant my very first letterbox!

" Oh Joy!!!"

Blessings to all you wonderfull letterboxers out there.

~~~~~~~ Angel Friend ~~~~~~~
