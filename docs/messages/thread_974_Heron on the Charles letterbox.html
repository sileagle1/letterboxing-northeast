As I was updating my website to include a story about our hunt for
this letterbox, I recalled that the bridge listed in the last part of
the clues is under construction and not able to be crossed. This
does not impede one's ability to fetch the box, but an alternate
crossing will have to be sought out in order to return from the box's
hiding spot. Allot a little additional time if you're heading for
this box.

(Note: We hunted this box on June 30. My guess is that the bridge is
still out of service today).
