Mansfield Ct restores it's Letterboxing Mecca title (part 1)

From: woodtree236 | Date: 2005-10-11 20:27:19 UTC
Hello everyone

For the past 6 or so weeks a group of us aka Mansfield
Maintenance Crew having been checking up on Leader of the Packs
boxes . At last count she had over 75 boxes just in Mansfield . My
original intend was to just check the Mansfield boxes for LOTP as
she is having foot problems ............

Well I am happy to report that with the help of the Maintenance
Crew which includes one famous dog, we have almost completed our
mission. To date we have checked 114 of LOTP boxes in Connecticut .
We have changed baggies, removed full logbooks, replaced new
logbooks, added outer baggies , rewrapped in paper towels and
recarved broken or missing stamps.

We have discovered that 3 of LOTP's boxes were accidently deleted
from the LNBA site . Mark is working to get these 3 back on
line .Thank you .

WE have restored the 17 BOX ( yes 17 boxes ) Kettle Hole Series
and the new clues for the missing boxes should be updated soon .
Thank you JUDY in New Zealand ( she is the webmaster for LOTP"s web
page)

WE are currently working on restoring the WONDERFUL Gazebo
series . Music Woman has recarved the missing pieces and Flutterby
has volunteered to rehide them in less obvious places . Watch for
that announcement .

The group is a hard working group and as you can see we have
branched out of Mansfield and our goal is to report on all her boxes
in Connecticut. WE would also like to replace some of the older
boxes that went missing a few years back .

More to come

A HUGE THANK YOU to the Mansfield Maintenance Crew for restoring
Mansfield's Letterboxing Title and helping the "godmother " of
letterboxing .

Pine Tree
