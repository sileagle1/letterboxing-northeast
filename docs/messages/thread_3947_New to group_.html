Welcome to Maine boxing! Our 'event season' is pretty much over for
this year... and the first one listed for next year is Jiffy's Tour
of the Fort. Others will be Kites by HappyDaze and I'm co-hosting a
camping weekend event in August. The big event will be Frightnight
5 - it should be a huge event... can't wait to see what Koko pulls
out of his hat this time.

More locally to you are Ldyslpa, MEPANJ, and Follow the Sun... they

your boxing range I believe!

Have fun!
