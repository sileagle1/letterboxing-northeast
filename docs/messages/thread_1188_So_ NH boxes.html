Just thought I'd report on the few letterboxes I've found so far!
I'm just starting and having a great time, though my significant
others are not so intrigued.

Anyway...

Gilson Road Letterbox, Nashua - a quick almost drive by box; found
in fine condition

Rosemary's Shower, Sunapee - quick and fun while visiting the
Sunapee Craft Fair

1886 box, Nashua - an easy find, good condition

Mines Falls Park - a really nice walk and the box was fine

Max & Jack Cheese, Amherst - another great place for a walk, a fun
and informative box

Ponemah Bog, Amherst - Always wanted to visit this spot and
letterboxing brought me out there. Box intact and drier than we were
as it was a rainy day when we went and water dripping from the
shrubs and trees made my fleece weight about 20 lbs.

Susie Sells Seashells, Rye- found intact, nice view.

Hollyberry letterbox, Exeter- a bit of a challenge to be discrete.
Box there and intact.

Elizabeth Jane Garner Bouruereau, Exeter - Again intact, a nice walk
along the river.

That's it so far...
