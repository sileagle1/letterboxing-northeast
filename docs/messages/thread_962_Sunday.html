From: Nancy | Date: 2004-07-12 11:31:20 UTC
Dayville, Chaplin, Eastford and Mansfield all were visited Sunday by
Lenny of the Needeeps and me. We hiked, walked, and biked our way in a
huge circle. Only one box was found flooded, one we think missing and
all the other 20 odd ones in great shape. One H.H. Raido, wet and now
drying out in my sun room . With the help of a heads up from ours and
everyone's friend ;-) , we found two unpublished ones. Lenny was a
great host . We biked the dike for the Needeeps Florida State Quarter
stamp and his choice of placement for his quarters , as always is well
thought out. :-) Thanks Lenny and all the planters for a fun filled day.
Butterfly
