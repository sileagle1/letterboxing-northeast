Surprisingly enough, I was the first to stamp into this series of 5
boxes created by Teach and Preach's 2 talented letterboxing kids.

be drawn to seek out stamps in a series in honor of a favorite author
of mine! I have to admit the lefts and rights and references to

looking for the boxes too soon! After backtracking and second
guessing, all 5 boxes were stamped into.

waterproof boots with the rapidly flowing stream up to my ankles.
