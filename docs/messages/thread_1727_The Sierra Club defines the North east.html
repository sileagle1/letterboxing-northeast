Hi all,

I was flipping through my AMC magazine last night and came across an
article on old growth forests in the north east. The article was
written from a Sierra Club book called the Guide to Ancient Forests
in the Northeast. Now you all may be asking, what do ancient forests
have to do with letterboxing ... and the answer is really nothing.
What surprised me was the definition of the north eastern US that
both the AMC and Sierra Club used. In addition to the 6 New England
states, New York, Pennsylvania, and New Jersey were all considered in
the north east. This did surprise me.

I kind of missed all the voting and discussion that this group had on
what states should be included in LbNE. Heck, until this all came up,
I was so out of it I thought LbNE stood for Letterboxing New England!
(I really need to pay better attention.) Anyway, we should have just
asked the Sierra Club for the answer and saved time LOL.
