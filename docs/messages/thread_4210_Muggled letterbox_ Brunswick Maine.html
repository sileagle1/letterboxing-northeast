The nice young man from the Parks department returned the muggled letterbox to me today. He is a former geocacher and had some idea of what it was. Alas, the stamp is missing but the box is reusable and the tiny hitchhiker can be dried out and moved on. I'll contact the owner and see if she would like to recarve or just retire the box.

I'm impressed by his thoughtfulness.
