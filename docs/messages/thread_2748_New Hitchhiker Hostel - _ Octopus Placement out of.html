The "Dyslexic Queen" letterbox, a part of the Octopus placement has
been upgraded to a Hitchhiker Hostel. The rules do not match those of
the Christmas Spirit Hostel across the street. This is a large hostel
and can accomodate boxed hitchhikers. It has 1 HH now.

Plese follow these Hostel Rules to increase fun for everyone

1. There should always be at least one hitchhiker in residence.

2. If you take a hitchhiker, you should leave a hitchhiker.

3. Exception to rule one: If you arrive with no hitchhikers, and there
is more than one hitchhiker in the hostel; you may take one hitchhiker
without leaving one.


The Christmas Spirit Hostel was a refuge for hitchhikers over the
winter. It needed to retain its hitchhikers so that people who donated
one could reclaim one in exchange later, if they desired.

The new hostel's rules are meant to promote hitchhiker travel, and
thereby fun.

Hitchhiker Etiquette 101
1. The hitchhiker you leave should be stamped into the hostel logbook
when you leave it. The Hostel stamp should be in the hitchhiker log.

2. Record any hitchhikers you take or leave, in the hostel logbook.

3. If the hitchhiker you are taking was not stamped into the hostel
(for example I forgot my stamppad today), do a good deed and stamp it
into the log.
