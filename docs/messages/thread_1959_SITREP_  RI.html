Breaking Hearts at Breakheart

Warrior Woman & I found Breaking Hearts at Breakheart yesterday.
This box is over 6 years old and is said to be the first planted box
in Rhode Island (uncomfirmed.) It was fun to be a part of
letterboxing history. Reading some of the old entries in the log was
especially exciting. The box is in great shape.

We went all over Washington county yesterday and found:

A day in the life: Boxes 1, 2, 3, 4. (Could not find box 5.)
Live & Breathe: Box # 1. This box is also 6 years old & a bit moldy.
Lucky Charms: all 7 boxes in great shape.
Wendigo: looking good!
His Growth: also in fine shape.

Boxes that eluded us:
Ghoti (mystery)
Cave Dweller # 2

All & all a great day!
