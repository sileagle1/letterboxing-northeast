What a weekend went to see MomaFox and Bald Eagle on

Re: [LbNE] Great Letterbox weekend Hit 506 PFX

From: Sandy Leclerc | Date: 2004-07-17 02:23:56 UTC
I was two days behind you on Black Crow, Forgotten Road, The Dead Drive-In, The Ghost Train, and Lady Slipper. I wanted to thank you for leaving behind Honor Liberty Hitch hiker. I had not gotten that one yet, and was glad you had not taken it when you were on the Forgotten Road. I also did the two boxes of Briar Patch, Phantom of the Boardwalk, Kraken in Cape Cod Bay, Oyster-ville two boxes, and Winged Deaths Head. I could not find GB Heron's Haven or A Very Gorey Letterbox. They were both supposed to be on the Town of Yarmouth's walking trails. I believe I found where they were origanally, but they were no where to be found. I will be contacting the placers and ask them about the boxes. Hope you had as much fun as I did.
Queen B
