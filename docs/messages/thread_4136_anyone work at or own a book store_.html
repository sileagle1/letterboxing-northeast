someone that does? Preferably a book shop, but possibly a library? I

Re: [LbNE] anyone work at or own a book store?

From: NANCY SPERRAZZA | Date: 2010-08-02 00:57:05 UTC
Yes, they know about the one that is there ,  since the desk is manned by
different people , some of them might not know about letterboxing , and then you
have to tell them all about it !! they are always interested for sure , and it
can get to take a long time ~ but perhaps you could just use your own judgement
after finding the one that is there -- and plant yours .  If they are interested
and are boxers , they will see the clues once you put them on line anyway .! 
When you do come to town, be sure to hunt the other boxes in both the down town
and several of the out buildings at the Main Book  Barn.   You will love all
three places .

have fun
butterfly
