I haven't been to George's Island this late in the season, so I can't
say whether the area where the Lady in Black is would be boarded up
early ... but my initial hunch says no.

She is not in an obvious place ... until, of course, you find that
corridor (in other words, the corridor is obvious when you find it,
but it is not obvious to find -- you have to explore EVERY part of the
fort before you'll discover it).

E-mail me offline and let me know where in the fort you went. I'm
curious to know where you looked and where you may not have. When you
send me the e-mail, let me know if where, if any, parts of the fort
that were boarded -- that will let me know if the Lady in Black is
unaccessible.
