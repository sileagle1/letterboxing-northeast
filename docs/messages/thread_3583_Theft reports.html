Has anyone reported these thefts to the police? They usually want to
know of any crime that has been committed in their jurisdiction, even
minor ones. They are well versed in dealing with this type of
vandalism. Of course we have to make sure that permission to plant has
been obtained for those locations before we report them.

The planters or owners of the box would be able to make a report to the
town police in each town. If the town does not have a police
department, then to the State Police.
The Bird Stamper
