From: fleurchild@yahoo.com | Date: 2004-05-23 21:36:37 UTC
Went out to Gloucester/Rockport today to do some boxing...found:
~ The Bull Frog Pond in less than pristine condition. The top of the
container is gone and things were a bit soggy, so its sitting in my
car right now waiting for a new container.

~ Young's Great Rock/Wigwam Point Light is in great condition and the
location is lovely. Just be careful of the stickers around the rock!

~ Castle of Love looks to be gone as there is a "Drill Here" stick
where I think the box would probably be. Either way, the property is
being built on so its probably not there

~ Eastern Point Lighthouse...we got distracted by a large crab that
needed to go back into the water, snail shells, and sea glass. I
believe we were in the right spot, but probably not. Either way, it
was a nice spot to do some beach combing :)

Jocelyn
