Thank you Domedreamers for making the one night event letterbox "The Wizarding Hour". It turned out to also be a nice mini gathering. Boxers came from 3 states, CT, RI and NY. Great carving job, it was really impressive. Coming into the mall it wasn't too hard to figure out where all the letterboxers were. My first sight was seeing these people sitting crouched on the floor in a group. It just had to be letterboxers. It was a nice night. Glad we went and thanks again Domedreamers for making it possible......Chuck and Chickabirdie
