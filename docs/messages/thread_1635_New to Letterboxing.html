Welcome Mumma&BunnyBoy!

I have no great wisdom to impart about boxing with young ones (I
have boxed with 7-9 yr olds) that isn't the same sort of approach
you'd use for most any activity with them -- mostly be ready to
enjoy that kids change the pace -- sometimes slow things way down,
sometimes darting about like dragonflies.

Don't expect to cover the same distances with a little one as you
would alone -- seems obvious, but you'd be surprised. I am sure you
are already toting all the requisite kid supplies, but just review
them with an eye to what might be handy during your jaunts in the
woods .... bug spray, sunscreen, water, etc depending on weather and
terrain.

Many letterboxes are geared for adults, and even those aimed at
children are still a little much for a twenty-month old so make sure
there is plenty of time for joy and discovery and adventure for your
letterboxing companion who doesn't have the benefit yet of
unravelling a mystery along the way to a box.

Have fun!
