Milestone

From: birder579 | Date: 2005-07-17 00:02:42 UTC
Hi all
I am pleased to announce I have reached another milestone. I knew I
was comming close, and it was just a matter of time. Today I went to
Rocky Neck State Park to finish up the boxes there that I started last
year. When I got to Pineappletopia of the Fruit of the Dune series by
Laurie, I hit F 500!

I also found another box after that to start on my next 500. It is
unlucky to stop at a round number.

So I have kept my New Year's resolution to enlarge my F-ness. Now I
know Wanda is watching for me to enlarge my P-ness also. Don't worry,
I have a three box series in the works. It is in a place where there
are no boxes yet (amazing). The only thing I will say is it will be a
long hike with my first hand carved stamps.

The Bird Stamper
P3 F501 X139 E7 A(dopted)5
