Good story

From: Bell Lady | Date: 2008-02-20 21:48:10 UTC
We were looking for Benjamin Tarbox's Tree letterbox in Middletown's
West Burying Grounds yesterday and the box appeared to be missing. As
we were leaving, we were approached by a grounds-keeper who handed us
the box! Apparently his daughter had found it and gave it to him. He
knew what it was but hadn't gotten around to replacing it. When he
saw us hunting around the tree, he realized that was probably what we
were looking for so he brought it to us. The box in now back in its
hiding place. A nice flat rock might be a good addition to the hiding
cover material. We did the best we could with sticks. What a great
stamp! We're so glad we got it.
