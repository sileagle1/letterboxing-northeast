Flutterby's Birthday Boxes-Lebanon, CT

From: Flutterby | Date: 2008-01-05 20:18:49 UTC
~My husband, myself and the "girls" went out to check on these boxes as I have had numerous missing reports-especially on the last box of the series.

Imagine my surprise, and delight, to find all three in place and in great shape. Big thanks to the last finder of each box for doing such a good job re-hiding them-one I even thought was missing!!!

We did, however, pull the series as the trail is incredibly overgrown. They will find a new home as this was such a wonderful thing that my teenage daughter did for me.

Flutterby~

P.S. Thanks also for the wonderful comments in the log book.
