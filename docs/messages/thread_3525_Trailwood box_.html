Trailwood box?

From: birder579 | Date: 2007-06-12 18:32:36 UTC
Hi All
I was walking at the Trailwood sanctuary in Hampton, CT today, and I
found what looks like an old letterbox. It is in a screw top plastic
bottle. It only has only one entry in the log book, from 2001. It has
a stamp and a coloring marker. It is located next to a stream near the
Hired Hand's Monument.

Does anyone recognize the description or location? Does anyone know of
any abandonded boxes there? The two clues that are on
letterboxing.org, Trailwoods Back Door and One Honary Cowboy, are in
different parts of the preserve. It seems to me there were more boxes
at Trailwoods at one time.

If no one recognizes it, I will recover it. Maybe I will replant it
with new clues.
The Bird Stamper

Re: [LbNE] Trailwood box?

From: Pamela Smith | Date: 2007-06-12 18:53:23 UTC
I'm not going to jump into the flames on this one, but is it possible
that it might be a WOM or mystery box? Not every box is listed
'publicly' and, while it's the exception, some cared-for boxes may
not get visitors for a long time if the mystery is especially
difficult. Is there any indication as to who planted it? That's
probably the place to start and knowing if the LBer is active or long-
gone from the hobby would inform your decision on what to do.
pezpam
