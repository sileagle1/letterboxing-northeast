Clues for The Great Pumpkin Letterbox.
From the western most corner of the pavilion with
your back up against the pole take a reading with
your compass of 300 degrees .Walk 22 steps right foot
only to a big tree. From the big tree take a reading
of 260 degrees and walk 27 steps right foot only. Now
take 8 more steps and find an oak tree with orange
paint on it. Follow the path down and look for more
orange paint on the ground or rocks. Follow the path
around to the right to a stump with orange paint on
it. Go around the stump and follow the path to a
yellow gate. Look at the gate on one end there is a
washer welded to the end. There should be some lithic
landmarks
there. Look between them and find a piece an iron
moving machine . Look under it for The Great Pumpkin
Letterbox. Please note that The Great Pumpkin will
only be around on Oct 2,2004.

Remember Chuck and Molly know nothing about The Great
Pumpkin so dont ask them any infomation. No Molly can
not be bribed with doggie treats either.
