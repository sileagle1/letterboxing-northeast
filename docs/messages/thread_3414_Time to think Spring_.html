Time to think Spring!

From: slpa2005 | Date: 2007-02-11 21:25:56 UTC
I don't know about all of you but I'm ready to think Spring...April
specifically! The One for the History Books Event is coming along
fabulously! The letterboxing community has been so wonderful helping
me to prepare for this event. Boxers that I have never met have come
out of the woodwork (or is that the woods?) to carve, to offer insight
and to help with planning. Thanks a bunch to all of you!

I only have one "problem" now.....we need more boxers to attend! I
have listed some hotels/cabins in the Skowhegan area for lodging on the
AQ website. If you are interested in splitting a room, let me know, I
can contact some of the other people staying in the area that weekend
and see if we can set something up. I am very sure that anyone who
chooses to spend at least one night in the area will not be
disappointed by the number of boxes here to find. We'll keep you
plenty busy for a couple days!

And, on top of accomodations and loads of boxes....I'm guaranteeing
perfect boxing weather and lots of fun! It doesn't get any better than
that! Take a few minutes to check out the One for the History Books
Event listed on AQ. I promise you, you won't regret the trip!
