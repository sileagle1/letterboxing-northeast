ME: Andre the Seal

From: Phyto | Date: 2005-01-02 23:17:21 UTC
Phyto and FOOL visited the Farnsworth and Wyeth Center in Rockland, Maine last
week. On the way back up to Bangor, with a picturesque Maine harbor sunset, we
stopped to visit Andre the Seal. Though our spot was lightly covered with snow, we
still managed to find and stamp into this sweet little box!

What a nice place to view the old lime kilns and see Rockport Harbor out of season.
The afternoon sunlight made the granite rocks along the ocean water glow with a
strangely orange hue. Contrast this with a "Parrish Blue" sky, it was just gorgeous.

Classic Maine...
