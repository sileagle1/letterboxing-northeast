Nashua River Letterbox/Cache

From: oldhounder | Date: 2005-08-30 01:00:22 UTC
The Nashua River Letterbox by Team Spoonhead sits in the
letterbox-basement but is alive and well. It is a geocache/letterbox
hybrid and very popular with geocacjers. Siamese and I were the first
letterboxers there in a long, long time. The location is interesting,
particularly if you a Rails/Trails cycler.

LMS Kids is in a similar situation. It resides in the basement but is
well-maintained and a popular hybrid at the summit of Oak Hill in
Littleton, MA.

I have contacted Team Spoonhead and asked that they relist their boxes
on LbNA. Don't know if that will happen.

Oldhounder
