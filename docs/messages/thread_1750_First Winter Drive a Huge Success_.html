From: Mark Pepe | Date: 2005-01-17 02:52:32 UTC
Hi boxers:


We're all thawed out by now and would like to invite you to visit our
website for some commentary and pictures of the First Winter Boxing Drive.

Plans are currently underway for the second drive in this series to
take place towards the end of February.

Have fun and just get out there & box!!!
