Well it is midnight and Sadie and Russ just dropped me off and they have
more than a 45 min more drive. However we are all smiling. It was a
wonderful gather. Big thanks to Merenwen and Olwe, and such a great
store. ! I could get lost in there for days , just dreaming. Everyone
was so friendly and many of us met again out hunting boxes
I finally hunted down some Maine letterboxes and had such fun stumbling
in deep snow on one trail and treading on soft pine needles on yet
another. We stayed overnight so had all day Monday to hunt , and we
sure did !
Thanks again, gathers are way cool
