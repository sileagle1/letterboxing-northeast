Hi boxers:

How do we begin to thank the Maine crew for such a great gathering?
Let's start with our hosts - phyto and Isoceles, Nautilus and Culex:
the location was the best, the boxes plentiful! Letterboxers in
Maine may be few but ya sure know how to throw a party! Thanks for
the wonderful event and the beautiful boxes.

Deb Blanchard: Thanks for the Dartmoor presentation and the time you
spent with us after. Your tips and suggestions should help make our
trip a better one.

Amymisha: thanks for arranging the wonderful pre-event gathering meal

one common interest!

All of the Maine letterboxers who have planted boxes there: thank

innovative states in terms of new boxes. You may not have tons of
them like here in CT, but they all seem so well-carved, thought out
and packaged like the treasures that they are. Sue and I will be back
to get all of those we missed.

We also want to thank all of those letterboxers who took the time to
talk to us, exchange a story or two, look for hints on HOW. It was a
great event.

Thanks to all!


Have fun and just get out there & box!!!
