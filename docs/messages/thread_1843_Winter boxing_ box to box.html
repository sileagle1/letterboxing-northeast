Not sure if anyone will ge this... I'm sorry I didn't do it up sooner. I'll
bring some copies of it tomorrow. :)

From Bartlett Brook, we're going to Pioneers in Lebanon. Turn left onto
Goshen Hill Road, and right onto route 16 north. The Exeter Cemetery is near this
intersection (Pioneers in Lebanon).

After Pioneers in Lebanon, go east onto route 207. Keep an eye out on the
left for the "Hidden Cemetery" which is actually called Center Cemetery. This
is where we will find the Hidden Cemetery series and Baby D Turns 2.

Following this box, we'll go back on 207 West and turn left into the parking
lot ofr Lebanon High School. We'll cross the street on foot to go to Blue
Goose in the Town Pound. (Just trust me on this order.)

Following that, we'll get back into our cars and go out onto 207 west and
then turn right onto Mack Road, and pull into the soccer field parking lot. This
is the location for Lebanon Lions.

We'll get back onto Mack Road and keep going in the direction we were headed
in, and then turn right onto Barker Road. At the intersection wth 207, we'll
take a left and head east. When we see the sign for the French Soldier's
grave we'll park and do Baby D Turns 1.

From here, we'll continue on 207 east and turn right when we get to the
center of town. We'll go right onto West Town Street and enter the Lebanon Green
Market and get Tymfurkoffi.

From here, Flutterby can direct us to the best parking area for the boxes on
the Green (Lebanon Loop 1, Red Brick Meeting House, A Night on Lebanon Green),
since she's a local and knows where to park there if church is in session. :)

Following the boxes on the Green, we'll head down West Town Street and join
up with Trumbull Road. Turn right onto Goshen Hill Road. Pull into the last
driveway of the Fire/Safety Complex and park on the right side of the building.
This is where we'll find Local Heroes.

After this, we'll go back up Trumbull Road toward the center of town and make
a right onto Exeter Road. We'll pull into Lebanon Elementary on the right
and do My First Love.

Then we'll get back onto Exeter Road and go to the bottom of the hill and
park in front of the Trumbull Cemetery on the left. This is the parking spot for
both Lebanon Loop 2 and Pot O' Gold.

After these boxes we'll head in the same direction on Exeter Road and make a
left onto Kick Hill Road. At the stop sign, go straight at the intersection
of Babcock Hill Road. Take the next left onto Chappel Road, and a quick right
onto Bogg Lane (about 1.3 miles from the cemetery). Less than 1/10th of a mile
on Bogg Lane, pull over on the left in between a barbed wire fence and a large
tree. Pull out your Lebanon Loop clues for box #3!

Now Head back off Bogg Lane the way you came and take a right onto Chappel
Road. Follow it to the end. Take a right onto 289. Follow for 2.4 miles. After
you pass the small plaza on the left, take the next left onto Village Hill
Road. Go down a half mile on Village Hill Road. The park is on the right, and
it’s pretty obscure if you’re not looking for it. If you reach Krause Road on the
left, you’ve gone too far! Turn around and go back, the park is easier to
spot coming from this direction. This park is the location of Lebanon Loop #4
and Bear Tracks.

Following this, we'll keep headin gnorth on Village Hill Road. Turn left
onto Cook Hill Road. Scovell Cemetery will be on your right. This is where we'll
look for the Castle of Scovell.

Now we'll head to our final destination. Continue on Cook Hill Road until
the intersection with Route 87. Take 87 south (or is it east?), and on the left
will be Liberty Hill Cemetery. This is the location of Gray of Liberty Hill.

And I believe that will do it! I'm pretty optimistic that we'll be able to
hit most, if not all, of these. It's a short way from box to box, since
they're all in the same town, and they're all drive-bys.

Special thanks to Flutterby. :) And to Queen B, since I cut and pasted some
of her driving directions off her clues. :)

See you tomorrow at 9!
