I just found out that my loyal hiking companion and nephew, Diesel,

Oh Pam,

I'm very sorry to hear the bad news about Diesel. My condolences to
you and the rest of your family on his loss.

Jim Willis (google the name) has some prose/stories that I've found
cathartic. See also, the Rainbow Bridge.

I Loved You Best
Copyright Jim Willis 2001


So this is where we part, My Friend,
and you'll run on, around the bend,
gone from sight, but not from mind,
new pleasures there you'll surely find.

I will go on, I'll find the strength,
life measures quality, not its length.
One long embrace before you leave,
share one last look, before I grieve.

There are others, that much is true,
but they be they, and they aren't you.
And I, fair, impartial, or so I thought,
will remember well all you've taught.

Your place I'll hold, you will be missed,
the fur I stroked, the nose I kissed.
And as you journey to your final rest,
take with you this...I loved you best.

So sorry,
Connfederate
