Rustypuff, our friend Patti, and I hit Charlestown and South
Kingstown, RI, on the 11th. We found the following in place, intact,
and dry:

Great Gathering
Inn of the End
Little Red Schoolhouse
Nurse Log

Hike On! HH was found with Nurse Log.

We did not find the following:

Trustom Pond #1
Trustom Pond #2

That night we remembered that we'd had the LBNA 100 PLB since the
Moose Hill Mach 2 gathering. We've finally stamped into it and will be
sending it on its way soon.

I was on the Cape for my company's annual snoozefes... er, staff
retreat on the 13th and 14th and found the following boxes in place,
intact, and dry, unless otherwise noted:

Cape Cod Life Savers
The Triker (sign gone, but box is findable)
Fort Hill (log very full!)
Lady Slipper (stamp is broken in half)
Winged Deaths Head
The Empty Tomb.

I did not find the following:

Chatham Rooster 2
Tot Toter
Wind Power

At one of these boxes, the Kissy Kissy HH, carved by RTRW, was released.
