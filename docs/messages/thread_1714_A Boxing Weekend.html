A Boxing Weekend

From: buddyandpigtographer | Date: 2005-01-02 19:02:26 UTC
On New Year's Eve, Literate Lagomorphs, Pigtographer and I went out to
North Providence and Providence - we started at the RIC Letterbox,
moved to the Square In The Middle box, and then we headed over to the
temporary Holiday 2004 box in Providenece. I loved all the stamps!

Today Pigtographer and I went down to North Kingston - we started
looking for the "Faces of Wickford" - the four box series had no
indication of being pulled (other than box 4, which was drying out) -
so after trying to find the first two boxes, we assumed that the
series had been pulled.

We went off to find Afternoon on the Amazon, which was in good shape
for being there for 4 years! Beautiful views of the frozen lake from
the path.
