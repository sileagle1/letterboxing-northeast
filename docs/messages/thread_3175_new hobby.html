Welcome to the hobby. My family (me, my wife, and 3 year old son) have
benn letterboxing for nearly 3 years now, and we love it. Though we
live in MA, most of my extended family live in Suffolk County. When we
vivist them we try to get a few letterboxes. There are quite a few
letterboxes in Suffolk County. There are several in and around Ridge.
Do a search on AtlasQuest or letterboxing.org. If you get out to
Hampton Bays, we just planted one out in Sears Bellow County Park in
August. We also found several more in and around Southampton. Good
luck and have fun.
