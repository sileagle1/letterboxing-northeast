We (my son and I) have been on this treasure hunt for a little over
a year. He is the one who helps more than he knows by `thinking out
of the box'.
Alas, lately, he has lost a little of the magic of the hobby.
I was pleased to have him, at dinner tonight, ask if we were going
to continue the `Tradition' of Letterboxing on Christmas Eve day.
A year ago to that day we found our first Hitch Hiker. What a
pleasant and unexpected surprise! After contacting the placer that
evening to inform them of the status of the HH we received a
poignant reply.
You never know how you can affect another's lives with a simple
reply!

I am delighted that this HH has started a Christmas `Tradition' in
our family.

Thank you, and Merry Christmas!.. to Russ et al!

"He hasn't been forgot, Lord,
