My Dog Ate My Cootie

From: Janet | Date: 2006-11-21 23:47:14 UTC
I am sorry to report that my dog ate one of the cooties that I picked
up at the RI Mystery Gathering. I did not get a chance to stamp in so
I can't even tell you the name of the cootie or the creator.

It was a tiny pink notebook on a metal key ring. Someone put it in my
pocket at the dinner after the gathering. I took it out of my pocket
when I got home and put it on the kitchen table. That was the last I
saw of it in one piece.

If this was your cootie I am very sorry. There is nothing left to it
except the metal key ring and two shreds of the pink plastic cover.

Moonstone

Re: My Dog Ate My Cootie

From: Janet | Date: 2006-11-22 12:23:55 UTC
the verdict is still out on which dog ate the cootie. Neither dog
will talk. Personally I think they are covering for each other.

I was in another room busy on my computer and sort of heard the sound
of the cootie's destruction but I thought it was just one of the
dogs' toys. By the time the sound registered in my brain that it was
not a dog toy all that was left was the metal ring and two shredded
pieces of pink plastic. When I walked into the kitchen neither dog
was anywhere near the crime site.

Moonstone

PS the new dog's name is Gina. Gina does have a history of eating
just about anything so she is the primary suspect. Indy is not off
the hook because she has been known to eat items made of paper,
especially if they smell good.
