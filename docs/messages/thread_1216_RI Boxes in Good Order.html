RI Boxes in Good Order:

Team Milagro was out this weekend for a quick set of three boxes - all in good
shape:

Nockham Hill Turtle
Though we didn't see any turtles, there were many remnants of turtle egg
shells about. Very curious! The clues describe the box as located beneath the
bough of a particular branch, but we found the box actually at the base of the
tree against the trunk and replaced it where we found it. Enjoyed excellent
sweet corn from Four Town Farm later that night (the directions mentioned we
should visit, so we did!)

North South Trail - Buck Hill
(Along with team Sock Monkey)
A nicely maintained rocky trail. The box was in the location described, dry and
well hidden. We found a tiny toad which our three year old named "Toady".
We then explained to her why exactly we couldn't take Toady home to live
with us...

White Mill Park Letterbox
(Along with team Sock Monkey)
An easy find, nice location and plenty of nature to see along the way. Box was
dry and replaced securely.

Thanks for the folks who placed these boxes!

Cheers,

Jason (AKA: Bazooka Joe)
Team Milagro
