Mansfield Maintenance-A Success!

From: MayEve | Date: 2005-11-07 00:19:56 UTC
How heartwarming to spend the day with a group of fellow boxers (aptly
dubbed "The Mansfield Maintenance Crew") replanting some of Leader of
the Pack's boxes that needed a little TLC. Hats Off to Pinetree who
organized this undertaking, and to all those who logged miles checking
on the condition of boxes, many I'm sure they had already found!
Several of the stamps had to be re-carved, and some of the boxes that
had gone missing had to be replaced altogether. It shouldn't surprise
me that when there is a fellow boxer in need, there are boxers who
step right up there to help. THIS, my friends is the BONUS of
Letterboxing! It was great to see familiar faces, Sojourner, Vinny,
Molly, Music Woman, Paul Mapline, Chuck, Molly, Chickabirdie,
CatLover/Gardener/Teacher and of course PineTree and Leader of the
Pack. There were also some new friends to be made: Golden Girl and
her Mom, and CatLover's friends. Thanks to all that helped make the
day a success, and thanks for letting me be a part of it! MayEve
