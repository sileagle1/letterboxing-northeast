Lebanon letterboxing day

From: rospa10 | Date: 2004-06-06 01:56:21 UTC
Once again the weather reports were wrong and we had sunny skies. I
headed to Lebanon, quite a letterboxing mecca, if I do say so! One
unexplored by me. I found Lebanon Loop (#4 of the series). If not for
letterboxing (specifically, Leader of the Pack) who would have known
this peaceful park existed? Wasplanning to find the others in
series today but, ah well. I shall return!
"Ol' John Joseph's Railroad Yard Series" is highly recommended.
Pleasant hike-great bird watchng spot--clues are clear and stamps are
really well carved.
I then headed to the "Castle of Scovell" series. After stamping in
one stamp and trying to fit another, I realized a return visit with a
sheet of paper would have to be planned. The stamps are certainly
well worth the visit. Just be wary. Houses are near by, plus people
park near the cemetary to go fishing at the nearby river.
Just a short jaunt today, but most enjoyable. Then home to weed
the gardens and move some plants around.
