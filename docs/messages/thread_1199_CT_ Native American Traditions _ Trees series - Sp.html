CT: Native American Traditions & Trees series - Spruce Box missing

From: T Prindle | Date: 2004-08-27 12:34:53 UTC
Sorry to cross-post, I wasnt sure if everyone also reads the 'big
list'.

The third box in our Mansfield, CT "Native American Traditions &
Trees" series, the Spruce Box is confirmed missing. Many thanks to
the kind boxer that reported it MIA yesterday. I went down there
this morning and searched everywhere for any sign of it. Scratch
that one out of the clues if you've printed them out and were
planning on doing the series.

CT: my thanks to the boxing angels!

From: T Prindle | Date: 2004-08-28 01:10:37 UTC
I returned home tonight to a message on my answering machine from
Chickabirdie. Chuck, the boxing angel, upon hearing of the demise of
the box, went out to search for it, and *found my NA Trees and
Traditions "Spruce Box"!!! I heard it was dragged two stone walls
away from it's hiding place (the Connecticut raccoons are developing
some BIG muscles!!).

I am so thankful to Chuck (Molly may have played a part, we all know
how she can find letterboxes by their rubbermaid scent alone! *wink).

The series is restored, thank you boxing angles!
