The Challenge o' the Sprite

From: TurtleMcQ | Date: 2005-05-01 00:31:31 UTC
While enjoying a warm brewed beverage late one night in an idyllic small
town far - but not too far - from her usual domain, a certain Sprite
wondered aloud to a certain resident of said idyllic (gorge-ous, even)
small town how long a specific sort of foreign object secreted inside any
one of the multiple locations of a certain well-known regional purveyor of
foodstuffs and sundries might successfully avoid detection.

The local resident took the Sprite's query as a challenge to all who
frequent said establishment and has set in place the first three entries in
what should become a disheartening (at least to those of the janitorial
persuasion) plethora of attempts to claim the honor of surviving intact the
lengthiest period of time before being lost, retrieved, or (gasp!)
confiscated.

The rules are simple. Identify the establishment. It is said that Fortune
smiles upon it, at least this particular year. If you are one of the lucky
ones with access to this haven of practically anything that might be
desired by ravenous mortals, create your own response to the Sprite's
challenge by the Ides of May. Be discreet in what you choose to leave
behind, and where. Wait, and then wait some more. To the victor comes great
spoils.
