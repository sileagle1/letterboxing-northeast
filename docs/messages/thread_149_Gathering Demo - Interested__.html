Hi Folks,

My boxing as of late has included hand-sewn felt pouches for my
stamps. Not only do they protect the stamps from the elements, but it
has tied in nicely with my hand-made logbook covers for a complete theme.

Is anyone interested in a quick demo on how to make an easy felt cover
for your stamps? I will provide some amount of felt and supplies but
I'd like to put this idea out there if anyone is interested to get an
idea.

On average it takes less than 10 minutes to sew the edges with a
simple stich and the supplies are very simple:

1) Piece of felt cut to size for stamp
2) needle and thread
3) button or string
and scissors

If anyone thinks they might be interested I'd be willing to do a 30
minute demonstration on how to do this. If not, I'll still bring a few
samples of ones that I have made recently and you may certainly ask me
personally. Please respond to phyto underscore me at yahoo dot com.
