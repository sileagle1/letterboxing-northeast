Mansfield Maintenance Day

From: woodtree236 | Date: 2005-09-26 20:59:41 UTC
Update : The original intent of Mansfield Maintenance Day was to
help out "Leader of the Pack". She is having( and has had for some
time) foot problems that have not allowed her to check on her MANY
MANY boxes in the Mansfield area. I contacted her and we decided to
have a day devoted to repairing her boxes . This date is/was
November 5th or 6th. L of P is a VERY busy lady and we did not set a
date .
........................however some people have read my story on
mark's website about the MM day. Over 10 people wrote or called me
to say that they would like to help out in any way.

Here's what has happened so far:
27 boxes have been checked ( new outer bags, new inner bags, new
journals when needed , and new paper towels

3 boxes have been discovered that were accidently deleted from the
main list ( look for these clues to reappear on tne main site in
October)

2 boxes that were reported missing were found !!!!!!!!

4 stamps have been recarved ( thank you Chuck and Music Woman)

3 stamps are in the process of being recarved.

2 sets of clues are rewritten with differnet clues ( look for
updates in Oct)

WE still have over 30 boxes left to check. If anyone is planning on
coming over to Mansfield Connecticut in the next month and would
like to help out ............Please drop me a line .

Thank you to Chuck and Molly, Music Woman, Mark Pepe ( will be
updating clues after teh Caper Gathering )Butterfly, Sweet Pea,
Wolfy, Painterly, Flutterby, CAtLover/Gardener, Maplines , Queen B ,
and Chrissy/Brandy , Wanda and Pete ( for hanging on to old clues )

Hats off to a great bunch of boxers !!!!!!!!!Hope I did not miss
anyone .

Pine Tree
