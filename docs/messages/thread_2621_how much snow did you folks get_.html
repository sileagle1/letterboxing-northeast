how much snow did you folks get?

From: Michelle Lee | Date: 2005-12-11 03:37:49 UTC
Greetings,

We got about a foot of snow out here on Friday. The only way to
letterbox around here is with snow shoes or cross country skis.
A new letterboxer and I where able to easily find Castle Rock and
Of Grass and Frogs letteboxes with about 2 inches on the
ground. But now there is just TOO MUCH SNOW around here.

The trip was a success - I got her hooked on letterboxing. She
even got an account on letterboxing.org and started to look up
letterboxes fot find (yahoo!)

Did any only get a few inches of snow (maybe the down in the
Cape)? How about Andover- did you folks get hit with a foot of
snow? I have heard reports that the Burlington area got over a
foot too! Are we left to only letterbox indoors until there is a thaw?

Or are there people who have planted boxes above the snow line
with well marked trails?

Choi reported last week that you could find Icarus in the snow. Is
that still true ?

Regards,

oldpatterns

Hi Archimedes,

It was great meeting you yesterday. After I left, I headed to my childhood beach (First
Encounter) in Eastham. I had a noticed a tree down here and there as I drove. Around

Take care, Orion
