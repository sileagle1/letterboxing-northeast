From: team_new_hampshire | Date: 2007-07-24 21:22:02 UTC
please do let me take this moment and let everyone know how
incredibly sorry i am that your boxes have gone missing over the past
week or two. I have lost 1 box in the white mountains to a park
ranger and i was very upset over it. i couldn't imagine what it would
be like to lose a handful.

now, it was brought to my attention that i might be thought of as the
possible culprit for these boxes missing. I am reaponsible for "the
alien invasion" that will be showing up at the redding bbq next
month. "the alien invasion" is nothing more than team new hampshire
having a little fun at a gathering. this little game i have in no way
involves "abducting" boxes. that is just not exceptable in my eyes. i
would not like it one bit if someone was to do it to any one of my
boxes.

i know that this has been going around as a possible excuse for boxes
dissappearing, but please do not point the finger my way. i hope that
at some point, the person or persons involved get caught and charged
if possible.

with that said, if there are any questions as to this "alien
invasion" you can continue to ask whom ever you have been asking, ask
me, or ask anyone who was involved in last years "alien invasion" in
pennsylvania. there were plenty of people there that can tell any and
everyone what kind of game this is.

david (team new hampshire)(alien invasion)
http://teamnewhampshire.blogspot.com
