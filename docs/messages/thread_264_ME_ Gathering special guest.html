From: Phyto | Date: 2004-02-23 02:11:00 UTC
Howdy Folks!

Those that are planning to come to the Maine gathering in March are in
for a special treat. I just confirmed that we will be having a special
guest for the event. Deb Blanchard is an educator from Maine who has
developed the Lakes Region Letterboxing Trail in Raymond.

Deb has also been letterboxing for 10 years and has made several trips
to Dartmoor. She will be sharing her slide show of photos from her
Dartmoor trip for those at the gathering. Deb will also be bringing
stamps and other memorabilia as well! It might be especially handy to
speak with her in preparation for those who are planning to travel to
Dartmoor this year with Mark or perhaps in the future.

I'm very happy that Deb will be there and I hope this will encourage
those of you who weren't sure if you should make the drive to come!

Hope to see you on March 21st. Nautilus and I will be posting more
information about this gathering in March after the excitement of the
upcoming POE gathering subsides!

-Phyto
