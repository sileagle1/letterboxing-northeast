From: alwayschaos | Date: 2007-09-20 09:57:59 UTC
Be very careful this time of year in regards to bears. Right now the
foraging is at high speed to pack on the pounds and with apples ripe,
they seem to be everywhere.

The cubs are getting out and starting to wander a bit further from
mom than before, so it's easy to get between a mom and her cub(s)
without even realizing there are cubs in the area.

It's sad and unfortunate that a mom and one of her cubs were
dispatched recently by someone I know because the mom kept bringing
them to the house to forage the apple trees not 15 feet from the back
door. She wasn't shy and started coming around during daylight
hours. After having to carry a 357 magnum each time they went out of
the house (and using same gun to shoot over their heads trying,
unsuccessfully, to scare them away) this person ended up calling
another hunter to take care of the issue.
