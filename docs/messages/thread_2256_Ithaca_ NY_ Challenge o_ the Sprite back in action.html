Ithaca, NY: Challenge o' the Sprite back in action!

From: TurtleMcQ | Date: 2005-07-28 12:42:48 UTC
Contrary to published reports, Challenge o' the Sprite boxes #1 and 3 are
not missing at all. They are, in fact, in great shape and just where they
should be. Box #2 is still missing, and due to the sensitive nature of the
its location, it will not be replaced.

If you choose to take on this Challenge, please remember to conserve space
in the very small logbooks by using only a portion of your signature stamp
if it's a large one. Future finders will thank you for leaving a little
room for them.
