Greek Gathering! A wonderful experience!

From: mainekokopellian | Date: 2006-06-25 21:41:00 UTC
I arrived home early this AM from a most wonderful adventure full of
quests.

First and foremost I must applaud Sapphireberry who poured MASSIVE
amount of time, energy , carvings and yes SWEAT into creating this
wonderful experience.. for a large number of folks who braved "bad
weather forecasts" and attended this event this was a special
one!....Most of the approximately 40 stamps came from her! Yeah some
of us contributed , but she exceded our efforts by
alot.......Bravo , seriously for your work.. You have shared an
experience that was truly unique.... I look forward to seeing all
the pictures that should come out of this.....


Cooties, Art Goddess and I drove from Maine and though it was a
rainy drive down, the Olympians were clearly smiling down on us...
The weather was fine all day! Not bright sunshine but clearly good
enough to box! So clearly those who let weather forecasts decided
for them .. you all missed a great event!

From the moment my companions and I arrived , you could honestly
feel the excitement and energy from those emerging from their rain
soaked vehicles and preparing for an amazing day of "quests"......

To everyone that did attend should attest to , these were some fun
extensive hikes for boxes...Several time I hear people remark at how
much effort the organizers must have made to hide these boxes...
The park itself is an amazing location for visiting and yeah doing
some boxes..... I was fortunate on a couple of quests to work with
Lazy Letterboxer and Letterboxing Ham and learn more about this park.
I loved being able to hear share stories and watch 2 people I
respect solve some clues:) Thanks guys!


These gatherings are always wonderful to share time with all of you
that enjoy this hobby. I met many new boxers and reconnected with
several old friends :) More and more these events connect us all
within the hobby.

A special thanks to those who shared some "fireside chat" and those
crazy night boxers who helped each other complete our 12 herculean
tasks at night :)Those night pictures should be umm interesting if
they make it online :)

Again BRAVO BRAVO! to the Greek Gathering organizers and all the
letterboxers who attended and made this a wonderful experience for me
and my companions.
Mainekokopellian
Sean
