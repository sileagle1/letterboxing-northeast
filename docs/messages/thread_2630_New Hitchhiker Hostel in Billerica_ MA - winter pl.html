New Hitchhiker Hostel in Billerica, MA - winter placements

From: morgunjp | Date: 2005-12-17 22:52:37 UTC
A new Hitchhiker Hostel, "Christmas Spirit" is available in North
Billerica, Massachusetts. It consists of 3 boxes, two of which are
far easier than my usual offerings. The third box, Glacial Action
provides a longer hike and some educational material. It is a micro-
box, a 35mm film canister. Opening was delayed until "Shotgun" deer
hunting season ended.

All three boxes are winter placements, and are immune to snow. The
main box is only partially sheltered from snowfall, but since its
volume is 14.5 liters, (equals seven 2-liter soda bottles) it is
still easy to find. The two satellite boxes are completely sheltered
from snow. All three are in plain sight, once you look from the
correct spot.

Many thanks to the people who helped me check the clues and
created/provided hitchhikers for the hostels. One of the outlying
boxes was converted to a hostel at the last minute to reduce
confusion while stamping into the 5 hitchhikers that remain in
the "Christmas Spirit" box. Many Thanks to Choi, DDD, and Wildcats.
Since they were generally ineligible for "First Finder," two First
Finder opportunities still exist.

If you have avoided letterboxing in the snow until now, and you live
in the Billerica area I hope that you will stop in to visit these
boxes sometime. The current plan is to remove all hitchhikers after
winter ends, for the following reasons. At some times of year the
last 25 feet to the main hostel is infested with ticks. In good
weather, the hitchhikers can return to active circulation. Some
hitchhiker owners might also want their hitchhikers in more remote
locations, during fair weather.

Enjoy,
Tiny Turtle & Morgun (with lots of help)
