From: Ellen Dill | Date: 2008-09-11 00:44:21 UTC
If you have time to solve some of Chunna's mysteries, they're always great boxes.  I agree about Alice in Orleans, so if I were doing that, I'd go across the street to Chunna's "A Pirate's Hunt"  -- both awesome series!  If you're staying closer to Yarmouth, Chunna has a series called "The Postman Cometh" which are really great stamps . . . and they are near our series "Never Forget"  -- always a good carve by T2, and okay by me -- also down the street from two of our favorite Archimedes' Screw drive-by's, including "Dump Picker" and -- oh heck-- anything by Archimedes Screw is incredible, let's face it!  We love those Cape boxes!
