From: RIFamily | Date: 2007-11-04 23:14:55 UTC
We were at a drive by last weekend, in the rain, in Rhode Island. When I
came back to the car with the box and looked at the log book, I said,

"Oh, guess who the first finders are, of course...."

I assumed my husband would answer... but no.....

from the back seat, my five year old son, who apparently pays WAY more
attention to things than we give him credit for!, said

"Wanda and Pete!"

We couldn't believe it!! I could have died laughing.

So I immediately "reenacted" the moment on video.
