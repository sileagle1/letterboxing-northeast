Pennsylvania Hex Series (Andover, Essex County, MA)

From: fleurchild | Date: 2006-07-12 18:21:29 UTC
I'm going to pull these boxes on July 29th, the first log book is full
and I think they are going to wing their way off to their carver in PA.

If you do go get these boxes, you might want to bring an dated index
card with your stamp on it to leave in the first box. Definately bring
bug spray and the clues to these and other boxes. You can easily make a
day of boxing in Andover :)

Opa
