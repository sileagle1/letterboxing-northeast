I had a wonderful day on Saturday finding 28 boxes in Chaplin. I
started off finding all ten of the Fairy Tale series, and the Fairy
of the Falls. I then found WWI Silent Night and the CT State Stamp
series-Windham County, which was my first find in that series. I then
traveled to get South Carolina and had Christmas Around the World.
And speaking of Christmas, I found The Best Christmas Pageant Ever
with the U.H.O. hitch hiker. I then went to Diana's pool and got Town
Crier with some Maple Falling Leaves and Molly's Day series. I ended
my day finding Knight & Day. What a great way to spend part of a
holiday weekend! Hope yours was as successful as mine.
