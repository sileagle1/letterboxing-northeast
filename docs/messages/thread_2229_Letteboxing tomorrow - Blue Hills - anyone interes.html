Letteboxing tomorrow - Blue Hills - anyone interested?

From: cpascott | Date: 2005-07-15 15:11:44 UTC
Hey all !

OK, so isn't it ironic that two-and-a-half-months after I move away
from the Blue Hills, I'm finally going do some letterboxing there?!

Anyway, I always prefer to letterbox with others, so here's an open
invititaion. My goal is to collect as many of the letterboxes in the
Blue Hills as possible, and I'd love a companion or two.

So, if you're interested in 'boxing with me, or even if you have all
the boxes but are willing to hike anyway, let me know ASAP.

The published boxes include:
* ABIF's My Love
* Arcane Mandala's Kells Dog
* BOB's Phantom
* Cavy Lovers' Blue Hills Birthday
* Dragonfly's Firelight
* Peppermint Patti's Blue Hills Weather and World Champs Reverse the
Curse (2nd box may not be there per the clues)
* Team Green Dragon's Time Travels Series (6 boxes, I have 1 of them
already)
* Winduphiker's Forest Hoops and Rainy Day

That's 14 boxes in the Blue Hills alone plus any mystery boxes that I
discover before I head out. I also have clues to a couple of boxes in
nearby Quincy and Braintree.

I'd rather not box in solitude, so join me!

E-mail me off list if you're up for it!

Thanks!


CPAScott

From: cpascott | Date: 2005-07-18 17:50:16 UTC
LOL. No, that's not really that ambitious! Honest!

Actually, I was successful in picking up all of the Time Travels
series plus pretty much every other box in the 1000 section of the
Blue Hills yesterday except for a couple I could not find. I took me
about 5 hours of hiking.

Found were:
Cavy Lovers' Blue Hills Birthday
Winduphiker's Forest Hoops and Rainy Day
TGD's Time Travel Series - Fiendish Things, The Traveler's Return, and
Morlock Holes
Arcane Mandala's Kells Dog
ABIFF's My Love
Dragonfly's Firelight

In addition, I was the first finder of Peppermint Patti's Blue Hills
Weather.

I was not able to locate one TGD box I hunted and intentionally
ignored a second 'cause I wasn't 100% that I had the right date
necessary. The sixth box in the series I already had. I'll be back
for the two I still need as well as the couple of boxes in the other
sections of the Blue Hills.

Speaking of which, does anyone know if the box in Fowl Meadow still
exists? It's no longer on the databse, but I have the printed clues.

CPAScott
