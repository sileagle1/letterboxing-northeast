Wonderful hike in Ashford, CT

From: rospa10 | Date: 2005-04-10 20:32:54 UTC
My husband and I hiked in the Yale Forest yesterday--was so perfect-no
bugs out yet, lots of sunshine and all those incredible laurals. I
plan to get back mid-June as Wanda mentioned they are in full bloom
then.
We climbed to the overlook and had lunch ( F.Y.I., NeeDeep's "Once
Was Mine" l.b. is there-I already had found it preciously). We then
continued on to Chuck and Molly's Boston Hollow l.b. and sat by a
crystal clear stream to stamp in and read the logbook. There were lots
of unafmiliar stampers and not that many familiar stampers-inners. As
noted in the clue sheet this hike (1.5 miles each way) is not
recommended for kiddos. However, it does make for a really nice
afternoon especially if you stop at the overlook for a while.
Other very nearby boxes are: Rain/Sleet/Snow, The Fall of the Pixies
and Nipmuck Natchaug.
