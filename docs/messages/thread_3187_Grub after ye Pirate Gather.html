For those Pirates who would like to catch some grub after ye gather, I suggest we get together between 4 and 5 p.m. at Paul's Pasta in Groton! A special treasure letterbox will be on hand for ye dinner.

To get there from t' gather, head t'wards the equator on Route 1. Turn t'wards yer port side onto Poquonnock Road STAYING on Poquonnock (it may turn to t' starboard at one point) until it turns into Thames Street. When ye get t' Thames, keeping the river to yer port side, Paul's Pasta will also be on yer port side just past t' Army Navy Store.

We need to get there before 5 p.m. since they become VERY busy at 5 p.m.!

Captain's Mate MW
