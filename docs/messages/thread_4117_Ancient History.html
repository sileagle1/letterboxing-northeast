Hi all! We've been out busily doing our spring maintenance and have an old logbook sitting in the sun drying out. It's from a defunct box planted 2/15/00 that we called Charland's Neck after our mountain biking friend John Charland who actually broke his neck at the spot where the box was planted. (He's fine now thank goodness). We thought you might enjoy hearing the names of the boxers who found it way back when...

Dan & Melissa (curious george stamp)
Chris & Carri (acorn and squirrel stamps)
Sarah (frog stamp)
Jeremy Disch (scientist in the wild)
Bob (Disch), Judy, Abbie, & Melissa
kancamangusdabull and Chef Stan
Shaileen & John and Tucker too
Betsy & Tim (Saturn and a Submarine Warfare pin stamp)
Eagle & Merlin stamps with no names but the note "Ha! Mrs. Ryan tricked Jonathon to acting like an idiot on the bridge rock. P0F3X1"
The Orient Express, Braintree VT
Kristin & Shawn (kissing stick figures stamp)
Sun stamp, no name, "So this is what an erratic is? I'm feeling a little erratic myself!"
Cheshire Cat stamp (we know who this is but for the life of us can't recall the name...)
The Elements of Hiking: Annie B, Evelyn, & Emma "but no Debbie"
The Ram (part of a group from an early era Gathering)
Wanda & Pete (Gathering too)
Tate & Sue "chasing after Wanda & Pete"
Steph & John (also of the Gathering)
Ben, Christofur, and Fern (from RIBNAG, remember what that stood for?)
The LunaTic
Pup (Ginko stamp)
Carrie, Chris & Nate (lizard stamp)
T&J (potted plant stamp)
Sarah (giraffe stamp)
Phillis (mom), Mae, Rick (papa) and Raillie
WaldenRun (bearprints and footsteps stamps)
Unsigned "I got your space ship to 2:31am, this is fun in the dark!"
A month later the same person with "no one's been here but me"

And that's the last. The series got what we call "retired in situ" with the clues pulled but the boxes waiting a few years for us to finally remove them.

A fun trip down memory lane, Jay & Margaret in CT
