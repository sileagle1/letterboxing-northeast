MA Moose Hill Gathering Mach 2

From: kimcalcagno | Date: 2004-04-21 14:14:31 UTC
Come one! Come all! to the Moose Hill Wildlife Sanctuary in Sharon,
MA on
Sunday, June 27 from 1 p.m. - 5 p.m. for the second annual Moose Hill
Gathering!

We are hoping to see some familiar faces and perhaps welcome some new
folks
(as the nature center has put this in their program bulletin). This
time we
hope for a lovely day and to be able to use the picnic tables
outdoors. Last year
we fit 80 of us into the nature center on that bitterly cold January
day
(shiver!).

The potluck nature of these events always leaves us with tons of
food, so
CSCM and I will provide cold drinks and leave the rest to fate. I
will carve an
event stamp, but others are welcome to carve too. There are several
boxes on
the sanctuary, but I'm sure there will be some event-only boxes as
well. Let us
know if you are able to come by RSVP'ing.

See you there!!
-Kim (rustypuff)
