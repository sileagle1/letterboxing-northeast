RI: 8 boxes found, 1 rescued

From: Christopher Martin | Date: 2004-08-15 18:48:17 UTC
Rustypuff and I found the following boxes yesterday before and after a visit to the
Washington County Fair:

The Hills Are Alive: Do, a Deer, a Female Deer
An unpublished box (that we missed last weekend)
The Minimalist Series
I Am Waiting and Watching for You

Aside from the exception noted below, all boxes were in place, dry, and intact.

The Minimalist Series had not been found in over a year, and while boxes 2 thru 5
were in good order (aside from a very wet ink pad in #5). box #1 was soaked and the
log was moldy. For some reason, the previous visitors had turned the ziplock inside
out and left it unsealed! We brought all of the contents with us, leaving a note in the
box for future finders. The log is drying out on our kitchen counter and we will
contact the placer to see what she would like us to do with it.

CSCM
