Where did we go?

Traveling from RI, we got there...a bit late (when everyone was
leaving!) and headed off with Alan and his crew. After the River Run
LB and the Northern Raven, we lost not only Alan and his class but
Perl and EagleScout2002 - so we backtracked, had some lunch and
started up the tower trail (we were a bit wary of the possibility of
us going to LB on the giant...with no map)...so we found Rubaduc,
Butterfly, and SwampYankeeSunshine - as well as The Engraver,
Corrina, Mr. Peanut and Patriotic Girl, all four of whom we met at
the POE gathering.

We had a great day (and a good hike to boot!)...it was a bit wet
(shoulda brought our galoshes!!)...but the view was INCREDIBLE from
atop the tower, the highest point in the park (at least I think it is
from consulting the map near the parking area)...thanks to Alan for
attracting quite a few letterboxers - we're kinda disappointed we
wern't able to get there earlier and maybe score some more exchanges
and the event stamps by Alan and Northstar - may we meet again!

We might be turning something we found on the tower trail into
something to do with letterboxing - but what...? We don't know yet -
all I know is that one man's trash is another man's treasure.
