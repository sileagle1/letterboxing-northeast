Thanks to Maine Kokopellian and his crew for a great Fright Night - man what a lot of work
you put into it! It was great to see so many local boxers!

On a sad note Sam I am has misplaced the log book she brought to the event - a 8 by 10"
scrapbook that she won at the Greek event at Wendell. It won't have her name in it, and only
has the stamps from the event in it. She spent the afternoon with Siamese collecting all the

stamps. One of them was found by the Wildcats - that was her hand carved music note. The
other part is her stamp that has the words Sam I am. I had that stamp made when she was a
baby (long before we where letterboxers). That is a commerial stamp with a wooden handle.

So.. please look in your trail bags and see if you have the lost stamp or logbook!

Thanks!
