Greetings. Thanks so much for spreading the word about this great event, including a gathering for Letterboxers!


The Treasures of Yarmouth Scavenger Hunt  is perfect
for singles, friends, couples, the whole family! Letter Boxers, too! This event
is not a race; it offers plenty of time for participants to explore and enjoy
the town at their own pace. Hunters will start by picking up a booklet and map
at the Cultural Center of Cape Cod between 10am and noon and will then explore
Yarmouth’s cultural, natural, and commercial gems before concluding at the
Cultural Center no sooner than 4:30pm for refreshments, prizes, entertainment,
and more. Locations on the Hunt will include: Packet Landing on the Bass River,
Judah Baker Windmill, Bass River (aka Smuggler’s) Beach, Parker’s River Beach,
Bayberry Hills Golf Course, Gray’s Beach/Bass Hole, Taylor-Bray Farm, Flax Pond
Recreation Area, The Cobbler Shop and Kelley Chapel, The Weeping Beech Tree, The
Bangs Hallet House, The New Church, The Edward Gorey House, ZooQuarium, Bass
River Sports World, and The Cultural Center of Cape Cod. At each location,
hunters will get their booklets stamped. Those participating at the Advanced
Level will be asked to answer questions at each stop. Every properly completed
booklet will become an eligible entry for valuable prizes, including a grand
prize for the winner at the Advanced Level. prizes will include a weekend stay
at a Red Jacket Resort, meals at fine Yarmouth establishments, and works of art.
Funded in part by a grant from the Town of Yarmouth Tourism Revenue Preservation Fund. For
tickets, visit the Center or call 508-394-7100. For more information, visit
www.cultural-center.org or email Lauren Wolk at lwolkculturalccc@...
