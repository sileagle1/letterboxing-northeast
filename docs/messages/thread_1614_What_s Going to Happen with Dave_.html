Well, heck. It's time I let folks know - I won't be getting in much letterboxing, soon. Army Reservists run a certain risk, and I got my marching orders. Nothing very dramatic - I'm an instructor, so I'll be going around the East Coast for a year (or so) training battalion, brigade and division headquarters units in lovely locations like Fort Dix, NJ, Fort Drum, NY and Fort Bragg, NC.
