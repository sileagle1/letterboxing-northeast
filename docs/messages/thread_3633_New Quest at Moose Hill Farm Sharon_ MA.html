New Quest at Moose Hill Farm Sharon, MA

From: cavy_lovers4 | Date: 2007-09-17 21:25:42 UTC
For those of you who are not on the Trustees of the Reservation mailing
list...
The Trustees opened a new property this month, Moose Hill Farm in
Sharon. The trail map is on their website. At the kiosk in the parking
lot are directions for a quest - though the number of copies was low
this weekend. So far, the quest directions don't appear on the website.
http://www.thetrustees.org/

Anyway, it is a pretty property and the stamp is hand made.

The Trustees are opening a couple of other properties later this Fall.
One called Signal Hill in Canton (next town to Sharon). I don't know if
they'll have quests there or not, but at minimum there will be new
trails to place boxes on!

Cavy Lovers
