It was so nice to be out in the fresh winter air even if I did forget
my gloves. Today I visited Lebanon again and was able to complete
the hidden Cemetary series which eluded me due to impending dark back
in December. Kiakahi"s carvings run the gambit from whimsical (like
Hidden Soul to serious like Hidden Cross. Thanks to added hints from
a fellow letterboxer, I was able to return to Exeter Cemetary
for "Pioneers". for "Pioneers in Lebanon". At Bartlett Brook I was
able to find Needeep's Missouri stamp, then on to the Lebanon fire
Dept. Hoowee..that was a very public place! However, I managed to
successfully stamp into "Local Heroes". Thanks you wonderful l.b.
carvers and placers!
