I'll admit that I haven't lived in Riverside in years (since the early 80's) but I had no idea that it
had turned into a party place. It never was through the 50's, 60's, and up to the late 70's.
That is such a shame.

I'll never forget the day that I hunted "Louff Carousel" and "Morty" (deep winter). It brought
back so many memories of shore dinners and the rides at Crescent Park, and I have family
buried in Little Neck as well that I got to visit that day.
