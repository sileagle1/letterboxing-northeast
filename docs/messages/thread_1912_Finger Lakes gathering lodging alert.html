Finger Lakes gathering lodging alert

From: TurtleMcQ | Date: 2005-03-31 16:12:32 UTC
(So sorry for the cross-post, but I've heard from people on all three of
these lists who expect to attend and I don't want any of you to miss this
info.)

For those of you thinking about coming to the Finger Lakes gathering in
central NY on June 11th, please be warned that lodging in the Ithaca area
is quickly becoming scarce due to Cornell's Reunion weekend.

If you don't already have reservations and can't find anything in Ithaca,
you might try checking the Cortland area as well. Local state parks with
camping include Taughannock Falls, Buttermilk Falls and Treman. I know at
least one family has reserved a cabin on Cayuga Lake - vacation rentals may
be a little easier to find because local schools will still be in session.
There's a new Hilton Garden Inn under construction in downtown Ithaca that
should be open next month, too. I don't know if they're taking reservations
or not yet, though.

You can find all sorts of information about local lodging, camping, places
to see and things to do here:

http://www.visitithaca.com/

http://www.cornell.edu/visiting/ithaca/visiting.cfm

Feel free to contact me off-list if you have any questions or need more
lodging info.

Looking forward to seeing many of you in June!

TurtleMcQ
