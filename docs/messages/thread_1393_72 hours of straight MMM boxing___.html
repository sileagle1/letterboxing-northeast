72 hours of straight MMM boxing!!!

From: T Prindle | Date: 2004-10-04 02:15:13 UTC
... with a little sleep time. The Dawnlanders had the MOST EXCELLENT
long weekend searching the MMM boxes with friends and family and met
so many great boxers on the trail, we cant express our appreciation
for the organizers but also for all the AMAZING carvings and log book
creations, not to mention the one day event boxes that were so
creative!!

We can relate to Preach and Chuck trying to estimate the event stamp
attendees, the log book we started for this event has only a few
pages remaining, and our inky pages are filled with the most
tremendous artistic letterboxing efforts ever released into the
wild!!

Near as we can tell, Just from the MMM we've found 58 of the listed
boxes, 4 amazing monster HH's, 4 monstrously delightful one day
boxes, 37 laid back exchanges, 12 crypt-ic PT's (oh and Joan,
dahhling, we must tawk soon!). Two cool event stamps.. and a
partridge in a pear tree.... oops wrong holiday! *grin

So many boxes... three weeks left!

Oogie Boogie!!! Oooooh, My Precioussss! It's Alive, It's Alive!!
Rush Gatherer and PowWow Dancer
The Dawnlanders
