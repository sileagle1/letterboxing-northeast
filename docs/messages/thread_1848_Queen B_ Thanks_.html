A VERY special thanks to Queen B and her daughter who were kind
enough to meet our Winter Drive #2 Gang out at Scovill Castle after
an SOS call when one of the boxes could not be found. We searched
and searched (Donutz and Music Woman especially ) but to no avail!
Just as we were about to give up (all bummed out because that stamp
completed the series), one of the Alphabet Bandits located the box
in the stone wall! A perfect ending to a perfect day.
