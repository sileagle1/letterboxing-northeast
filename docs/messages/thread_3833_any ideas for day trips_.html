OK, all you boxers with kids. Where can we go this summer - day trips -in and around CT? I am not averse to traveling to RI, MA or NY even with the price of gas. Ideas? Let me have em!

Re: [LbNE] any ideas for day trips?

From: Ellen Dill | Date: 2008-06-26 19:01:59 UTC
I don't know how old your kids are, but mine have been boxing since they were 6, 9, and 11 -- now the two younger ones are 9 and 12.  Certainly in CT, the Noah's Ark series is AWESOME and we spent about 3 1/2 hrs. doing that one day.  The Berlin dog park -- same thing!  Now in Massachusetts, I highly recommend boxing down Cape Cod -- where there are so many wonderful boxes, or Andover -- which has more boxes than any one town in Massachusetts, unless So. Yarmouth has more,  I would guess.   We have planted, but not yet posted, a series in Foxboro, MA, which is kid-friendly.  There are ten boxes planted in the state forest now, and we're planting ten more on the other side.  Then we have other series planted in Foxboro, although we'll be retiring it soon.  There's also High Rock series in the same forest, but from another side, planted by Sagacorn.  You can keep an eye out for that.  There's Gloucester, MA; you can visit Rockport while you're
taking a break from boxing.  There's lots of great spots but these are just a few of our favorite areas!
