Lucky Stiff Update

From: davyschris@aol.com | Date: 2006-06-08 03:52:12 UTC
Hi, everyone! I know I didn't post much in the way of ticket information
when I first posted my Lucky Stiff clue, because I didn't have it. So here are
the specifics, for those who are interested:

June 8-10, 15-17, 22-24

Thursday and Friday $25 admission/byo
You will be seated at big tables and are allowed to bring munchies, dessert,
a pizza, whatever your little heart desires. :)
Doors open at 7 and the show is at 8.

Saturday is $35 admission/buffet dinner
Doors open at 6:30, dinner at 7, show at 8.

Tickets can be purchased at www.smarttix.com or by phone at 877-238-5596.
Just look under Connecticut and type in the title Lucky Stiff into the search
box. I think we're the only play they're handling in CT anyway. ;)

All performances held at:
Church of the Good Shepherd, Colt Memorial Ballroom
155 Wyllys Street
Hartford, CT 06106
Directions available at www.montagetheater.com (but don't read the synopsis
of the play... it gives EVERYTHING away!)

*Very close to the new convention center, if that helps you to navigate!*

More questions, you can email me. Davyschris@....

The May Day Lillies are running the spotlight, so you can meet up with them
too!
To get the box, just stick around for a bit after the show and we come out
and greet the audience after we change out of our costumes.

Chrissy
aka Brandy, You're A Fine Girl
