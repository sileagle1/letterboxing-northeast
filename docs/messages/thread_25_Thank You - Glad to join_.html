From: craftwoman_3 | Date: 2003-12-08 03:51:09 UTC
Hi everyone,

My family and I have only been letterboxing since September, but boy
have we had a lot of fun finding boxes since then! My 2 year old
daughter loved it when we let her pull the box out of it's hiding
spot. She got excited everytime we mentioned the word hiking.

I had met some really nice folks at the Mansfield gathering and was
excited that I had found a hobby that fits with my love (and my
husband's love) for the outdoors!

That's why it had saddend me so much to see all the hostility on the
LbNA site. I stayed silent, but was getting pretty discouraged about
my new hobby. I have been considering quitting the hobby all
together.

I'm so thankful that this new site has been created and that Preach
let me know of it. It has put the excitement back into letterboxing
for me. THANK YOU to all that made it happen!

I am planning to go to the F-500 letterbox meet at Kathy John's in
Storrs CT. on Saturday the 13th. No, I'm not THAT good! I don't have
500 finds, but I do want to meet more of you and get some more
exchanges while I'm there. Can't wait til Saturday.

Happy again,
~~CraftWoman~~
