From: (no author) | Date: 2004-10-07 22:43:01 UTC
I went letterboxing today at the Bradley Estates to get Cavy Lovers limited time only Autumn Letterboxes. Both were wonderful stamps and log books. The Bradley Estates in Canton are beautiful.

While I was there I saw a deer while I was stamping into Boo! She jumped away when she saw me and then came back and stood off the trail watching me. I then ran down to grab Duct Tape and Whimsy. (This stamp changes from time to time and I try to get each one!) And when I came back up to go back to my car....the deer was there again waiting and watching me! It was so awesome!

I went by myself as Bunny Swordsman is still on the mend and Kirby Swordsman didn't want to go. The sad thing is I didn't have my camera with me. I usually bring it when I am with the Swordsmen but I didn't bring it because it was just me! Of course...if the Swordsmen were with me I'm sure they would have scared the deer off way before we got near her!

She was awesome! This is what letterboxing is all about! Finding great letterboxes and enjoying nature! Thank you Cavy Lovers!

Peppermint Patti :)
http://www.expage.com/ppletterbox

GO PATRIOTS!
