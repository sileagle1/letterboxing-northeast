the continuing saga of the A Major Award HH

From: dave & diane | Date: 2006-02-12 22:03:44 UTC
Hey - this should go down in the record books!
I just got an email about _A Major Award/Celebrations HH_. Seems that the original stamp and the _Celebrations_ Letterbox logbook have turned up in Massachusetts.
Dave and I had launched _A Major Award HH_ way back from a now defunct RI letterbox in Coventry, RI. The HH is based on a scene from one of Dave's favorite movies - "A Christmas Story". Remember the father receives a telegram informing him that he has won "A Major Award". The award turns out to be a sexy pink and black leg-shaped lamp (not a bowling alley).
One of the hitchhiker's first trips was to DisneyWorld. At some point the Lampshade stamp left the _Celebrations_ box with the _Celebrations_ logbook for parts unknown. The origninal logbook was then returned to Dave and I and Dave eventually re-carved the stamp and we sent them both on their way again!
I'm not sure how we're going to separate the travels of these two HH's. Maybe we need to rename the original stamp's travels _A Major Celebration_, since the second stamp has the original logbook with it.
