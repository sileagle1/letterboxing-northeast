As requested here are the details for the next winter boxing drive (who's
excited?)!!!

The projected date is Sunday, February 27. Looking at my schedule, that
seems to be the only weekend day I'm free, between family stuff and rehearsals
(I'm in another play... and there will probably be a box there just like there
was for Drood).

We'll be doing this one in Lebanon. I've planned a lot of boxes, just like
last time. However, since they're all in one town, we'll be spending a lot
less time driving from box to box. Here's the route, in case you want to print
the clues:

1. Sunny Days series (pending how much snow there is on the ground). There
are 9 in this series, but it still adds up to a pretty short walk.
2. Pioneers in Lebanon
3. Gray Goose in the Town Pound
4. Hidden Cemetery series/Baby D Turns 2 (same location)
5. Baby D turns 1
6. Red Brick Meetinghouse/Jonathan Trumbull Library (first in Lebanon Loop
series) (same location)
7. Tymfurkoffi!
8. Local Heroes
9. A Night on Lebanon Green
10. My First Love
11. Pot O' Gold
12. Lebanon Loop 2
13. Lebanon Loop 3
14. Lebanon Loop 4/Bear Tracks (same park)
15. Castle Scovell series
16. Gray of Liberty Hill
17. Lebanon Lions

Whew...

Soon I'll send out a list of box-to-box directions for everyone to print, but
first I'm going to email it to Flutterby, since this is her hometown, so she
can check it for accuracy.

More details to follow!
