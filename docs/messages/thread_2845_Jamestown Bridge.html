Jamestown Bridge

From: dave & diane | Date: 2006-04-18 04:38:34 UTC
Well, looks like tomorrow is the day that the old Jamestown Bridge comes down. I'm hoping to see it happen with my Mom from somewhere in Jamestown. Her father and bBrother helped to build the bridge. My Father-in-law also worked on the bridge and will be able to view it's demolition from the Saunderstown side (on his 89th birthday). I found a few websites that have great pictures of the old bridge. Maybe even of the old resting place of the Drew Clan Letterbox. Too bad that one went missing before Dave and I started 'boxing 3 years ago. Dave and I planted a new series down in NJ in the Pine Barrens to commemorate different types of Bridges. Unless we find a local to adopt it, we will probably pull the series when Dave's active duty is done. But for now the "Bridges of Burlington County" does homage to the Jamestown Bridge (a combination truss and cantilever bridge) and 4 other types of Bridges.
Diane
