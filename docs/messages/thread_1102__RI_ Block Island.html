From: cavy_lovers4 | Date: 2004-08-13 01:51:07 UTC
We had a lovely day on Block Island (Wednesday), but were less lucky
than usual in finding boxes. Course it didn't help that I forgot the
compass in our dash out in the morning!

We did find Southeast Lighthouse Letterbox- its fine.

North Lighthouse Letterbox is located in an area that is cordoned
off with signs asking people to keep off in order to protect/restore
the dunes. So, we couldn't check on that one. Frustrating as we
could see the landmark, but gotta respect the environment.

Tried for White Rock at Black Rock, but either we took a wrong turn
or we just weren't on the same wave-length as the placer. When we
got down to the ocean, we couldn't match up the landmarks. So, we
didn't get close enough to have an idea whether its there or not. Oh
well, had a nice walk.

Tried briefly for the Indian Cemetery Lighthouse, but since I
couldn't check the compass direction, I didn't want to mess up the
lovely old rock wall scrounging around. Have to try it again someday
when I am better equipped. While there a nice fellow stopped to
chat - saw us looking around (its right next to the road) and
wondered if we were geocaching, which he was interested in. He
hadn't heard about letterboxing, so we gave him some info.


We did find the Clayhead box and we so happy we went there. The
trail is shady and pretty and the beach at the end of the trail is
gorgeous! Definitely a highlight of the day.

We also found Treasure Chest at Sandy Point.

Have to get back there to look for the rest!

Cavy Lovers
