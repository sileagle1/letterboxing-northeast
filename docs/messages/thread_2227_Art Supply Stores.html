Commercial Supply does not carry cat's eyes, but does carry most everything
else for carving. I usually get cat's eyes at Michael's craft supply. I live
in RI now and there is a great stamp store in Wickford. They have got every

Chica Ink
29 Phillips Street
Wickford, RI 02852
