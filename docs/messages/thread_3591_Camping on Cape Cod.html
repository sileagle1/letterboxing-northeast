Camping on Cape Cod

From: AC | Date: 2007-07-26 16:05:12 UTC
Hi All,



We're throwing together a camping trip to Cape Cod. We'll be arriving Sunday, August 19th and
leaving Wednesday, August 22nd. We've reserved a group site 2 in area 2 at Shawme-Crowell State
Park in Sandwich that holds from 25 - 50 people. Depending on how many we wind up with, the fee
could be as low a $1 per person per night (or less if we have more than 25 people). So far we have
Kreepy Kelli and family, Talking Turtle and the Alphabet Bandits, and the Wildcats for a total of 10
people.



There are definitely boxes on the Cape, - 30 in Sandwich, another 50 or so within 10 miles. This
isn't a boxing event per se but there will be an "event" stamp, if anyone is interested we can night
box.



For those who have never been or are interested in going even if you have been, we'll be going on a
whale watching cruise leaving from nearby Barnstable on Tuesday. Which is about $35pp.



If you are interested in joining us, e-mail me off list to reserve your spot at our group site and
if you'd like to join us for the Whale watching cruise.



TT
