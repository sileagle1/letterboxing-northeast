Although it was delayed by a week, the box of the month is now in place. I received it yesterday afternoon and placed it today. It is a wonderful creation by Batty Girl of TX and you won't want to miss it.

I do, however, have to caution you to do the red trail loop in a counter clockwise fashion and to head back the same way. The field of nettles is very high and unforgiving as of this evening!!! Poor Baby D had to be carried through it and  Doodles and Reilly were trying to walk without having their feet touch the ground. Other than the fact that it was a bit painful (to me as well in shorts) it was actually rather comical watching it. At least my husband was smart enough to wear long pants and boots!!

Because it was placed a week late, it will remain out longer than usual. I will be pulling this box early in the morning on September 29th.
