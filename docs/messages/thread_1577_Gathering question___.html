Re: [LbNE] Gathering question...

From: hannahkat@cox.net | Date: 2004-11-08 13:48:30 UTC
Gatherings can be quite overwhelming for a new 'boxer. Everyone is trying to get exchanges with every new person they meet. Even if the rest of the crowd are veterans and already know each other, they will all be clamoring for this new person's stamp. I would say it's better to go out and actually letterbox a few times before attending a gathering...though you will surely be inspired by all the extremely nice people you will meet at a gathering.

-Kim (rustypuff)
