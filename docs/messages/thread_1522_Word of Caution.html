Word of Caution

From: flutterby768 | Date: 2004-10-29 13:48:53 UTC
~I wanted to let everyone know about an incident that happened
to me this week. I was in Scotland at Spignesi Wildlife Management
Area. I was attired in my blaze orange as was my dog. I saw a couple
of hunters (wearing blaze orange as well) across a field, made eye
contact and proceeded in opposite directions. Later on we came across
each other (while I was stamping in) at a stone wall. We talked, the
dogs barked etc. There was no mistaking the fact that we were all
aware of each other. The hunters moved on and I proceeded 20 steps
down the same wall to a bonus box. As I was stamping into my log book
I heard a shot and bark from the tree I was standing next to rained
down on me! I was furious (and scared). I confronted them out on the
trail. Their response was-'well, it is hunting season. You shouldn't
be out in places where hunting is allowed!'
I am not in any way against hunting or hunters. I also do not
want to come off as believing that all hunters are this careless and
inconsiderate. Carelessness makes me furious though. I realize that
all of us are out there and I would like everyone to be safe. Please
everyone have on blaze orange and make some noise-bells, whatever. At
least as hikers we will be doing all that we can to be safe. If there
is anything else that we should be doing please post to the list so
that everyone will be aware of it.
Flutterby~
