#4 If anyone would like to take over the duties of Clue Master please do

#5 For those who have never played clue, the basic concept is that from
a blind pool, a suspect, weapon and location are chosen. The remaining
stamps are then shuffled again and passed out to those playing. The
object is to exchange stamps with everyone playing until you can figure
out which ones are missing from the original pool. Once you think you
know the correct missing pieces, you DISCREETLY whisper it to the Clue
Master. The winners will not be announced until 1st, 2nd and 3rd places
have been confirmed. Prizes will be awarded then.
