Interesting thought ... clearing a state

From: cpascott | Date: 2005-10-05 14:22:32 UTC
Hey fellow 'boxers.

Out of curiousity, I was looking at the number of published boxes in
New England (3,297) and starting thinking -- has anybody made it a
goal to clear a state?

Now that I've started 'boxing in Connecticut, I've made it a goal to
clear towns. For example, a full 'boxing day in Eastford a month or
so ago picked up all the boxes in that town except one (of course
someone has since planted another box there since my hunt). A similar
attempt in the town of Ashford was somewhat successful, until foiled
by the challenge of Paige's Tales.

Anyway ... although a state like Connecticut, with over 1600 published
boxes (and hundreds more WOM) might take years to "clear", a state
like Rhode Island (250 published) or Vermont (75 published) might not.

Of course, my weekends are rather full in the fall, and clearing a
state requires some serious effort, but it's gotten me thinking that
maybe, just maybe, I'll concetrate most of my letterboxing hunts in a
particular state in an attempt to clear it. Hmmm ... Rhode Island
here I come?


Thoughts?


CPAScott

Re: Interesting thought ... clearing a state

From: wandaandpete | Date: 2005-10-14 21:42:09 UTC
Yes, interesting to find out that more folks are now catching on to
that idea of doing all the boxes in a particular state like we've
been trying to do for years! Besides our home state of RI, where we
have found over 500 boxes, not counting our own several dozen, of
course, we used to mostly concentrate on CT. Every time we'd get
within a box or two of completing CT, out would come another dozen or
so boxes! Ultimately, between bonuses, add-ons, WOM's and many boxes
that have come and gone, we figured it would be pretty impossible to
get them all! In fact, it was pretty amusing to us to see that the
total number of boxes listed on LbNA for ALL of New England is
several hundred LESS than the number of boxes we have already found
just in CT alone!!!

Anyway, it's a nice goal, but the idea of "filling" a state can be
just as fun as "clearing" one. After all, when we first started out
letterboxing, there was only one box in the entire state of Nevada -
a Mapsurfer at the complete other end of the state from where we were
going - so it was absolutely thrilling to be able to double the count
of Nevada boxes by planting two boxes near Las Vegas, instead
of "clearing the state" of its one! And if we ever get to North
Dakota, we'll hope to double the box count there as well!:-)

Hope this "letterboxing party" goes on for a long time yet to come,
and that we never actually "clear" any of the states, as new boxes
continue to get planted, even as old boxes go missing or "retire"!

Cheers and thanks,

Wanda and Pete
