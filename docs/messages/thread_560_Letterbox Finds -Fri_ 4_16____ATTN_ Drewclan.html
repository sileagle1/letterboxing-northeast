On Friday, Moonbeam and I ventured out to Gillette Castle to have
lunch along the CT. river. Afterwards, with our bellies full, it was
off to pick up a few boxes. First we visited "Cove Cemetery"(cool
stamp!) then off to "Sherlock's Home" ***Both boxes have full
logbooks and dried-out inkpads!---also, box#1 needs a new
container*** Being that I was with no supplies, I could not provide
the needed repairs.(sorry!) Then, the "African Queen"(good condition)
and "The Grinch in CT" (another cool stamp S&R!) We followed those up
with "Flower Drum Song" on the way home.(logbooks are just about full
also!) Then it was back to the house for a quick rest before setting
out on my own to Sleeping Giant to grab the "Tower" box(M&M's) for my
last find of the day. Thanks for all the cool boxes guys!! --
