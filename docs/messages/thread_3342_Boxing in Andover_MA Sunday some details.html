I think a good central location would be Wild Oats in
Andover. It's the Rt.28 exit off of Rt. 495 (heading
to Andover, not Lawerence). You'll go through an
intersection with a car dealer on the left and bridal
shop on the right. Go straight until you pass a mall
area on the left (McDonald's etc) and start heading up
a hill. Wild Oats will be on the right. Have everyone
meet at the parking area to the right of the store.

This is where we will converge...!
