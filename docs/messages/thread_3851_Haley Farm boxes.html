Haley Farm boxes

From: quinny75@aol.com | Date: 2008-07-16 00:04:21 UTC
I visited Haley Farm in Groton, CT today with a camp group. This was their
first time letterboxing (for many) and they were really excited. Sadly, the
2 box series, The Cemetery and Picnic Rock, was in terrible shape and should
really be completely redone or removed. The 1st box was just where it should
be, but the box was broken in many places and the contents were soaking wet
and filthy. The stamp was broken into 2 pieces. I wished that I had brought
some supplies with me to repair it, but really everything needs to be
replaced. The second box has a really neat spot and container, but sadly it has
been overtaken by geocachers. There is a nearby cache that is supposed to be
hidden within 50 feet of the letterbox, so I am guessing that many people see
this 1st and assume it is what they are looking for. The mailbox was full of
loose papers and trinkets, but the stamp was nowhere to be found. The kids
were pretty hot and getting tired by this point and were very disappointed
when they couldn't stamp their books. I assumed since the series had been
found in May, that it would be good to find, but really it needs serious
maintenance. Didn't see a contact placer link for this box, so if anyone out there
knows how to contact Karen, please let her know the poor condition of her
boxes.

On a happier note, we did successfully locate the Drew Clan's Canopy Rock
box. Someone added a replacement log book a while back and the stamp, book and
box were all in very good condition. Thanks for ending our day on a high
note!!
