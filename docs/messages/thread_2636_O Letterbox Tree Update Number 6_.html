O Letterbox Tree Update Number 7

From: Mary Nelson | Date: 2005-12-26 22:35:44 UTC
The O Letterbox Tree is moving to a new tree because of a visit from a Grinch. The boxes and decorations are being refurbished and will be available tomorrow (Tuesday) with these corrected directions:

From Route 6 take Station Ave toward South Yarmouth. Turn right at the second set of lights onto Old Townhouse Road. Go to the end and turn left on West Yarmouth Rd. Take the 3rd left (Sheffield) and drive to the end. Turn right, go to the end and turn right again (this is just going around the corner). O Letterbox Tree is located in front of the first house on the right (40 Mockingbird Lane). This is a private residence and a residential area. Please drive slowly as there are always many children on bikes. It is only about a 5 minute drive from the highway. Please do not approach the fence that encloses the backyard. It drives the dogs crazy.
