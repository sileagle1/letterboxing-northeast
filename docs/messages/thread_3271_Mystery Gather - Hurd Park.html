Mystery Gather - Hurd Park

From: donutz716 | Date: 2006-10-15 02:39:42 UTC
Wow! What a nice intimate affair! Hats off to Swamp Yankee, Sunshine, Ace, Grumpy, Hez, Mona and all who carved stamps and planted for the event. It was fun to see all my letterboxing friends and meet new ones. Hurd Park is a great place for hiking and this is the best time of year to get out there and enjoy the fresh air! I didn't get all the boxes, but the ones I got were so much fun to find and I will definitely go back to get the rest before the year is done. There was more than enough food, a nice toasty fire to remove the chill and smiling faces all around. Thanks to all for a wonderful day!

Enjoy!
donutz716
