Holcomb Farm update...

From: dthreedvm01 | Date: 2010-01-21 03:30:37 UTC
Jonah and I went to Holcomb Farm to check on The Tree Letterbox on the Pine Grove (green-blazed) trail above the cow pastures (across Simsbury Road from the parking lot), as it had no online logs in over a year. We were pleasantly surprised to see it is in good shape with a roomy, dry logbook, a decent inkpad, and a stamp in fair condition. The last section of trail leading you to the box is a messy combination of icy, muddy, and puddly, so choose footwear and clothing appropriately!

Happy Boxing!
