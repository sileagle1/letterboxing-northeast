April bookmaking programs at Southwest Harbor Library

From: limur | Date: 2008-03-26 14:03:27 UTC
For all you boxers who make your own logbooks, I thought I'd pass on
some information about upcoming April programs/talks at the Southwest
Harbor Public Library (where I work.) One of our staff is involved in
a book and card making group/exchange and their work will be showcased
in the library along with several talks by some of the people involved.

All events are at 7pm on Thursdays: On the 10th there will be a
workshop at on making artist's books; the 17th will be on a talk and
workshop on making pop-up books; and on the 24th will be a talk and
workshop with the members of book artists group.

For more information contact the library at 207-244-7065.

Enjoy!
