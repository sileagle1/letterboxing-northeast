From: Bell Lady | Date: 2007-05-06 02:01:07 UTC
What a beautiful day to be out letterboxing and to make it even more
special, I logged my 2000th find at Union Pond Park with Napoleon
Dynamite Flippin' Sweet. And that's exactly what I thought!

I don't know who was boxing ahead of us but most of the Napoleon boxes
were very poorly concealed if at all. No wonder some boxes go
missing. We did our best to hide them completely. We also tracked
down Guarding the Hitchhiker Hostel and the Ship It series. Enjoyed
them all ending the day with F2005.

Bell Lady
