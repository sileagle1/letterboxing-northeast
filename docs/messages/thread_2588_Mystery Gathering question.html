Hi all ... a little late to the praise party, but I'll add my anyway!

A great gathering ... surprised to see so many people at this one,
especially compared to the relatively small mystery gathering last year.

Good to see Brian and Lori from Team Green Dragon come up from New Jersey.

I was glad to meet the group of Cape Cod 'boxers, all of whom I know
by signature stamp but never before had met.

And what a great little village Wickford is! I'll be back with the SO
to explore more.

Of course, always thanks to Pointer Party for letting me tag along on
the hunts (Kreepy Kelli as well).

And -- for those who like to share in milestones, I finally hit F500
at the event -- with Mark & Sue's Kitchen Angel box. It's taken a
year-and-a-half to get here, but I'm hoping that it will not be
another year-and-a-half to hit the illustrious F1000 mark! Brandy,
Warrior Woman, Team Randalstik and anyone else who wants to -- I'm
counting on you guys to continue to 'box with me so I can get that
next milestone!! :-)


OK, question ...

I have misplaced the clues to the Path of Angels series, and haven't
recorded the proper name and carver of each of the three stamps in
that series. Could someone list them, please?


Many thanks!

See you all at the next gathering!!


CPAScott
