I do box maintenance on all my boxes in MA and RI each early spring
and late fall. I find my boxes in all kinds of states, from gasp!
faint to pristine. I just finished my early spring maintenance last
weekend (phew!).

I would like to give my Last Finder's Award to -

(drum roll please)

Bluebird, Pine Tree, Wolfy, Sweet Pea, and Sky!

Westerly, RI. You found them on December 31, 2005. You put the
boxes back together and re-hid them so well that three months later,
after a New England winter, they were still dry and intact and
*still* completely well hidden!

(round of applause)

Warrior Woman
