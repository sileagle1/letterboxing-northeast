Pt Judith Restored

From: Warrior Woman | Date: 2004-01-06 20:10:39 UTC
Just a quick note to let everyone know that I received word that the
Pt Judith Letterbox was in tough shape.

As the maintainer, I went down today and restored it with fresh new
double ziplocked bags and a new container.

No damage was done to the logbook or the stamp, but the proximity of
this letterbox to a nearby geocache creates a lot of non-boxing
inquisitiveness, and the box gets much non-boxing wear and tear.

This is my third restoration in the last year! But the box is worth
keeping as Mad Mary (the original placer) is a great carver.
