ME: Lakes Region Event

From: Phyto | Date: 2004-07-25 11:35:48 UTC
Thanks to all those who made the trek up to Raymond, Maine and braved
the Route 302 traffic! The Lakes Region Letterboxing Event was a great
time had by all - though it was fairly short.

Boxers traveled from as far away as Cape Cod, Rhode Island,
Massachusettes, and even the Brown Knight and family were in
attendance all the way from Indiana! It was nice to see new as well as
familiar faces.

Here's a few of the people that were present:
Phyto & Isosceles of Bangor, Maine
Nautilus of Sanford, Maine
Deb Blanchard of South Portland, Maine
Team Green Dragon of Taunton, Mass.
Phynstar of Maine by way of NY.
Oceanwytch of Portland, Maine
Dragonfly of Cape Cod, Mass.
Team Caffeine of Providence, Rhode Island
Brown Knight of W. Lafayette, Indiana
Wanda of Wanda & Pete
Gryphon of Westbrook, Maine
Beedle & Mac of Richmond, Maine
Postal of Edgecomb, Maine
Uneksia of Hillsboro, NH
The Dynamic Duo

I'm sure to have missed a few here and there. There were students from
Deb Blanchard's elementary school classes! There were also plenty of
nearby boxes to be found - some that I'm sure I even missed. Wanda may
have been heard saying something to the effect that it was the most
concentrated number of boxes she's ever found in one area! ??!

For those who boxed afterwards, did anyone try the Holt Pond Preserve
Porcupine Den? Nautilus, Phyto and Isosceles braved this box, but with
regrets as Phyto got stung by a hornet. If you attempt this box,
please confirm that it is present with the placer or preserve. I was
disappointed to be stung by a hornet on top of not being able to find
the box. Where on earth does the trail go "up"????

Nice to see some faces again and meet some new folks.
phyto

sychronistic. Anyway had a blast and wanted to say Thanks you, it was great to meet
some of the Letterboxing community!
