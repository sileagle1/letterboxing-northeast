Record broken Sunday

From: Donna Magner | Date: 2005-06-06 12:08:27 UTC
Hi all,

Well, wish I could say that I broke my own record for finding the most boxes in one day, but alas - that is not the case. We broke the record for finding the most snakes and the longest on the trails at Rocky Neck state park! First we say a 12 inch garter, next about a 15 inch garter. On the blue trail, we say a 3 foot black snake. Then we say a 4 foot (plus) dark colored snake with faded markings. This one made a noise to let us know he was there. It was very similar to a rattle, but we don't think it was a rattler - maybe it just hissed. All I know is we were quite happy to get back to the truck and get on our bikes!

Stay safe and Enjoy!

Donutz716
