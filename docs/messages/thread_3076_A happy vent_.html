A happy vent!

From: jps225 | Date: 2006-07-18 04:24:35 UTC
I cannot explain why I enjoy this little pastime to others well, but
it just amazes me how much time I have seemed to invest in it. Just in
the last two weeks I hit a personal milestone, though. In July 2004
Time Magazine's article on Letterboxing was handed to me by my wife to
get me to get off the couch, and have fun with the whole family. I
made my first box before I even found my first Box (Prayer Rock, Vt)!
For some bizarre reason I came to the conclusion that I would place 40
boxes in one year. Well, not one , but, two years later I did it.
Trout Brook Valley #4: The Burgundian Castle is 40 (though 41 and 42
just came a week later in Arizona). It seems silly to be proud of this
stuff but there are a heck of a lot of memories with my kids in
placing most of these (my wife will hunt these but not place them).
Sorry for the vent but it just needed to get out!

J. Peter

PS I have lost a few so far, but never one in a Connecticut rock wall!
