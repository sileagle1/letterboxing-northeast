Hi all --

Sounds like the Maine gathering was a success (stings aside). As
noted earlier, the Weathergleams were spoken for to the eastward
that day, attending a wedding, but improved upon their time away
with a little boxing. Alive and well:

The Early Bird (nota bene: this log is full; we stamped the inside
back cover),
Back Beach I,
Bass Harbor Vista,
One More Round,
Peacock's Pandora, and
a L'ovely series.

Not found: Charlotte's View.

Merciless time constraints, driving rain and little legs kept us
from any serious attempt to orient ourselves expressly, much less to
vary the theme, sorely tempted though we were. Our all too casual
investigations near beaches and firs in the clouds were predictably
unrewarded, and properly so. However, there will be a next time.

And with far less bravado, probably a next time after that...
