From: Team Safari | Date: 2007-06-18 21:59:59 UTC
Oh, just wait to see how HUGE poison ivy gets near the ocean around here. It grows in clumps on the ground like an innocent ground cover, up to big bushes that will swallow you whole. ;-) Trust me on this, I fell into one. It also will grow into a thick, hairy vine that will engulf, choke, and suck the life out of a full-grown oak. I'm not kidding. Come to RI and be amazed. Someday we will disappear into a p-ivy patch between CT and MA, and people will forget RI was ever here. (Though, many people already don't know we are here, and think we are part of NY. But I digress...)

Please let us know if you want to chance boxing here. We are not careful or carful... we are caffel here, and we won't let it get you if you box with us.

Welcome to New England!

Mary
Team Safari

RE: [LbNE] Newport, R.I. ???

From: RIFamily | Date: 2007-06-19 20:40:27 UTC
Oh, there are so many!

On the way, if you go via rt 295 (might add 5 minutes to your time vs rt 95)
you can stop at the rest stop there.
http://www.atlasquest.com/lboxes/showboxinfo.html?gBoxId=27741
Very quick... good for a leg stretch.

On the way over to Newport you can stop at this one in Jamestown... another
quick one
http://www.letterboxing.org/BoxView.php?boxnum=4361&boxname=Island%20Sanctua
ry

This one says it's missing, but if you are going to be right there anyway
(and if you are in Newport then at some point you will be at Brenton Point!)
it's an easy one worth the try since it sounds like the planters haven't
checked for themselves yet, so perhaps it is there!
http://www.letterboxing.org/BoxView.php?boxnum=25150&boxname=Skyscraper

We attempted this one a few weeks ago... left very confused and the placer
hasn't replied to me yet
http://www.letterboxing.org/BoxView.php?boxnum=30686&boxname=The_Triskele_St
amp
So I don't know if I would suggest trying it or not.

Have a great time!

RIFamily
