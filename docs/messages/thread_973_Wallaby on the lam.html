Does anyone have the talent to do a Wallaby Hitchhiker?
Taken from the AP line in last Sunday's paper:

"PLYMOUTH - A Plymouth woman is putting her neighbors on notice to be on the lookout for a kangaroo-like creature hopping through their backyards. Her pet wallaby, Joey, escaped three weeks ago and has since been spotted by two residents, including one who claims the marsupial was eating her birdseed. Jan Veilleux, 48, collects exotic pets. She said Joey knocked out her sliding screen door and took off. Plymouth animal control officer Julie Swiridowsky said it's likely that the overweight, grayish brown, 60-pound wallaby is either dead or has been adopted now."
