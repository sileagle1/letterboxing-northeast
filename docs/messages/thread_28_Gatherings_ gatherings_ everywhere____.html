Just to throw my two cents in--not that it matters since I probably
won't be able to go to any gatherings in the northeast anytime soon--
but the whole "Maine or Massachusetts" location seems kind of silly.
The two areas are so far apart--a good four weeks on foot, I'd say--
it would be beneficial for gatherings in both places. A MA gathering
might prevent Mainers from visiting, while a ME gathering might not
allow a lot of those from CT, MA, RI, etc. to visit.

And if all gatherings were created solely on majority rule, every
gathering will always be in the same place. Most people will vote
for whatever location is closest to them, and CT is infamous for
their large boxer population. Throw in all those adjacent states,
and no other states would ever have a chance of hosting an event--
especially at the farthest off distances such as Maine.

So, my vote is for a gathering in both locations. =) Preferably on
different dates, though, so people can go to both gatherings if time
and energy permit. =)

Unfortuantely, I'm even farther off the beaten path than Maine, and
shall not be able to attend either event. But, I'll do my best to
make a Portland, Oregon, gathering that Funhog suggested. ;o)
