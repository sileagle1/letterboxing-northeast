I was looking around in the geocaching forums, and I found this
thread. It looks like the CT DEP is going to require permits for
geocaching. Their concerns do not presently extend to letterboxing.
We should keep an eye on this and see how it develops.

There may have been an unfortunate placement of a geocache in a
historical location that started the concern. From the discussion it
looks like when a park manager is brought into the activity and has
geocaching explained to him or her, they are enthused with the
activity.
