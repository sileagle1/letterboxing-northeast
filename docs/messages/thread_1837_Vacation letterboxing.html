Vacation letterboxing

From: J. BRIDGMAN | Date: 2005-02-25 03:40:53 UTC
Schools are on vacation which means that Mr. B can go letterboxing
with me. Took advantage of a couple of great days and went out to
see what we could find in the snow. Headed out to Lebanon since we
won't be able to go on the coming Winter Driveby and picked up
several of those. Snow on rocks makes an interesting challenge. We
will certainly return for more one day. Today, we hunted down the new
ones in Simsbury placed by sea-cret and were first finders of all
four. You had better be in pretty good shape to get to a couple of
them! It was a great day to be out.
