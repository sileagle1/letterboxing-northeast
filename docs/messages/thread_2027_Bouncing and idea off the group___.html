Bouncing and idea off the group...

From: AC | Date: 2005-05-02 21:30:19 UTC
Hi All,

I'd like to bounce an idea off you all.

I have been approached by a local historical society who is planning
their annual fundraiser home tour. The proceeds of the tour help
support the preservation efforts for a Newburgh, NY Revolutionary War
era home. This year they are interested in doing something a little
different to entice "new blood" to visit the historic homes and have
asked me to conduct a road rally style event.

Here are some of the details which are still in a VERY rough form.

Number of stops: 6 definitely, possibly 10
Event only stamps
You must attend the brief tour of the home to obtain access/directions
to the box which will be on the property.
As it is a fundraiser, there will be a fee of approximately $25 which
covers the tours for all the homes and the souvenir book.
Date: Sunday 12 June 2005

I have discussed with the person trying to organize this event my
thoughts. One of the appeals of letterboxing it that it is a low
cost/free hobby for the finders (I've tried to convince Lascivious
Lion/Box Hound that it's free, but he yain't buyin' it). And that I
wasn't sure how well that would translate into an "event" for
letterboxing. While I love the idea, I think $25 would be prohibitive
for many. It does however; cover the whole family so that might be a
bonus.

If you all have any suggestions or tweaks that you think might make this
possibly happen, I'd love to hear your input. Thanks for your time.

Patiently awaiting your input,

Talking Turtle
Of the
Alphabet Bandits
