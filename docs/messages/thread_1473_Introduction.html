Hi everyone,
Some of you may know me. I have been on the usa group a few times
but I need a smaller, more local place to belong to. I am slowly
trying to place some urban boxes in nyc. I placed two, though one is
missing and I need to check on it. Have one more ready and have
asked permission to place another one. It's slow going. Urban boxes
are a unique undertaking and can be time consuming, researching
places to place them, checking the places out, etc.
I was at the Palmyra gathering in NJ, my first one. It was great!
Everyone was really nice. LetterboxingBee did a great job. I'll be
at daspirit222's gathering in Medina and hope to meet some of you
there.

From: grumpygrinchy | Date: 2005-02-02 13:56:40 UTC
We did indeed. I had printed out numerous clues along our route,
put them in files by state, and we stopped whenever possible. We
spent an entire day in Houston, half a day in Tucson, and several
days throughout Florida before heading north. Our find count jumped
from 164 to over 220 on our journey. Now that we are in deeply
frozen New Hampshire, and the Grumpy half of our team is working
during the week, our find count is slowing. I have hopes for the
weekends, however. Just have to figure out which boxes are
realistically available in the snow and ice.
