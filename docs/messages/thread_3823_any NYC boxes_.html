I am taking my Girl Scouts to NYC in July. Most of the Manhatten boxes I found have been retired or are listed as unavailble. I guess they go missing quickly in the Big Apple. The Firemens Memorial box is still active and I found 2 in Central Park that we may be able to try. Anyone know of any others that are close to downtown, Times Square or Museum of Natural History area? I'm surprised there aren't more in Central Park or inside the library or some of the museums.

From: Melissa Ray | Date: 2008-06-16 20:50:19 UTC
Hi Shelley,

It's hard to keep any box going in NYC, due to high rate of boxes
taken. My son and I have found the two boxes at the boat basin,
which is within reasonable distance of the Museum of Natural
History. The first box is very small, and I almost missed it, but my
son spotted it. We did find one box in central park (Charlie
Chaplin), but later found out that the box had been retired and was
about to be dismantled. There's a great one near Union Square in a
costume store called Halloween Adventure. It's a fun store, and the
box/book are specially themed to match.

I hope that helps!

Red Cloak
