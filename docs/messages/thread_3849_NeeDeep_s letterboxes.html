NeeDeep's letterboxes

From: Chuck & Amy | Date: 2008-07-15 17:26:28 UTC
About a week ago Lenny of The NeeDeeps came over for a visit. One of the subjects that we talked about was letterboxing. It turns out that he is not going to be letterboxing anymore and he turned all his boxes over to me for adoption. Most of the boxes haven't been maintained or clues updated for a few years. I have started to check on them already but with checking the adopted boxes, my own boxes and looking for new boxes, it's going to take some time. I will probably be putting some of the boxes up for adoption to other boxers.This was discussed with Lenny and he's ok with that.  As I put some of the NeeDeep boxes up for readoption, I hope some of you volunteer to help keep some of these historic boxes alive.....Chuck & Amy

Re: [LbNE] Re: NeeDeep's letterboxes

From: Chuck & Amy | Date: 2008-07-16 17:54:10 UTC
Thanks everyone for the offers to pitch in and adopt The NeeDeep's boxes  I'll be posting a list of available ones on LBNE. He had about 80 boxes listed. I know that there were no boxes in Hartford, Litchfield or Fairfield counties.  I'll take the boxes that would be best for me, and post everything else. Volunteers can then contact me to adopt what they want. Anything left over I will also keep, no problem. I took on the responsibility of adopting the boxes thinking that I may be doing them all anyway so every box adopted by another person is one less than I expected. The NeeDeeps were good friends and great letterboxers. It will be great to have the boxes up and running again. Keep in mind that the boxes will almost certainly need maintainence. I've already found that some needed clue adjustments. Trails, houses, posts were painted different colors. New trails have been made. Landmarks such as trees, standing and across the trail are gone
etc. Some of these boxes are 6 years old so they are going to need some tlc to get them back in shape. Some may even be missing and no longer exist. I guess we just retire those and put the clues in the archives. Will try to get that list together soon.....Chuck and Amy ._,___
