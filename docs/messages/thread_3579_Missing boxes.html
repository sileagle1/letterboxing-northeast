As one of the folks who has had their boxes removed by an unknown
person or persons I just wanted to say that having been robbed three
times within the last year and a half, this is just a glitch in the
road for us. I'm saddened that one of the boxes that was my favorite,
and one that I worked very hard on, was taken, but in the scheme of
things it's just a game/hobby after all. The folks who removed our
boxes are just as cowardly as the people who robbed us. It'a a shame
that they couldn't put their talents to some pursuit of greater good.
People are suffering and dying all over the world and these people
couldn't find anything better to do than spend their energies on
hunting down tupperware in the woods to destroy. If it wasn't so
pathetic it would be laughable.
If they are reading this post, they have my permission to fill my
tupperware with writing materials or toiletries and send them to a
charity of their choice.
If they don't have a favorite charity, which I tend to think may be
closer to the truth, they may use the containers for leftovers.
