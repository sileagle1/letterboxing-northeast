Virus alert

From: Brian, Ryan & Lori | Date: 2006-06-13 00:51:32 UTC
I wanted to post and warn people about this new "New Graphic Site"
virus that appears to be going around. I'm no expert at this kind of
thing but I do know that it has effected some of you here on this
list. The virus seems to take control of peoples email and sends
this message out to groups they belong to as well as people in there
email directories. I've always had a first post monitored function
running on this list but this new virus seems to have found a way
around my precautions. I'm pretty sure that Carole Burke didn't post
the msg you see below. Carol correct me if I'm wrong.

I've always done my best to keep this talk list as free of this type
of thing as I possibly could but as these things get more and more
complicated the best of my intentions seem to be of little or no
good. All the advise I can give is that anybody who opened the link
in the posts should run a virus scan as soon as possible and I will
do what I can to keep the damage to a minimum.

Thanks to all for your Patience and understanding while we weather
this storm.

Brian
TeamGreenDragon
