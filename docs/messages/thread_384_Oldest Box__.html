Re: Oldest Box??

From: Steve and Heidi | Date: 2004-03-19 13:06:50 UTC
The way we've heard early CT LB Lore.... "Misery Loves Company" is
indeed the 1st box in connecticut -- placed June 27, 1998. Elaine and
Jerry's Bluff in Groton is close though, also placed in '98. Both
are creations of The Ram (and Eastern Mountain Sports). Stopping in
at the Crystal Mall and asking Eric may be the best way to solve it
though, he's one of the handful credited with creating this
letterboxing monstrosity that CT has become.


-Steve and Heidi
