Help needed for a letterboxer in need!

From: Pam | Date: 2007-03-02 11:28:20 UTC
For numerous months now, one of our dear Atlasquest letterboxers,
Purpleintexkim, has been dealing with some major ordeals, including
her husband being seriously ill (injured?), and now a bad storm has
destroyed their storage shed with all of their children's summer
clothing, blankets, quilts, collectible items, and anything they had
to store because they live in a small home. Thankfully, the house
had no damage, however, they lost a lot. (Though, no letterboxing
supplies were lost ;-)

They happen to have a Walmart near their home in Texas, and I was
wondering if anyone from the snowy north would like to donate $
towards a Walmart gift card, so they can start replacing some of
their lost items. I feel that one card would be easier than multiple
small denomination cards. I have a paypal account and would be
willing to centrally locate the donations to send one Walmart card.

If you would like to donate quilts or blankets, or children's summer
clothes directly, I can get a list of children's sizes.

If you are interested in helping, please e-mail me off list at
mindizney@.... In this time of Lent, let's help a letterboxing
family get back on their feet.
