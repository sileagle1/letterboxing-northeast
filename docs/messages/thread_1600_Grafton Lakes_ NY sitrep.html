Grafton Lakes, NY sitrep

From: Pamela Smith Lenox | Date: 2004-11-15 13:12:47 UTC
My daughter and I got just darn sick of being cooped up due to various
recent illnesses and accidents, so we headed out on the trail
yesterday. Of course, with the snow and leaves, things were slippery,
so we're sore from slipping on slopes and turning ankles on hidden
rocks, but we had a ball!

We found Grafton Lakes State Park - #3+4 of the series. We made it as
far as the picnic area before the beach before my daughter decided
she'd had enough, so #1+2 will have to wait for another day - just as
well as the lake was gorgeous now and we can't wait to see it in
springtime. :-)

Anyway, we were 3rd finders on both boxes and both were in good shape.
The ink pad in #3 is somewhat dry, but since most of us hike with ink,
that's no big deal. Bags and boxes and hiding places are all intact.

Box on!
pezpam (and Miss Buggy)
