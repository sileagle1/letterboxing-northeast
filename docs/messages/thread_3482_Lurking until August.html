Hi New England Letterboxers. We lived in southern New Hampshire for 11
years before we moved to California 3 years ago. It was just AFTER we
got to the left coast that we discovered letterboxing. Finally, we get
to spend a week in Ogunquit, Maine in August and plan to spend most of
the time boxing (and eating Lobster.)

next several months we will be reading this group to learn more about
letterboxing in the great northeast. Thanks for all the hard work you
have contributed to this hobby. We look forward to finding some of
your boxes, and leaving a few of ours for you to find.
