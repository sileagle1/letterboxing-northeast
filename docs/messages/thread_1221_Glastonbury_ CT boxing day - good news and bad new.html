Glastonbury, CT boxing day - good news and bad news

From: btoesatbarker | Date: 2004-09-01 21:05:57 UTC
Had a great day in Glastonbury today. I got 17 for 17 boxes! Did Old
Route 2 Gas Station before Music Woman pulls it. (My appologies to
her for following the last person who stamped in backwards and upside
down and did it myself!) I then went to Shoddy Mill, got a little
confused, but found the box farther along the trail than I thought.
The box was left out on the ground, not hidden at all. Thankfully, it
was off the trail quite a bit so no one could see it. I then went to
Frog Pond at Cotton Hollow. That was also much farther down the trail
than I thought. Next to the Audubon Center for Sunshine Daydream and
Audubon boxes. I was there before the center opened, but got lucky
when one of the ladies who works there arrived just as I was getting
ready to leave, and she let me in to get the bonus box. I next
visited the bats at Poppy's Riverside Bat House. I looked into the
house, but could not see any bats. They must have been way up inside.
Going to Candlewick Kennels to get Casey-Girl was a moving
experience. If you have been there, you know what I mean. My last
stop was at Blackledge Falls for the Good Grief! series. Went from
Schroder right to Lucy by mistake. I missed four boxes in-between!
Found them on my hike back, though. What a beautiful spot. I wish I
had read the clues before I left home, since they mentioned
Deertracks in the Snow. Oh well, just an excuse to go back! All the
boxes that I visited today were in good health, but on a sadder note,
several along the Good Grief! trail were exposed. One was not hidden
at all, just left behind a tree, and others with 4 or 5 small sticks
that did not cover the boxes in the least. If anyone knows the
Lamberts of Glastonbury who use a frog stamp with a top hat and cane,
please explain to them the importance of covering the boxes
completely. They were the last ones to stamp in and since this has
been such a hot topic lately, maybe it's time to name names! Anyway,
I will be going to Hartman with Flutterby tomorrow and meeting up
with Butterfly there. Can't wait!
Queen B
