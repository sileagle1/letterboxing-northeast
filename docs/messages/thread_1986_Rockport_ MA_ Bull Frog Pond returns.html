Rockport, MA: Bull Frog Pond returns

From: TurtleMcQ | Date: 2005-04-20 12:23:26 UTC
Call it yet another sign of spring, but the supposedly lost Bull Frog Pond
box in lovely Rockport, MA has been found alive and waiting for new
visitors! The little frog was just where I left it, only tucked a little
deeper into its home where it apparently hibernated quite comfortably
through the long winter. I've added the clues back to LBNA now that it's no
longer presumed missing.

Many thanks to Carol and her kids for making the extra effort and rescuing
this box for others to enjoy.
