Rescued two boxes yesterday (RI)

From: Nancy | Date: 2005-02-09 14:38:31 UTC
Yesterday I went to find the Towers letterbox and as I was
walking to it I could clearly see it from a distance. The person who
was there last was a nonboxer and had written that they thought it
was a box of weed(?), if they meant the kind that you smoke I didn't
know the locals hid it in tupperware. I placed the box behind the
rock and it is no longer visible.
Next I went to find the Few, the Proud. I found the Grey Owl HH,
of the critter racing fame, with his feathers so ruffled, I guess
cause he's stuck in RI, that he'd popped the lid clean off the box.
Good thing I got him out of there before the rain, snow, and sleet
that's expected to fall on that very special box tomorrow. I had to
take grey owl home with me cause the other boxes that I visited that
day weren't large enough to house the poor critter but I did promise
him a trip to CT after the storm and he's really happy about that.
Prairie Girl
