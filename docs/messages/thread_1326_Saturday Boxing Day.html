Saturday Boxing Day

From: maydaylillies | Date: 2004-09-26 01:23:24 UTC
OK so today we decided to head out boxing with Stacy's mom.

We started off looking for the Pheonex Tree but the clues were too
confusing and we couldn't find the turn off so we gave up.

Instead we continued on and found the Melanie (friend) box at the MDC
reservoir in West Hartford. But was soaked so we dried it out the
best we could put new bags on everything and replanted it.

From there we headed over to Canton. We visited Mill's Pond Park and
first found the Runaway Soccer Ball. We got kinda confused because if
you count from the ditch there was another sitting tree about 11 paces
from a dead tree...but we eventually found it. Stupid Ivan soaked
this box as well. But once again we dried everything out and replaced
the bags. However the ink pad was moldy so we had to throw that out
as well, so be aware that there is no more ink pad in this box.

While we were there we also looked for the artistic dog poo. It was
kinda a cool sculpture. We interprated it as being a worm that the
fish above it would eat, who would in turn be eaten by the birds above
that, but not sure. Anyway not trouble finding the box and it was in
great condition, no repairs needed.

Finally we decided to look for "think outside the box" on talcott
mountain in Simsbury. We always love that walk, done it a million
times but never knew about these side trails that they were on. Found
the box in great condition. Just changed the outter bag since it was
starting to get lots of holes in it. However we were surprised that
it appeared as if no one had logged in to the book in about 2 months.

All in all a pretty successful day. Now its time to rest up so we
have a lot of energy to letterbox in mansfield all next month.
