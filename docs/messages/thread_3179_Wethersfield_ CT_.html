Rubaduc,
You are correct, it is a Christi production. I corresponded with
her via e-mail a couple of times during the spring about her
letterbox, and she graciously gave me the clues. Thanx Christi!
(That was about the time I was planning to plant my "Red Onion" 'box
but had just learned of her original plant--it was mentioned in
another 'box's active, published clues.)
I posted here or on the LbNA talklist asking if anyone knew the
placer of the original Red Onion LB, and thereafter she contacted
me. I was hesitant to duplicate someone else's 'box but she told me
to plant. (I still think her stamp is better than mine. And mine
would have made a great hitchhiker starting out from her 'box! :p)
The long and short of it is that she has not had the time to check
on the 'box or repost the clues...and it is a beautiful stamp, too.
