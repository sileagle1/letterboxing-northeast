The Road to the World Series HHs

From: May Day Lillies | Date: 2005-01-17 01:32:26 UTC
The Road to the World Series is a set of two hitchhikers, one for
the Yankees and one for the Red Sox. The goal is for each of these
two HHs to get a higher score than the other. Both HHs will be
retired when the Yankees and Red Sox open the season against each
other on April third. One point will be awarded to each HH for
every letterboxer, letterbox and state for which it visits. However
we want to keep these HHs in Yankees vs. Red Sox territory so we ask
that you please do not release it anywhere outside of New England or
NY. In addition an extra 5 points will be awarded to each HH if
they are able to obtain a stamp from all 6 New England states and
NY. An additional extra point will also be awarded if the HH
reaches the opposition's city. If you come across either of these
HHs please email us at letterboxingct@... with an update.
In addition please email us if you have this HH come April 3rd so we
can make arrangements to get it back from you. Thanks and good luck
to both teams *wink wink* (GO YANKEES!!!!!)
