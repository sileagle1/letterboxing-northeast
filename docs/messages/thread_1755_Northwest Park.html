Notice to CT boxers.
The series called Plant an Herb Garden at Northwest Park in Gramby will
be pulled by the end of the month. I have been informed that the area
where the Series is located is to be enlarged and much cutting of the
brush has already begun. The Dill Box has been cut in half by a machine
already, by accident. The series will be re planted later in the
spring . Thank you to all who have hunted these boxes. It is very
enjoyable to read the Dill Log book even though it has been chopped in
half. The Voyagers handed the log book to me as we met on the trail
yesterday. Thanks Bob and Caroline
Notice will be posted on the clue page soon.
happy boxing, and there are many more letterboxes in this park. Go get
