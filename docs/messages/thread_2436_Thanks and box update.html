More thanks and praise for the Flamingo Caper ... Lazy Letterboxer,
you're my hero!

Pezpam just posted about the boxes along the Waushaum Ponds Rail
Trail, all of which I found with Brandy You're a Fine Girl, Tony &
Veronica, and the Pointer Party ... that little 3-mile round trip took
us more than a couple of hours and it was well after dark before we
finally claimed the last box. As reported, all were in fine shape,
including Bill & Craig's Waushaum Ponds Rail Trail box, also on the
same trail.

Also happy to report the launch of It's Tiki Time HH by the Pointer
Party in one of the boxes along that trail ... presumably on it's way
to another box now!

All-in-all, I recorded 60 finds -- including 31 Caper boxes, 26 other
published boxes and hitchhikers, 3 mystery boxes, and 1 personal
traveller. A single day record that launched my PFX count over 500
and placed me within 69 boxes of F500 ... a mark I intend to reach by
October's end!

Thanks again to all ... especially the event's organizers and the five
'boxers who stuck with me on our hunt in Sterling!
