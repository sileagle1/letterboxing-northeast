From: greenthumbnh | Date: 2006-03-20 17:16:24 UTC
I wanted to share what with you all about the return vistor that paid
us a visit last night, he woke up for the season .Got my heart racing!!

I am glad that I wasn't out in the woods!

We had a vistor at 7:30. Quite large vistor, Don't know if it is male
or female, but it was here for awhile. I was taking pictures right out
of the back door, between the slats of blinds. He / she looked right
at me a few times. Then it tried to climb the tree out by the red
feeder. OH MY! ! It was awesome. Joe walked in the room and stood at
the window while I kept Lady quiet.

We were just talking about this subject. Tomorrow the feeders come
down.. It took my finch bag of feed and laid down over by the trash
barrels. I took pictures with Joe's camera, I hope they come out.


Talk Soon Gail
