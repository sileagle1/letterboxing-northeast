F1000! And a Mansfield report

From: davyschris@aol.com | Date: 2005-08-25 00:08:47 UTC
Well, I did it! My goal was to hit F-1000 before I have to go back to work.
I go back tomorrow, so I knew it had to be accomplished today!

I was in Mansfield (what better place to pump your F count?) and I wanted to
report on what I found/didn't find. If anyone has any information on boxes I
didn't find, please let me know. I don't know if I just had old clues and
some might be retired, or were known to have been missing for a long time, etc.
And there's always the possibility that I was just looking in the wrong spot.

Couldn't find:
Three Wizards #3
Merrow Park
Friendly Skies
Thank You
Chico y Frio Hermano
The Quest #4 (the knight)
Fuzzy Wuzzy of Puddin Lane
The Peep Study
Sit and Relax
Eagleville Dam
Last Day
Kettle Hole Trail #3, 4, 9, 11, 12, 16

Alive and well:
Three Wizards #1 and 2
The Legend of Puddin Lane
Going to the Chapel (the groom was my 1000!)
Kettle Hole Trail #2, 5, 6, 7, 8, 10, 13, 14, 15, 17
The School House
Mansfield General Store (had this already, but since I stopped in for lunch I
looked at it again)
Rub a Dub Dub
BIMP
Got Milk?
In Search of Spring Heeled Jack
Mystical Legends

Kettle Hole Trail #1 is there, but there is no stamp in the box. The sign
for #6 is missing, but the post is there. The box is fine. It's fairly obvious
even without the little sign where the #6 should be. Ditto for the #12, but
I still couldn't find the box. :/

And that's the end of my boxious summer. Tomorrow it's back to work... and
I'm already planning on going out again after school! :)

Chrissy
aka Brandy, You're a Fine Girl
