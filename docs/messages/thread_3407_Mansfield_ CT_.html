Rubaduc and Gizz found "Going to the Chapel" this afternoon. We were
the first to find them since Sept. of 2005....the clue says they were
going to be pulled in Aug. of 2005.
The groom is doing very well but the poor bride is a bit worse for
wear. No log book, the box was a frozen lump and she was incased in a
sheet of ice. We carefully thawed her out enough to stamp in but didn't
have a spare log book with us to leave. She's safely back in the wall
in a new baggie for those who want to find her, some of the clues have
disappeared but a bit of sleuthing will do the trick.
Rubaduc
