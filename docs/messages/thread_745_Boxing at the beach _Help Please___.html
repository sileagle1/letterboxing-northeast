From: btoesatbarker | Date: 2004-05-28 11:14:55 UTC
Hi, last September I did some boxes on the Cape. I did four boxes
along the canal - Cape Cod Canal Railroad Bridge, Belmont Rock, The
Herring Run and Bourne Bridge. I also did The Gateway to the Cape Cod
Canal and The Anchorage in Sandwich and The Glassworks in Sagamore.
All of these boxes were relatively close together. Secrets of the
Knob and Nobsque Light in Woods Hole are two others that are close.
I could only find the first box of Andrew's 10th Birthday in Mashpee.
I hope this gives you some help and some ideas. I can't give you any
ideas of where to stay since we always stay in Falmouth Heights. Good
luck and happy hunting! Queen B

Just to warn you, the traffic is going to be incredible probably and
you'll have to probably pay for parking at the beaches.
That being said, I'm partial to the Dennis/Harwich area, which is
about 1 1/2 hours from Boston and is right in the middle of the lower
cape.
Good boxes to get:
Any box by The HeadLess Hunter (Scargo Tower,Black Crow, Ghost Train,
Forgotten Road)
Reunion
Chatham Rooster 2
Flax Pond series
