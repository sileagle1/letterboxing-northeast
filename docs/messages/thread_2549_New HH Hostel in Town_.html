New HH Hostel in Town!

From: oldhounder | Date: 2005-11-07 20:43:10 UTC
"Picking Apples with Sarah" is a new hitch-hiker hostel located
somewhere in MA. Some folks don't realize that a HH hostel can be
treated just like any letterbox. It is just that the box is seeded
with two hitch-hikers and therefore should always have at least two in
it. The clue list is a little unusual. Check it out on either LbNA or
Atlasquest.

The Lazy Letterboxer and her consort, The Letterboxing Ham, along with
their buddies Matt and David, known as Nick and Nora Charles when it
comes to Pink Flamingoes, were first finders. Congratulations.

oldhounder
