Weekend Report

From: dave & diane | Date: 2004-04-26 04:06:32 UTC
Diane and I did a little boxing this weekend. Here's what we found:

Rhode Island:

Trish K does nice letterboxes in great places! Dingoes at Dinnertime is still there in good shape, as are the Dolphins at Daybreak and its Epilogue.

NCBC is still trooping along in North Kingstown


We wanted to reach F150 this weekend, since our One Year Letterboxing Anniversary is coming up, so naturally we went to -

Connecticut:

Started the Jumble Series, and got as far as Box #8 before the rain and SLEET hit us. What's up with that?!? We'll have to return to buy a vowel. Neat series, and in good condition. That's a WHOLE lot more than I can say for the road leading in! If/when you go, note the sign that says "Road not maintained". They are very truthful people. My little Honda was - erm - challenged.

We met a gang of assorted adults and children on the trail. It was their second day of letterboxing! They are currently in committee over a name. 'Army of Confusion' was mentioned. We also ran into Jumping Mouse and Lightning on the trail - a neat run-in with friends from the Winter Gathering in Westborough, MA. They aren't online, so we gave them the info for the May Day gathering. Sadly, we're working that day.

We also sometime today found the Connecticut Mystery Wolf LB. I bumped my bald head on a ceiling, so I need replacing. The box is fine, though.
