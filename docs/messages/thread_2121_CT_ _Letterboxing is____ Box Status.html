From: T Prindle | Date: 2005-05-31 12:19:48 UTC
The Dawnlanders and Leaf Lady had great time doing this new series in
Goshen on Memorial Day. The weather was beautiful and the waterfall
area at the end was gorgeously lit. We had a great deal of trouble
finding box number 9 ... and after a half hour's search of the area we
were just about to give up, when I spotted a bit of pink gleaming from
the leaf litter!

The container had been crunched open and the poor stamp showed
thousands of teeny tiny chew marks, leaving for a rather interesting
micro-tooth-carved stamp impression! Not knowing better to do, we
placed the stamp and remains of the container into a plastic bag and re-
hid it according to the clues, with the addition of softball sized rock
on top of it to inhibit the critter from absconding with it again.
We're afraid the container needs to be replaced, but we were thankful
the stamp was found! It might be good to weight down the other
containers.

Thank you for a wonderful and delighfully amusing series!
