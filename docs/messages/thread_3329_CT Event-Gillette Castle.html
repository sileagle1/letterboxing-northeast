CT Event-Gillette Castle

From: swampyankeesunshine | Date: 2006-11-18 20:50:43 UTC
Hello fellow boxers. The DEP launched a "No child left inside"
program this summer featuring a different State Park each weekend
offering activities and prizes to participating families. It was so
well accepted, that they are doing another one at Gillette Castle,
Dec. 2&3, from 10 a.m.-3 p.m. They have asked us to "man" a table
highlighting Letterboxing. They are supplying paper, stamps, and ink
to interested people. They have an event stamp,also. We are planting 2
new boxes. Anyone wishing to stop by and check the activities out,
and maybe lend a hand, would be greatly welcomed. It will be a great
day for kids of ALL ages, with lots to do. Hope to see you there!
Sunshine, Swamp Yankee, Hez and Grumpy
