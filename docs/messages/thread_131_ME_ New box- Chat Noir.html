Chat Noir

Easy Box. Handcarved stamp.

Located at the Robert B Tristam Coffin Wild Flower Sanctuary.

From Route 1 in Woolwich, take Route 127 North. Turn left onto Route
128 and drive for 5 miles to Chopps Point Road on your left. You can
park in the area to the right of the intersection.

Start the trail at the right hand corner of the parking lot. As you
walk you will pass a stone wall on your right and walk between big
boulders on your right and left.

Continue on the trail until you see a tree with a blue blaze on your
left and a large mossy rock on your right. Just ahead you will see a
tree on your right with a New England Wildflower Society medallion on it.

Look left. Do you see the large boulder atop a small hill? Carefully
climb the hill and explore the Boulder. Chat Noir is hidden in a
crevice, carefully covered with leaves and twigs. Please rehide
carefully and well!

You may continue on the trail and loop back to the lot, or turn and
walk back to your car.
