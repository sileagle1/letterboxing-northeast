From: zess devine | Date: 2007-08-15 13:08:32 UTC
Hi donutz716,

Have you tried using the AtlasQuest Trip planner? I've used it a few
times for drives to and from Ohio, and it's pretty cool. From the main
page, move your mouse over to the "Letterboxes" tab along the top, and
the fourth option down is Trip Planner. You choose the interstate,
then the starting & ending points, and how far off the interstate you
want to limit your driving (e.g., 5 miles, 10 miles, etc.).

Have a great trip!!

~zess~
