Chatfield Hollow Gather

From: Debby | Date: 2004-11-07 20:08:00 UTC
Hiker Lou, Butterfly and I were out on this gorgeous day to
secure our gather plans and finish planting.

The gather time is 10-4 at Chatfield Hollow State Park on Route
80 in Killingworth, CT. The raindate is Sunday.

You will need to park outside the gate and make the short walk
to the pavilion on your left. Please bring a food item to share and
a log for our campfire! Lou is making chowder, I will have a beef
stew warming on the camp stove.

You will want to wear some orange if you do any of the boxes that
are in the forest (green area on Chatfield DEP map), since there
is hunting in there except on Sunday.

Bring your Killingworth clues! There will be more posted this
week by us and others. There will be at least 50 boxes in the
park, at least 6 just for the day, plus an event stamp. I have heard
from Dr. Hawley Griffen that he will be there. To get his personal
stamp, ask the right person (a different person than at the
mystery gather!).

We look forward to seeing our letterboxing friends!
