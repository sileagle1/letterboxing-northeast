Sudbury Valley Trustees offering letterboxing prize

From: cpascott | Date: 2006-03-27 15:12:11 UTC
As many Massachusetts and other area boxers may know, Sudbury Valley
Trustees (SVT), a land trust that owns and protects conservation land
along the Sudbury, Assabet and Concrd Rivers, actively promotes public
use of their land and has placed "official" SVT letterboxes on its
property.

They are now offering a prize (they don't say what) for finding at
least half of the 10 official SVT boxes placed on their property.

They call it the "Letterboxing Challenge" and it will run through
August 31 of this year. Simply find the boxes, collect one of the
green SVT medallions inside and, when you have 5, go to the SVT office
in Sudbury to collect your prize.

More information at http://www.sudburyvalleytrustees.org/page.php?id=120

Figures that I've already found many of the SVT boxes, but there are
still a few to go. Just went after "See-Der Pine Cones" yesterday,
and once again I can highly recommend the SVT boxes ... they are all
EXCELLENT hikes and great finds!!
