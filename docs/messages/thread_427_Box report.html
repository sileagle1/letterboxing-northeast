From: cavy_lovers4 | Date: 2004-03-26 21:07:50 UTC
I took advantage of this beautiful day to get a new box and check on
2 of mine.

I visited Jay and Amy's "Happy Birthday, Baby! Letterbox" in
Walpole. Lovely stamp located among some impressive, grand beech
trees. I enjoyed the weather while sitting on the "foot" of the tree
to stamp in. Do be careful in searching for the right "crotch of the
tree" though - the first one I checked [not the correct one] was
full of beer bottle pieces. I tried to clean them all out, but I
can't guarantee there is no glass left.
I also added a bag around the box, as there was a little moisture in
the box - no damage to the book or stamp, but the stamp pad seemed a
little watery. You might want to use your own as I did.

Next, spring weather having inspired my whimsy, I put a new stamp in
the Duct Tape and Whimsy box in Canton. While there I saw
butterflies!

Finally, I stopped at my Fly like an Eagle box in Canton. I TOOK
THIS HOME FOR MAINTENANCE - it will be returned soon. This park is
lovely in the spring and I saw more butterflies there.

Can't believe that only one week ago I was trudging through snow to
do box maintenance! Hope this weather lasts!
