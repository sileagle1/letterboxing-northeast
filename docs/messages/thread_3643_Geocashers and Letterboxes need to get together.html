Re: Geocashers and Letterboxes need to get together

From: JPS jps | Date: 2007-09-25 21:29:19 UTC
This is great advise. I have placed a number of boxes
and always depend on my experience as a geo cacher to
think of possible problems. Alot of boxers know what a
geocache is, or at least its not a box. Newer geo
cachers can get confused and make off with stamps.

I went to find a geo cache yesterday and thought
about how great a spot it was to put a box. The GPS
unit had me going around so much that I knew that no
box was safe by this hill side. Common sense should
usually rule. I will most likely go to the other side
of the trail and say turn around and take a look at
the hill.

All letterbox placers need to be aware of geocaches.
Its that simple and complex.
