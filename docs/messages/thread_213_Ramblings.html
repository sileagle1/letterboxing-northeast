On the subject of event stamps, be creative and do something
clever with the stamp. Don't worry about public opinion.
Brian carved a wonderful stamp, which took him many hours
and brought it to a gathering where many people stamped the
image into their books. Later we took the stamp and hid it
as a mystery box. I am aware that one or two people found
it and were not happy that they had already had the image,
but that doesn't affect us. There are others who have found
it that praised the clues and the location as well as the stamp.
We are proud of that box. You should be proud of your creation and
do with it as you see fit.

And on the subject of winter boxing, we attempted to find 6 boxes
on Saturday, only to find 2. However, I'm delighted that we were
motivated to get out there and not give in to the temptation to
pull the covers over our head and hibernate all weekend. Anybody else
get out there this weekend?
