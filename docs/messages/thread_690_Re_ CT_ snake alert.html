Please, try not to be alarmed if you happen upon a snake. Be
respectful. After all, we are encroaching on their territory.
Snakes are not agressive. They will escape as quickly as they can, if
surprised in their natural habitat. This desire to escape applies to
the huge but harmless black snake (racer)referred to in
Sunshine/Swamp Yankee's post as well as the 2 types of poisonous
snakes that live in CT. (Copperhead and Timber Rattlesnake) They are
easily identified by the diamond shape of their heads. And they will
not strike unless cornered.
