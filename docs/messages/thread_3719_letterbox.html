Hi, migdalia, and welcome to letterboxing! I can't tell you how many people
discover our little pastime accidentally like this.

What I suggest is first that you get yourself a personal rubber stamp and
stamp in to the logbook and leave a little note. If the logbook contains the
email address of the person who planted it, you can safely send them a note
telling them you've found it.

Then, if you can, try and re-hide the letterbox exactly as you found it.

Thanks for caring for this little treasure! Jay in CT
