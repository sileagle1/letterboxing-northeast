After visiting the Cape yesterday, I've decided to pull my Beach Plum letterbox for the
Winter. First Encounter Beach could'nt even be driven into due to a massive puddle/lake!
I'll be placing it back in the Spring with maybe some revised clues. It seems that the spot I
picked is a favorite teenage drinking spot. It's such a great spot though...

The funny thing is, it had been hidden under a pile of logs. The kids used the wood to
build a campfire and obviously found my box. But one young man named Jon adopted the
box as his person diary and made such entries as " although I'm an infidel I will keep this
Beach Plum safe" then he would make entries about the weather etc. I kind of hated taking
his blog away. But I may have to put Beach Plum in a different spot because other kids may
not be as protective. Thanks Jon for keeping our Mother's letterbox safe!
