found an unknown box in holyoke

From: Kim | Date: 2005-08-29 12:17:20 UTC
While in Holyoke on the Refuge Brook trail, and after I found the
correct Refuge Brook box, I came across another LB lying open in the
trail located near some large square concrete blocks. The spiral
notepad was soaked and on the ground, the small stamp (a rough carving
of a bird) was still in the box, swimming in a pool of water. That was
it.. no baggies, pencils, ink or anything else lying around that I
could identify. I took it home and after the notebook dried out I was
able to peel apart the pages. From what I could see from the orange
blur.. the notepad was stamped/signed only once. I think the date was
May 25th? and it said the Donahue Group. I'm thinkng this may be the
planter because there didn't seem to be any LB title page. I'm also
thinking this box is/was not listed anywhere? Does anyone know anything
about it or who owns it? K*
