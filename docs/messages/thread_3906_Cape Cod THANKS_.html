Cape Cod THANKS!

From: Wisconsin Hiker | Date: 2008-09-22 02:52:56 UTC
We were very happy to have the opportunity to meet several of the Cape
Cod letterboxers today! Many thanks to Chunna, who organized the get-
together. We were able to meet Chunna, the esteemed Archimedes Screw,
Cape Cod Lightning Bug, Cape Cod Letterboxer, Mumma (of Mumma & Bunny
Boy), Samoset 3, churchguysNprincesses, and Sapphire Berry. We
appreciated the chance to talk with the local boxers, and also find a
few new boxes in the area.

Unfortunately I only took a few photos, but have posted the 3 I do have
in the Photos section:

http://ph.groups.yahoo.com/group/LbNE/photos/browse/e8cf


The weather has been great for the first 2 days of our visit and it
looks good for most of the week. If anyone else will be visiting Cape
Cod between now & Sunday SEP 28, shoot us an email and perhaps we can
get together for some boxing and/or a meal.

Wisconsin Hiker & Matini Man
