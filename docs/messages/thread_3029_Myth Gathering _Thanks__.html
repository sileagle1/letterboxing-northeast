Myth Gathering "Thanks!"

From: YahYah | Date: 2006-06-25 22:44:24 UTC
Just got done unpacking from the Greek Myth Gathering weekend camping
trip... WOW... What a fantastically amazing - incredibly... !awesome!
gathering!

We arrived at the park first, were a little worried, lol... we didn't
know the area, didn't know where to camp out... it had poured down on
us, torrentially, on our way through New Hampshire (cars slowed down
in the 55/65 mile an hour zone to ... 30... and 25... with caution
lights flashing ... it was just miserable, and confidence
shaking...) . Thank goodness we pulled the truck over right before
it slammed us. Absolutely take that to mean "right before it slammed
us"... we no sooner had taken a few drenchable items out of the back
of the truck before we drove down the road, literally, 30 seconds,
and the sky opened up like you wouldn't believe.

We trudged on.... drove through 2 of these rainfalls, and just kept
on-a-cruisin... NOTHING was going to stop us from making this
weekend. If we were going to be boxing in the rain? We knew we
wouldn't be the only ones! =:D That's the great thing about die
hard boxers, lol, you just *know* you're not the only one doing
this "Crazy...what the heck am I doing? Am I Crazy? I *must* be
crazy!" letterboxing adventure - heck no. =:D

We weren't at the tent site more than 15 minutes when The Wildcats
showed up, and about 20 minutes after that SagaCorn. We had a
wonderful pre-event camping night, and a great time boxing together.

SapphireBerry and WoodSprite - you really outdid yourselves. This
event was everything we imagined and about a hundred things more. It
poured on Friday night, but we were all nice and cozy warm (and dry)
under a screen tent, we all had tarps to keep our tents bone dry...
it was an adventure for the books! And the boxing day itself? A
little rain in the morning? Pffff, that was nothin'. This event was
bigger (HUGER!) than the rain - without a doubt!

The icing on the cake (besides the views from Mt. Olympus) was the
night boxing. What - a - !RIOT! I heard someone say, about the
night time boxing and the box hidden in the porta-potty, lol, "ohhhh
Ms.SapphireBerry, what a sense of humor you do have...." lol.. and I
laughed when I thought about SapphireBerry and WoodSprite hanging out
(for a VERY long time, lol) in the men's room Porta-Potty just
waiting for some desperately gullible letterbox seeking junky
(namely, my husband) to open the door only to have you two Jump out
of the darkness screaming - scaring the pants off the poor guy (if I
paid ya, would you do it again? lololol!).

MonoLinguaPuss was the only "kid" there after dinner, but man did he
have a blast riding his bike around the park boxing with people to
locate the easy and moderately difficult boxes with his father's
trusty headlamp. He even found 2 boxes, in the dark, that we hadn't
yet looked for! (he loved the glow sticks for the 12 night boxes SB).

Boxing from dawn til dusk and into the night. Now *that's* what I
call !boxing! We all went to bed utterly exhausted last night and
when MLP has to be woken up in the morning because he's still so
tired, lol... you *know* you've put on a great event... =:D

We thank you, from the bottom of our hearts, for this wonderful
experience, and we thank everyone who was there that we met, for
being such wonderful people and allowing us to share this experience
with you. (We were a little worried no one would show up with the
rain being as bad as it was before, around, noon time).

YahYah of "The Pointed Thinkers"
