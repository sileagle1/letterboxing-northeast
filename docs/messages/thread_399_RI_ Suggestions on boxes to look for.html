From: Janet | Date: 2004-03-26 17:37:38 UTC
Hey if you want to hook up to go letterboxin while you are here let
me know.

My recent favorite series in RI is Alice in Wonderland (in Washington
County.) The stamps are lovely hand carved stamps. The trail is one
of my favorites in RI too. A couple of the boxes may give you some
trouble finding (The land marks are not real clear) but worth the
drive from Bristol. It is best to take two cars though so you can
just walk one way. If you do go out to Washington County there are
lots of other boxes in the general area too. I could set up an
iterary for you that would keep you boxing for weeks LOL.
