field of dreams in Salem NH

From: Michelle Lee | Date: 2006-05-22 13:09:21 UTC
For those of you who have visited the extremely soggy Field of Dreams letterbox in Salem,
NH. I spoke to the girl scout council around here (which I am a member of) and found the
troop disbanded quite a while ago and there was no way to reach the original members. I
adopted this box over the box in the winter and finally back went up and replaced the entire
box. I didn't want the you folks to get a poor impression of girl scout letterboxers :) (FYI
there wasn't a box- just a baggy with a stamp and inkpad..hence all the water) There is now
a hand carved stamp and a dry log book. and a lock and lock box. So, if anyone wants to get
a new impression for that location - it is up and running! I know several of you visited there-
but where underwhelmed by the stamp and condition of the box. You should be much
happier with it now. My daughter will be up in camp in that area this summer - so if there
are any problems with it - please tell me as I will be around there a few times to pick her up
and drop her off.

regards,

oldpatterns
