Thanks to the RSVP feature on Atlas Quest, we now have over 40 people
signed up for "Boxing by the Bay" on Sunday, Sep. 4th! To RSVP please
go to this page: http://www.atlasquest.com/events/event.html?gEventId=62

Just a few reminders about the gathering for those who might be
traveling from far away.

Please bring a picnic (brown bag) lunch for yourself. There are many
nearby lunch spots and a local natural foods Co-op, but there are no
cooking facilities or refrigeration in the park. Bring a folding camp
chair if you have one. There will be picnic tables at the park.

If you're still waiting to book a camp spot or reservation for that
weekend, I would suggest that you do that quickly. We have a list of
suggested spots for folks on our event page listing:
http://home.gwi.net/~moxie1/Clues/belfast.html

Please email Phyto or Isosceles if you have further questions!
See you there ~
