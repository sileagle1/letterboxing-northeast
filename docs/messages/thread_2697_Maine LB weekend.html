From: Kim | Date: 2006-02-12 15:26:15 UTC
Hey Maine boxers, I have some good news for you(I think), especially
since I noticed recently some concern expressed by Phyto. I just
returned from spending a "girls' reunion weekend" with several my
family members in Bangor. Boy that place has changed alot over the
years...but I digress. We were looking for some things to do as a
group during Saturday, and rather than shopping, movies, etc.. I
suggested going to find a nearby letterbox. Just one. So five of us
went while two chose to stay back, still needing recovery time from
the previous evenings' festivities. We printed up the clues,(all of
the ones from Bangor area) then we went to do the Thomas Standpipe.
They're hooked. I couldn't make them stop. We skipped lunch and kept
going. Eventually I convinced them that we had to get back home to
the others. We did 5 boxes (I believe they were all Phyto's boxes and
they asked me to tell you kudos) They can't wait to make their own
stamps and logbooks and to get out and do more. They're going to get
husbands involved,their kids.(all grown up geez)..etc... well some of
them will take to it, some won't. You know how it is. But we're from
all over the area. Carmel, Hermon,
Glendale,Belfast,Orrington,Bucksport and Corinth, just to name a few.
So anyways, I wanted to give you a heads up... They are already
thinking of cool places to plant a few for you too! Well, now that
the snow is coming, you may need to wait a little... but anyways. We
had a great weekend. We're planning another similar event for the
summer in southern New England. That's the news.
See you on the trail. K*
