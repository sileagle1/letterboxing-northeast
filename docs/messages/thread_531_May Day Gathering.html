From: MommaFox | Date: 2005-05-03 01:43:55 UTC
Wow! I think I'm rested now!

I am SO glad everyone had such a great time, and so happy to hear that everyone liked the scavenger hunt idea! I was a little worried when the rain didn't seem to want to stop Sunday morning, but I guess I shouldn't have been!

I really want to thank some people too; Teach and Preach (Robin & Dan) especially. When I asked them to help me I promised them to have all the stamps carved and boxes ready to hide many weeks in advance. Well, we all know how life can get in the way! I didn't have the boxes ready until Monday night! They were still willing to help!! So a BIG thankyou to you guys for sticking with me.

Another thank you goes to Sue (Sojourner) for all the online support she gave. She was the one who convinced me to rethink the whole thing when I cancelled a few weeks ago. She emailed me often to see how things were progressing and "listened" when things weren't going quite the way I had planned. So, thank you Sue!

Mark Pepe to the rescue!! The bad thing about a free website.......you can't have hundreds of people all trying to get information and clues all at once, it'll shut the site down!! Mark was so kind to put all the info on his wonderful site and saved the day! Thank you Mark!!

Thanks to Sunshine and Swamp Yankee for the truckload of firewood! And also to all the others who donated wood. Unfortunately Bald Eagle is not good with names and couldn't tell me who you all were!

Thanks to Mary from the Maplines for helping with cleanup!

And most of all, thanks to my husband, Bald Eagle, for sticking with me and telling me to go ahead and plan this thing even though he felt I had too much on my plate already!

Speaking of Bald Eagle, he would like to thank Mark, Brian and he thinks Grumpy (?) for thier support with that little incident!

I can't believe how many of you are already talking about next year! There will have to be a slight change in dates if I do this next year. My son is getting married April 29th so it'll be after that. Dont' you worry, I'm already thinking! But, Brian........I don't know about 40-50 of them!

Oh, sorry Seth about the hotdogs!


Everybody rest up the weekends looking good so far!
