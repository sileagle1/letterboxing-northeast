They have some great classes we would take as a group.

It will be two classes back to back for a total
of two hours. Here are the classes:

Narragansett Bay Critter Diversity and their Habitats
Students will explore biodiversity in Narragansett Bay by meeting our
animals up close and personal in the habitat exhibits they call home. We
have over 150 species of animals found in and around Narragansett Bay.
Topics to be explored are: diversity, habitats, and adaptations.

Sandy Beach Exploration
Explore the sandy beach shoreline and see what the tide has left behind.
Learn why so many animals and plants call these areas home and see how these
habitats serve as nursery areas, environmental buffers and feeding grounds.
