MA: Boxing in Chelmsford

From: Jude Griffin | Date: 2005-04-13 11:52:33 UTC
After school, Team Freckles shot up to Chelsmford, northwesterly of
Boston, so that the shortest team member could garner a first finder
certificate for logbook.

It was a beautiful afternoon, and this new box was in a lovely
out-of-the-way park. Following the clues, we wandered down the dirt
and then gravel paths, found the birch tree nearly in the river, and
discovered Beehives and Bumblebees. We took the box down the path
aways so we could open and stamp in at leisure far distant from the
hiding spot. The sun was bright, the river beautiful .. the older team
member was yearning for a nap.

No first finder certificate, but a new hitchhiker being launched was
discovered. We will make sure this HH hops into another box this
weekend.

Before racing home to beat the worst of the afternoon commuter
traffic, we spent an hour looking for Deep Brook and Dragonfly. Deep
Brook was just placed this weekend, and Dragonfly was just confirmed
as in place last weekend, so we were obviously just interpreting the
clues incorrectly.

Lovely reservation, once you can find the sign at the entrance. Very
quiet under the pine trees, no one else there. Saw a woodpecker
hopping up a tree.

cos I don't see any down feathers.
