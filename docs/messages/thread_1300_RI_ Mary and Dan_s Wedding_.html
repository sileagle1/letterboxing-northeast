RI: Mary and Dan's Wedding!

From: mindizney@aol.com | Date: 2004-09-19 11:39:44 UTC
Yesterday, I had the privilege of stamping into Mary and Dan's Wedding Event
Box in North Scituate, RI, which was placed as a one day only box in honor of
their wedding! Their bridesmaid Warrior Woman was thoughtful to put out the
box. Though it was pouring rain, both Moonstone and I found it successfully!
We then headed out to do other boxes, most of them library ones while the rain
continued to pour. By 3:00 p.m. the rain had almost stopped, which I was
thankful since Mary and Dan's wedding was starting in North Scituate! After
finishing up our wet boxing adventure, I decided to drive through North Scituate
on the way home, and what do I see?????? Mary coming out of the church in her
gown after the ceremony! She looked absolutely gorgeous!!!!!!! What a
special treat!!!!!!

Congratulations Mary and Dan! I hope you have many wonderful years
together!!!!!

Music Woman
