Many thanks to those who have been in touch with me regarding this
box. It appears that it didn't actually "go missing" as I had

location since I don't want to be responsible for anyone getting
poison ivy!! I will also do my best to get a new log book down there
as soon as possible. I can't tell you how happy I am to know it is
there, as it is one of my favorite stamps!

Happy hunting all!
