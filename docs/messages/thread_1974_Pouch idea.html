Pouch idea

From: swampyankeesunshine | Date: 2005-04-15 00:06:16 UTC
While boxing this weekend I found myself carrying my little Tupperware
sandwich container that holds our personal stamps and Log, in my hand
as we hiked mile after mile. With my new walking stick, made for me
by Grumpy, in one hand, as well as clues, it's a bit cumbersome but
it's a bit of a pain to retrieve it from my backback at each and every
box. Soooo, I went to North Cove Outfitters in Saybrook today in
search of a pouch (just the right size) to fit on my backpack's
waistband, and Voila!!!!! I found just the right thing in a kid's bike
bag. It's complete with adjustable straps that fit very nicely on the
waistband in front where I can reach it. It also has a zipper on the
top for easy access. I'm so excited!!!! Just HAD to share--
Sunshine :0)

Re: [LbNE] Pouch idea

From: Donna Magner | Date: 2005-04-15 00:34:30 UTC
Sunshine,

It's really cool when you find something that works better for you while LBing. I carry a lot in my backpack and every time I had to put it on, take it off while stamping in - made my back ache. I notice while hiking with B'fly that she had a nice shoulder bag that holds her stamping stuff. I went to the army navy store and found a canvas shoulder bag that fits everything so nicely and makes it much easier, like your pouch. I still wear my backpack, but my stamping supplies are in the shoulder bag. My back is better and the stamping in goes much faster. Thanks for sharing. Maybe someone will benefit from it!

Donutz716
