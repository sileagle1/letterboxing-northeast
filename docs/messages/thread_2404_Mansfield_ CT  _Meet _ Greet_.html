Mansfield, CT "Meet & Greet"

From: sue_sojourner | Date: 2005-09-26 11:22:41 UTC
Beedle from Maine, phynstar from NY and sojourner from CT will be
spending Columbus Day weekend letterboxing in the Mansfield area.
This will be Beedle's first experience with CT 'boxing, and what
better introduction than in Mansfield!!

If you're planning on being in that area on Sunday, October 9, bring
your lunch and join us for a short "Meet & Greet" at Mansfield
Hollow State Park!

It's not a gathering, just a low-key, no-frills opportunity to chat,
eat lunch together while getting some exchanges, and enjoy the early
fall weather.

WHERE: Mansfield Hollow State Park picnic grove, Bassetts Bridge
Road, Mansfield, CT. (Off Route 195)
WHEN: Sunday, October 9th
TIME: 12:00 to 1:00pm

From the entrance, drive as far as you can go, past the ball field
on the right and the 2 wooden outhouses, to the picnic grove. You
won't miss us - we'll have a bright orange tablecloth on our picnic
table!

Hope to see you there!
