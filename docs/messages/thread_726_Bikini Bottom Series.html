Re: Bikini Bottom Series

From: btoesatbarker | Date: 2004-05-22 21:27:55 UTC
I guess the second time is the charm, since I went back and found the
boxes today. I want to thank everyone for their help. I actually met
Joe at the park as he was leaving after checking the boxes and he did
tell me that the compass reading for the first box was supposed to
bring you to an old pineapple juice container which held another
clue. That is why the reading was off to get to the box. He also let
me know that for the second clue, the three foot barkless section had
fallen down, and to look for the base - and to mention it in the
first boxes' logbook. Anyway, it ended up being a very successful day
as I got 15 out of 15 boxes. What a great way to spend the day!
Thanks again.
Queen B
