With the rapid rate of development in the northeast, each year many miles of
trails are lost. Along with the loss of outdoor recreation also is the loss
of acres of trees and wildlife habitat. Organizations like the New York/New
Jersey Trail Conference and the Open Space Institute work very hard to
create new outdoor spaces and campaigns to increase community awareness.



On Saturday 3 October 2009, my friend Elizabeth (of Rabbit's Friends and
Relations) and I will be participating in the 2nd Annual People for Trails
Hike-a-thon at Bear Mountain State Park. Funds raised will help with trail
maintenance and the creation of a new Trails for People interpretive exhibit
at the Harriman-Bear Mountain State Park.



We were going to go for the strenuous hike of 9 miles, but remembered back
to last June when we hiked 5 miles in preparation for the Summer is HERE!
Gather and realized we weren't twenty-something anymore and opted for the
moderate 4 mile hike. We would like to suggest a $5 per mile pledge. My
personal goal for this year's Hike-a-thon is $500. Anything you can do (big
or small) to help me reach that goal is greatly appreciated. If your place
of business would like to sponsor us, let me know! Keep your pledge
receipts as your donation is tax deductible.



Please visit my pledge page! http://nynjtc.dojiggy.com/theboxers If you
are unable to pledge online, you can download a printable pledge form here:
http://nynjtc.dojiggy.com/images/uploads/6362/pledge_form_6553.pdf



Starting today, I'll have a pledge PT with me at all times. make a pledge
and no matter where we meet, on or off the trail it's yours! And of course a
box or two will be planted along the way!



Thanks for your support!!!

Talking Turtle
