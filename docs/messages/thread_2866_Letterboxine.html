Letterboxine

From: kathiekelleher | Date: 2006-04-25 19:16:57 UTC
One of the best thing that came out of the Ice Cream Sunday event was the introduction of
a zine dedicated to all things letterboxing called Letterboxine. This is the creation of the
very talented and creative Team Radandalstik.

It's loaded with all kinds of good letterboxing stories, poems, info and my favorite- an
interview with Warrior Woman where she reveals the origin of her trailname. Watch out
nosey RV people, she's armed now (with a signature stamp)!

If you were fortunate enough to get the premier issue at the Event, curl up with your copy
and be amazed. This is just the thing to tide us over from one event to the next and
actually creates a new niche for the written word. I wish Team Randalstik the best of luck
with future issues and hope we get details soon on how to get future issues. Bravo!!

Take care, orion
