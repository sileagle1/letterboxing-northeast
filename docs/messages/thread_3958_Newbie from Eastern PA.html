Welcome to the hobby! Most of the folks in eastern PA tend to hang
out at the Letterboxing Middle Atlantic Board. Here is the link:

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/lbma/

We have 'boxers from VA, MD, DC, PA, NJ and NY mostly (but
distinguished guests from places such as the NE pop up from time to time).

new folks.
