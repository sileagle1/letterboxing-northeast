I found my logbook!

From: mindizney@aol.com | Date: 2004-01-03 13:51:09 UTC
Since the rain was coming down quite heavily here in Manchester, I decided to
get up extra early to head to Windham to find my logbook. It must have
fallen out of my pocket while hiking, since it ended up being near the foundation
closest to Falling Stars. The outer cover was almost soaked through, but
thankfully, the pages were only minimally damp, and all of the stamps inside were
not damaged!

The more expensive logbooks from art supply stores really hold up well during
a night of wet weather!

Thanks to all of you who were going to look for my logbook! I hope you don't
make a trip out to Windham for naught.
