Ashford, Ct. letterboxing Day

From: rospa10 | Date: 2004-08-07 02:20:31 UTC
Isn't this weather glorious? though some of my gardens are choking
with weeds{{cough! cough!)) I escaped for an afternoon of boxing.
First stop: Ashford's Nipmuck Picture This l.b. It sure had me
studying the unique rock formations of our N.E. forests. (Chuck, box
was very wet inside, but not cracked. I dried and rebagged
everything. No harm done.) On to Nipmuck Trail l.b. Very nice hiding
spot, though I kept thinking of the hiker recently bitten by a snake
on Bear Mt. Poking sticks are our friends. Heard, then saw a black
snake on my way out.
On to "Rain/Sleet/Snow"..I thought! that's the trouble with
printing double-sided clue sheets. I meant to get R.S.S. but followed
the clues to :Once Was Mine" by mistake. Thought that 3/4 mile
description was not feeling right.. Nope, did not bring Boston
Hollow clues but do plan on a return this fall if not sooner with
husband and picnic lunch in tow. What a pristine view!
Ran out of time to do any more Ashford boxes today, but am eager to
return for more. I hiked on parts of the Nipmuck Trail I'd never seen
before and it was lovely (and cool).
