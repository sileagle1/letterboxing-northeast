Let's Carve! Event in Western MA

From: Cathy | Date: 2010-06-16 13:17:53 UTC
Come join us at Edwards Library in Southampton MA for a carving workshop. This is a follow-up to our recent mini-meet and letterboxing workshop. We will briefly discuss selecting an image and transferring it to the material. The focus will be on gouge carving with tips & tricks, and a bit about mounting the finished stamp. If you can come please bring some carving material and your knives (we will mostly use #1 tip). Let's carve!
