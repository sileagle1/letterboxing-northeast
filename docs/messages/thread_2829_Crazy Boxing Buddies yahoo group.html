Crazy Boxing Buddies yahoo group

From: mainekokopellian | Date: 2006-04-12 07:46:08 UTC
I truly appreciate that several people have joined our small group to
chat up this hobby & share some adventures...

I would like to clarify that the is a group of friends old & new who
simply love the hobby...Several of us wanted another forum to share
picture clues, special boxes and maybe maybe contribute back to this
wonderful hobby in a small way...I thought a fair criteria for
joining would be to have exchanged or shared a boxing adventure with
the moderator and or others in the group....For Example if you had
attended our Frightnight Gathering, you would have exchanged with
many if not all of us...and you would be in the group....It was fun ,
and harmless little "rule" I guessed...

I have received a couple nastygrams from a couple people inferring I
am an elitist and reek some kind of evil mojo thing...bend on world
domination of the Letterboxing universe from my little corner of Evil
(Maine)....

To that I state LOL loudly....

Anyways I just wanted to share this experience with the community and
those who know me should get a grand ole chuckle out of what I
mention above...
I believe always in the integrity of this hobby and how so many of us
work hard to share & promote this wonderful hobby.

I appreciate & thank you all for allowing me a moment to share this...

Humbly;
Mainekokopellian
