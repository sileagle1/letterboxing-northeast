Discover why leaves change color and take part in a leaf hunt relay,
test your skills at letterboxing and orienteering, sample some CT
grown produce, carve a pumpkin, see some of the horses that help
patrol our CT State Parks and Forests, meet new, and reconnect with
old, friends. Bring a picnic lunch and your hiking shoes. The trail
is relatively flat and excellent for beginner hikers. Bring your
family and friends. Everyone is welcome to come.
