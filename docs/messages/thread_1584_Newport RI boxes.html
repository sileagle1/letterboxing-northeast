From: mindizney@aol.com | Date: 2004-11-12 02:29:22 UTC
PSU Fan and I went out in search of the Newport boxes. We went Land Sailing
first, and believe we were in the correct area, but no box..... Has anyone
found this box lately?

We then went out to Castle Hill and checked on the Castle Hill Light box,
which is in ok shape. I unfortunately forgot a new ink pad for it, but was able
to use the one in the box to stamp into PSU Fan's book. The weather was
beautiful but the wind was really whipping around the point!!!!

Then we decided to hit Fort Adams, and found the correct wall, but no box.
On our way back down the hill, we found a dirty square Rubbermaid lid, which we
think could have been part of the box. We fear that it has been trashed. We
realize that someone may have been there in Oct. 2004 (seeing that there's a
note on the Drew website concerning bringing ziplocks etc...). Has anyone
found it more recently?

At least PSU Fan was 1 for 3 on the day, and I got to do some shopping at
Dansk (they're having a store closing sale, BTW... for those interested) for
Christmas. It's too bad that I didn't get a box too!

Music Woman
