Rhode Island recap

From: fleurdelislb | Date: 2004-04-12 12:13:22 UTC
Back to the grind now for me, but I thoroughly enjoyed my vacation
last week in Rhode Island! I didn't get nearly as many boxes as I'd
hoped to find (I came away with 13 and a hitchhiker), but I have to
keep in mind that my letterboxing companion had bronchitis and wasn't
up to hiking miles and miles each day. We mainly stuck around the
Bristol/Newport/Little Compton area. Many of the hiding spots
showcased gorgeous RI scenery and so many of the stamps were
beautiful!

Thanks to all who offered suggestions about where to box up there and
who helped me out - I greatly appreciate it! I can't wait to return!
