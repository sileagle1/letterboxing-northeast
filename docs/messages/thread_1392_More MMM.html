From: ladylee84@yahoo.com | Date: 2004-10-04 01:07:28 UTC
What a great time I had yesterday. Not only did I get 130+
exchanges (hey it was my first event!) but some finds as well.

But the best part of the day by far was the realization that
letterboxers are from all varying size, shape, color, gender, age,
occupation, location etc. etc. etc. What an all inclusive group of
people! With some hobbies - well lets just say that for some
hobbies you sometimes must have a special skill to fit in. But with
letterboxing, pretty much anyone can do this. Yes some of us are
still befuzzled by those "deep clues" and others will never carve a
stamp that is worth letting the dog chew on. But that doesn't
matter. I have never felt more welcomed in another group before. It
was so nice to finally put a face to those trail names/stamps that I
see floating around on the boxes that I have found or on the
website. Thank you so much for a wonderful day!

Happy boxing!!
