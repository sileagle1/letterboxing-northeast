You are invited to participate in a web-based survey looking at the
profiles and behaviors of individuals who participate in letterboxing.
This research is being conducted as part of a doctoral dissertation
about the activity of letterboxing. Any visitor 18 years of age and
older may complete the survey, and all responses are anonymous. To
voluntarily participate, go to
http://portal.bsu.edu/inqsit/inqsit.cgi/fink and select
`letterboxing'and 'continue'. Use your trail name or a pseudonym to
sign in. For more information about this research project, please
contact the Primary Investigator - Marisa Fink, Ball State University,
AC 414, 2000 W. University Avenue, Muncie, IN 47306, 765.285.1962,
marisafink@.... Thank you for your participation.
