Dear CPAScott,
Music Woman and I found Tea Party two weekends ago. It was
as advertised, but as I was looking in the wall, one person
stopped to tell me (I said I had "dropped something") that rats
live in there! There was a little cairn above it. When you sit there,
you do see the masts and also, there is another box VERY
NEAR. It was also interesting because as we walked to the box, I
saw my former boat (36' Uniflite) sitting there; we sold it years
ago and I was just about to tell Pam, "We used to have a boat
like that," when I realized it WAS ours. Very fun to see it. Contact
me again if you don't find the box.
