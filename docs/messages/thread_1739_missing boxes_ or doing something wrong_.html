There is no list of missing boxes that I know of. Generally the
availability of the boxes depands on the placer or maintainer taking
care of them, replacing them when needed, or updating the clues to
reflect their current status. Sometimes this doesn't happen
promptly, and there is no record of the box's status. There is also
no list of placers who have lost interest in the hobby, and their
boxes go unmaintained.

Often times searchers miss the boxes or have trouble following the
clues. This happens to me on occasion. I never use the
term "missing" for any box that is not mine. I do report that I
was "unsuccessful in finding the box". I would use the "contact the
placer" function on the clue page to alert them. They may respond
if they know the status.

You are wise to search for the box before bringing the young ones
out. This avoids much dissapointment. You can search the message
archives with the name of the box for any reports from others who
were "unsuccessful in finding the box". Use the unlabeled space
next to the button labeled "search archive" on the "messages" page
of the talk list.

The easiest thing is to look for another box in your area to find
one that is ready for visitors.
