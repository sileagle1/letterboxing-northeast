From: kathiekelleher | Date: 2006-05-21 12:29:09 UTC
Hi Limur and Sanfroid,

I didn't go to check on the boxes until Saturday as we had more pouring rain on Friday-
AARRGGGHHHHH!
But yesterday was sunny and warm and I'm happy to say that Chain, Chain, Chain is snug
as a little bug in it's wonderful hiding place. The last visitor did a fine job of rehiding I'm
pleased to say. Oak Hill is in perfect shape as well. I didn't go to Yeat yet but it's way
above the water line. I'm going to plant one of mine today so I'll visit them and contact you
if there's a problem. No news will mean good news!

The river has receded and things are back to normal for most folks. The National Guard is
down on Plum Island hauling out tons of garbage that floated down the river and landed
there. Things as far away as Goffstown, NH! I'd like to see that but I'm sure they're not
allowing anyone near there.

Well, I can't wait for your visit in June and the little "souvenirs" you're leaving behind!!!

Take care, Orion
