Echo Lake Mansfield

From: Chuck Straub | Date: 2004-10-06 02:01:34 UTC
As strange as it sounds, don't go to Echo Woods in Mansfield to find Echo Lake. From route 195, go down cemetery road to Joshua's Trust Pond lot. Same location as my Ponde Place letterbox. Same place as the MMM Monsters of Cemetery Road. Follow this trail. It will go along the shore of the lake and comes to a nice drive up area for fire trucks to pump in water. Short walk. To get to Echo Lake, go this way.....Chuck and Molly
