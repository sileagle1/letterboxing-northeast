If you liked "O Letterbox Tree" compare it to "Christmas Spirit" placement

From: morgunjp | Date: 2006-01-02 15:55:21 UTC
"O Letterbox Tree" 11 boxes
"Christmas Spirit" 9 boxes (8 easily accessible)

Both are Christmas Themed.
Both seem to be immune to snowfall.

"O Letterbox Tree" Christmas Season only
"Christmas Spirit" Winter only

"O Letterbox Tree" 11 Special Boxes
"Christmas Spirit" 2 hitchhiker hostels, 1 micro-box, 6 hitchhikers

"O Letterbox Tree" Arcimedes Screw & 3 other placers
"Christmas Spirit" Choi & 3 other placers

"O Letterbox Tree" Cape Cod
"Christmas Spirit" N. Billerica, MA

Coincidence: One of Arcimedes'hitchhikers was a charter member, but was
one of the first to be exchanged out. You need to leave a HH to take
one. If you leave one without taking one, you can come back & take one
later (so the number of HHs could decrease later on).

OK, I can't boast about my carving, but the Santa stamp took me longer
than any other stamp so far.

"Christmas Spirit" was designed as a winter version of the Littleton
hostel, "Picking Apples with Sarah," before I heard about "O Christmas
Tree." All 3 placements "immune to snow."

You don't need a hitchhiker to come & collect all 8 stamps. So, if you
enjoyed "O Christmas Tree," you might also enjoy "Christmas Spirit."

Enjoy,
Morgun
