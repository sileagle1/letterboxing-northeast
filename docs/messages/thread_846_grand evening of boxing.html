Love these June evenings! We had light until 8ish and were able to go after the north half of "Up North/Down South" at the Carolina Management Area. I'm really getting to like that area for hiking.
The box is alive and well.
We had the clues to "bobbin' bobber" in the notebook and decide to check to see if we could locate it. It's still there and hadn't been visited in over a year. Imagine when Wanda and Pete only had 1214 finds! What a lovely spot the Carolina Trout Pond is!
I couldn't find the clues to that one on the website or in the 'basement'.
