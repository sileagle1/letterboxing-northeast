~Boy Scout Troop 58 of Lebanon, CT is holding its annual Earth Day
activities this Saturday April 23rd.

In honor of this I have carved a special event stamp that was
designed by one of the scouts.
It is very easy to receive this stamp.
All you need to do is:
come to the Lebanon Green near the library
on Saturday between 8-2
go to the information tent and ask for the box
in order to get it you have to say what you have done to help the
Earth
(this could be many things planting, cleaning, picking up trash
etc...)

There will be many other things going on as well
D.E.P. interactive displays
free trees for planting
a limited amount of free bluebird houses
butterfly feeders
a plant sale
hot dogs/chips/soda for sale

There are also A LOT of boxes in the town of Lebanon. Many of which
are drive bys. I will also have a limited amount of clue sheets to a
WOM set of boxes in town.

I hope some of you can make it.
