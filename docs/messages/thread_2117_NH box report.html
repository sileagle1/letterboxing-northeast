Miss Buggy and I spent the weekend visiting my folks who are testing
northern NH out as a possible retirement location....... We only
managed to go after 3 boxes in between rainstorms and hail (ok, ok, and
yardsaling....), but had a great weekend. :-)

pezpam


Old Man Lives On -- Couldn't locate this one. Found what *had* to be
the boulder, but the base was completely flooded after the rains. We
poked through the water and muck hoping to find it and looked around
the area just in case it had been dragged away or somesuch. No such
luck. Bummer, especially since there was a bonus box advertised. Ah
well - we were the only people around, so it was a nice walk.

Little Dragonfly and Pemi Trail boxes -- Both in place and in great
shape. Miss Buggy had fun trying to decide if the large boulder
mentioned in the Pemi Trail clues *really* was as tall as Nana if she
stood on Pop's shoulders. :-)
