From: T Prindle | Date: 2005-04-22 13:25:30 UTC
Last weekend PowWow Dancer and I were doing the Presidential Animals
in Coventry, CT. In the second box 'Jack the Turkey', in addition to
Jack and the boxe's logbook, we found another ziplock with a small
square commercial stamp depicting a cartoon of a little girl, along
with a pinkish cat-eye stamp pad... but... no logbook!

In the box's logbook we looked back three or so entries and finally
found one that said 'Dropped off the Suzie-Q Hitch Hiker' -- so we
found out the name of this little girl. We've no idea where the
logbook might have got off to. If anyone might have picked it by
accident, or hear's of Suzie-Q's logbook please drop us an email so
we can re-unite them.

Otherwise, we'll make a new logbook for her? and set Suzie-Q on her
way, in the hopes that she might eventually find her way back home
(re: there's no contact for the placer when the logbook gets full).

Hope to hear something on her!
