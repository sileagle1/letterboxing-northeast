From: Pamela Smith Lenox | Date: 2005-09-05 12:35:49 UTC
Well, since it was cooler yesterday, Miss Buggy and I headed out to
grab a couple of local boxes.

Lisha Kills Preserve series - We found the Protector of Hidden
Treasures and he's a cute little bugger. Box and all in good shape. We
missed the other box, but have been assured that it was there a couple
of days earlier, so we may go back for it.

Lock 7 - cool stamp! We were also lucky enough to be there just as the
lockmaster started the process to let a small boat through, so Miss
Buggy got a quick engineering lesson along with a ncie day out and a
good stamp. :-) Hardest part of this was the stealth factor - would
have been fine except that there was a couple sitting at the nearby
picnic bench and they just wouldn't respond to the brainwave-based plea
to leave, darn them. We had to fake things like rocks in our shoes and
wait for them to glance away so we could make another grab for the box.
Took several tries, but Miss Buggy was having fun putting her spy
skills to use. LOL

Thanks to the planters. It was such a nice day to be out and about.
