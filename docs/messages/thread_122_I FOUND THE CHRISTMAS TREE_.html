I FOUND THE CHRISTMAS TREE!

From: Warrior Woman | Date: 2004-01-03 22:46:46 UTC
I started my New Year letterboxing for six hours straight!

Woo hoo!

I swung by and got my first letterbox of 2004 with none other than a
Drew Clan Production - Great Gathering of 2001.

Next on my list was Pete and Wanda's Favorite Ornament Series, which
I did and even found the elusive pickle. I found Psychokiwi's
Gingerbread Man and SpringChick's Kindergarten Keepsakes.

Since the light was still pretty good, I decided to tackle Laurette's
additions to the series, even though I knew that the Christmas Tree
was gone.

I entered that section of the location and was fascinated by the
scenery. I haven't been to the Pacific Northwest in a long long time
and this area really reminded me of it. Needless to say, out went
the idea of letterboxing and in went the idea of exploring.

A magic letterboxing pixie must have been with me, because a HALF A
MILE AWAY FROM ITS INTENDED LOCATION, FAR OFF ANY KNOWN TRAIL, JUST
SITTING THERE IN THE SUN, was a Blue Topped Rubbermaid container. My
heart stopped. I opened it, and there it was. The Christmas Tree
Letterbox. What a beauty!

I ran back to the proper location, checked my bearings and compass
reading three, then four times, and replanted The Christmas Tree
where it belonged. I must have gotten it right, too, because after
replanting it and going on with the clues, I immediately found "Gold
Star" and "Candy Cane".

The light was starting to fade and me + low light = disaster, so I
will have to go back some day to find "Stocking", "Silver Bell",
and "Angel Grace".

Happy New Year!

Warrior Woman
