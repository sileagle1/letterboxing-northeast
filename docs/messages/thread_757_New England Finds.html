From: Don | Date: 2004-09-06 23:19:42 UTC
Hi
My letterboxing treks this past couple of weekends have been great.
I hit a 100 % find.
On 8/28/04 we found:
1. Chamberlain-Reynolds Memorial Forest Letterbox near Center
Harbor, NH and planted by Dancin' Feet. Our search for this box
took place in the evening and just as we found it, a Loon started
its mournful call. We had a resident from NH with us and she
couldn't believe she never had been to this beautiful area before.
The power of letterboxing.

2. Tannery Hill Bridge LB in the town of Gilford and planted by Son
of a Sailor. We arrived at the covered bridge in the dark and were
surprised by a bridge lit with lanterns for old town day. We
retrieved the letterbox and opened it under the light of the bridge.

On 9/05/04 I found the Des Types A La Porte letterbox series in
Taunton, Mass. and was planted by Teamgreendragon. After a great
trek around the park I found all 3 boxes in good shape.

On 9/06/04 while biking the East Bay Bike Path, from E. Providence,
RI to Bristol, RI and back, I for the Jogger and the Wheelchair
Letterboxes in good shape.
