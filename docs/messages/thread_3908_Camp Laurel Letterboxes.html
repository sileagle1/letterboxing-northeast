Camp Laurel Letterboxes

From: Connie Ziko | Date: 2008-09-22 23:13:44 UTC
 Recently there were 4 letterboxes planted at Camp Laurel Girl Scout Camp in Lebanon and they are listed on the LBNA site . These boxes were planted by scouts .  This is private property and people have asked how they can get these boxes .

I have talked to the caretaker at Camp Laurel . I work there in the summer and I am a Girl Scout Leader . The care taker has asked that people not come to the camp and ask to look for the boxes . This is private property . The camp is used all year long . There is a HUGE insurance issue for non Scouts .  There are CAmporees most of the year after that the camp is open to conferences and workshops for all scouts and leaders.

I have written to the planter and asked that the listing of the boxes be removed from the LBNA site . I have not received a response. They can keep the boxes there but not list them for the public to see.

There are  other boxes at this camp .  I am the keeper of  them and I check on them every summer . The clues are for Girl Scouts only . Most troops know this.

I have emailed the LBNA webmaster  and asked them to remove the listing of these boxes and why ......The webmaster has removed them .
