Hello,

I'm just posting to let everyone know that the "Enchantments
Letterbox" has been removed for a short time, due to the box getting
run over by a truck.

Funny thing, it's an INDOOR BOX!!!! I posted a picture in the photos

extreme structural damage.

Anyways, I will be replacing the box soon. Check out the picture.
