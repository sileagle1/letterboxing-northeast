Re: [LbNE] Poor Music Woman and Kreepy Kelli

From: Pam | Date: 2005-02-14 00:45:18 UTC
Well, today was not one of our better letterboxing days. As Debby said, Kelli and I were hit by a car coming out of a local church in East Lyme. I'm quite sore, but I'm doing ok. Kelli, however, is much sorer (is that a word?) than I am, especially since she is already nursing a previous condition from another accident. We were both sent by ambulance to Lawrence and Memorial in New London, where we spent hours in the emergency. Needless to say, we won't be boxing for a while.

I don't think Kelli's had the purple heart LB, though I've already received it. My injury the first time was much tamer than this one, so I really think I earned it this time!

Thanks so much for all of your prayers, and a special THANK YOU to Butterfly and Bluebird for helping us in a scary situation. Butterfly and Bluebird stayed with my car until my brother and sister-in-law showed up with his flat bed tow truck, and tried to keep us calm during the ordeal.

Thanks everyone for your e-mails! I hope to see all of you soon!
Music Woman
