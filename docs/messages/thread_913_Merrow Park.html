Merrow Park

From: btoesatbarker | Date: 2004-07-02 21:28:18 UTC
Hey, I was wondering if anyone has done this box in
Mansfield/Mansfield Depot lately. Callie and I went to the park and
did several boxes there today. While looking for the Merrow Park box
we found the stone wall with the 5x5 endcap, looked under it for any
place where a stone could be pulled out that was big enough for a
box, and came up empty. We then looked under all the small stones
that we could pull out under the cap at the junction of the wall, and
still came up empty. Any other suggestions? We were able to find the
Three wizzards, Callie got the Last Day (I already had it),
Willimantic River Trail, Champlion's General Store, Thank You, and
the Mansfield Depot Train Trestle. I left Driftwood hitch hiker at
one of those places, too. Yesterday we had a great day finding
Journey into Imagination. We got soaked in the only place in
Connecticut that I think it poured! Oh well, had fun anyway. And
finally found all the boxes after retracing our steps. Any help with
Merrow Park would be appreciated.
Queen B
