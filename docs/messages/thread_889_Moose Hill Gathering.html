Moose Hill Gathering

From: mindizney@aol.com | Date: 2004-06-28 03:42:28 UTC
I just got back from Moose Hill (yes, it IS 11:31 p.m.) and what a great
gathering it was! It was so nice to meet so many new boxers! I haven't even
counted up the exchanges yet, but they were plentiful! Thanks to so many who
stamped into my Manchester United letterbox! Your stamps will be heading to
Dartmoor in August and the British boxers will get to see the stamps from some of
our boxers here in the states. Thanks to Rustypuff and CSCM for putting
together a great event and thanks to Moose Hill for allowing us to have the event
there!

After the gather, we were successful in finding a couple of Camera Obscura
boxes and some other mystery boxes as well! Thanks to Donny who was able to
fish the red dude ;-) out of his hiding place. Kellie and I looked in the hole,
but didn't see anything, but somehow Donny was able to sense that it was
there! It was also nice to see the BIG Virgin back in place after it's unfortunate
demise. CO, you did a GREAT job on the cutting, pasting and scanning the old
ripped up logbook! The stamps were also GREAT! Wow..... bring a BIG logbook!

Thanks again for a great day! Now I have to stay up all night to finish my
grad class homework... which I got in the mail BEFORE THE CLASS EVEN
STARTED!!!!! UGH!

Happy Hunting!
Music Woman

Moose Hill Gathering

From: Connie Nicolas | Date: 2005-08-22 14:43:57 UTC
As expected, this annual gathering was casual,
relaxing, friendly, and extremely enjoyable. The
weather cooperated for the most part, allowing us to
sit at picnic tables to our heart's content as we
exchanged stamps and good conversation, as well as
enjoying a potluck lunch. Those of us who boxed
weren't hampered by much more than a few sprinkles.
Thanks again, Peppermint Patti, for some awesome new
boxes in Sharon. I highly recommend the TV Land
series, Disc Golf, and of course, Summertime at
Crescent Ridge Dairy! And thanks, Rustypuff, for all
your work in putting together such a successful
gathering.
A grateful letterboxer, Songbird
