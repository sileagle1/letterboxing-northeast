In a pure sense, event stamps should be retired after the event has
taken place. However, Art is meant to be shared, so why hide it
away? I don't think a person who is unable to attend an event should
be deprived the honor of obtaining a stamp image which someone spent
time and creative energy carving, with the intent of sharing with
others. Placing the stamp in a letterbox, so finders have to earn the
right to stamp in, makes a whole lot of sense to me!
