From: Janet | Date: 2004-10-25 23:13:04 UTC
I have all the monsters boxes in an excel spread sheet with miles. I
used the miles in the clues when given and a rough approximation of
miles based on time when distance wasn't given. I gave the drive
by's 0 miles. The total came to 32 miles. I would say the actual
mileage was slightly less than this but pretty close maybe about 30
miles. so if you just did 1 mile a day in the month of October you
would be done by Oct 30 LOL.

moonstone
