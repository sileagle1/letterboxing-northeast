slanting the count

From: Phyto | Date: 2004-04-12 18:50:56 UTC
Hi folks
I appreciate the comments and suggestions about my decision to pull my clues and access
to them being on our site only. It's really no different - it eliminates the middle step of
going to the LBNA site and just going to ours. I do hear Dave and Diane however, it does
get confusing when you're searching by an area.

I really like Pete's suggestion of posting one clue/link if you will on the site with a grand
box total count (that of course i guess i could update...) and that way the official "box
count" for maine would be "official" if you will and that it could directly link to our home
page thus pointing people in that way from the lbna site. I'll have to chew on that, but i do
like the idea.

The truth is that I'm not in it for the numbers. If i were, then i would have been completely
absorbed in boxing my way through Connecticut ( no offense ) sooner than now. i'm in it
for placing and crafting clues and stamps that bring people to those places that are so
very special to me. This of course includes mostly Maine, but in some cases - out of state
as well.

The *only* issue I really see with the way that i'm choosing to opt out of the LBNA site is if
the population of placers/finders increases in this state and those who are unaware of my
clues just blindly go about placing boxes in the same spot - then that would be tacky.

I don't know what the best solution is, but I figure that people in england have been doing
something right for a very long time...

-phyto
