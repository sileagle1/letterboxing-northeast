Spring into April Drive!

From: Mark Pepe | Date: 2005-05-01 00:23:20 UTC
Hi boxers:

I know many of you are preparing and downloading clues for the May Day
Gathering, but I had to take a few minutes from those activities to
thank MayEve and Wild Rover for such a fun and box-filled event. The
debut boxes are superb and we heartily encourage others who could not
make it to look for these once the clues are posted.

RTRW - big thanks for opening up your home to 30 or so hungry,
exchanging, stamping, wet, cold letterboxing addicts! The warmth of
your house and our welcome were very much appreciated.

It was great to see some old friends and make and exchange with new
ones. And it was wonderful to see the bigger-than-expected turnout for
today's event, with boxers representing 4 states attending.

Guess the weather can't keep a good boxer down! :-)

Mark
