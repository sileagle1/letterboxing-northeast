boxing in Saratoga Springs

From: Pamela Smith Lenox | Date: 2005-01-17 19:14:24 UTC
Well, despite the cold, the snow held off long enough for my daughter
and I to find 3 boxes up in Saratoga Springs this lovely MLK weekend. I
am extremely grateful for the plethora of warm stores selling hot
chocolate in the area as well as to the planters of the boxes listed
below. :-)


Let's Party is in good shape. The ink pad is starting to bead up and
fall apart, but the ink still works.

Fountain of Youth is also fine, though the seeker is considerably more
exposed to the public in winter than it appears they would be in
summer. I don't believe anyone saw us get the box, but the hiding spot
isn't very hidden with no leaves on the trees.

Final Exam. Well, we found the box and stamped into the logbook, but
the stamp itself is missing.

Box on!
