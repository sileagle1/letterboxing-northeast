Super-Cheap Custom Rubber Stamps

From: Alafair | Date: 2004-02-27 00:44:14 UTC
Hi Everyone,

Just thought I'd pass this on.

Though I generally carve my own stamps, I needed something special
for a non-letterboxing project.I found a great ultra-cheap source on-
line for custom made rubber stamps. It is Thai Rubber Stamps, or
www.thairubberstamp.com. Yes...it is in Thailand!

I was hesitant at first, so sent my very simple designs to them as
*.jpeg files and they sent a proof and confirmed the price I came up
with on their website...a whopping, horrendous $6.70 total....and
that includes shipping of $1.00...from Thailand!

I paid by credit card through their source, a legitimate bank in
Thailand (I checked on-line), received confirmation of payment both
through the bank and from the stamp company. Two weeks later, my
package arrived!

They did a really nice job. These stamps are mostly text, 1" in
diameter, about 12 pt. Arial, with a tiny graphic. They stamp very
well and even come with a plastic handle.

Note that I designed the stamp in MSPublisher so all the lines were
sharp and distinct. They cannot handle shading. I'm not sure how
they would do with a drawing, but you could send a *.jpeg of it and
ask them to send you a proof and price quote. They can make stamps in
quite a few different sizes. They usually respond to emails within 24
hours.

So if you're into faux postage, or are not into carving your own
stamps and want something special, I think it may be worth the risk
for such a low price.

One caveat to all of this is that I am truly concerned I am
supporting child labor by doing this..or putting American rubber
stamp makers out of business ;-). Unfortunately, there is no way of
knowing this. So...I guess my advice is to trust your conscience and
do what is right for you.
