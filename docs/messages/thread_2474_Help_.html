Falls Village hiking

From: Josef Carlson | Date: 2005-10-23 14:03:51 UTC
Hey all! I went out to do the the "End of the Trail" letterbox in Falls Village yesterday. It was terrific! Near the trailhead is an amazing waterfalls! Since this has been a "moist" October, the waterfall is really gushing! The hike along the Mohawk Trail itself was great...so much to see. The AMC hut served as a good rest area, alhough I found a smoldering campfire nearby. Thank you to the End Gang for a great rainy autumn adventure! (You are right, it is scenic in autumn.)

PS...if anyone knows, does the Mohawk Trail pass through Dudleytown? It's been about 5 years since I explored that area...very mysterious spot.

Josef
