Gathering in South Yarmouth!

From: wolkl | Date: 2011-09-12 19:16:27 UTC
We have a wonderful letterboxing gathering opportunity at the beautiful Cultural Center of Cape Cod on October 2nd, 2011. It is part of our Treasures of Yarmouth Scavenger Hunt, and there will be over a dozen new hand-carved stamps along our map route, plus the chance to gather with other letterboxers at the reception in the evening. The event is only $12,(children under 12 are free) and includes the entire day's adventure plus a reception with live music, food and drinks. Truly valubale prizes for successful "hunters"! The day allows plenty of time to go do additional letterboxing - and there are a LOT of boxes in Yarmouth. We have already posted on Atlas Quest - but would love to learn how to get the word out throughout New England with your help! Thank you! Any questions call 508-394-7100
