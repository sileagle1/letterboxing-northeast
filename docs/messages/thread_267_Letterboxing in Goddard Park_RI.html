Letterboxing in Goddard Park,RI

From: Don | Date: 2004-02-24 10:41:10 UTC
HI
Had a little time this weekend to go letterboxing and found two boxes
in Goddard Park. The Pony Club Box is alive and well. The Pirates
Past Noon LB is in need of some TLC, One journal is full but someone
left a new one also the stamp has been broken. I've also driven the
Lime Green Punch Buggie HH to another state.

Hope everyone has a great time at the winter gathering. I was
planning on attending but got a call yesterday from the Blackstone
Valley Park Service looking for help from my scouts on a river clean
up for the same date. I'll be thinking of you as we hall 2000 tires
out of the Blackstone.

Trail Turtle

From: dmartin361 | Date: 2004-02-25 11:33:18 UTC
It's funny you said that. I started letterboxing on the first day of spring last year with an accidental letterbox find on the Blackstone Bike Path and 75 finds to date haven't looked back. I have plans on placing two boxes along the river this year. One will be at the location of the old Lonsdale Drive-In. I was the land surveyor that helped turn it from a abandon drive-in, to a nature wetland with a lagoon and two islands. There now laying out a new section of the bike path.

Oh to Brian of Team Green Dragon, I got my dates crossed the river clean up is Saturday so I'll be coming to the gathering.

See you there
