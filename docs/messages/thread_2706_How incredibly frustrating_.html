From: wink2267 | Date: 2006-02-14 21:21:55 UTC
Here's a small tip (bit of help).
Go to AQ, and search for (from) an area. Your area (wherever you
were). Say if you were in BlahBlah Mass, you'd search everything
from BlahBlah out for 5, 10, 25, 75 miles in all directions. You
can pick a spot, and it'll search out from there. Maybe this can
help some. *shrug*
-WINK
