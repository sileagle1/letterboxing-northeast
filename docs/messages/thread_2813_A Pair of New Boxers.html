A Pair of New Boxers

From: Sara | Date: 2006-04-08 02:05:31 UTC
Hello Everyone!

My boyfriend and I have been boxing since the beggining of this
year. As I'm sure all of you already know.. its a addicting, and we
love it. We hail from South Jersey & Long Island, but are currently
in our fourth (and final) year at the University of New Haven in CT.

We are going on Sunday to find 12 CT State Forest boxes (we want
those free walking sticks they are promising to those who find all
thirty) and we plan on picking up some more individuals boxes while
we are up there (since individuals' boxes are probably more fun). If
anyone has a box in Litchfield County, CT let us know and we will
make it priority!

Hate to post this on the LB North America group and this one.. but
does anyone know of a LB (or a series) on a trail that runs along
Old Marsh Pond in Bristol, CT? It was the first box David & I ever
found, and what got the idea in our heads to begin with (3 years
ago! .. sometimes it takes a while to get started). We can't find
clues anywhere online to a box over there.. and if any one knows it
would mean a lot to a young sentimental couple!

Just wanted to introduce ourselves since our *HappilyEverAfter*
stamp will soon be appearing in LBs everywhere! :)

We love our new sport!

Sara & David (Team*HappilyEverAfter*)
