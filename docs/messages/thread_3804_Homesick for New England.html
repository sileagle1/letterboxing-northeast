Homesick for New England

From: oldhounder | Date: 2008-05-14 19:46:58 UTC
I rarely read group boards and even more rarely write to them. Today
it just happened that I opened LbNE and saw some familiar names. Felt
good to see some Northerner input although we have a growing batch of
very good friends down here in the deep South (Florida). Guess I'm
getting a little homesick.

Up North, Siamese and I box central MA, southern NH, as much of the CT
River valley of VT/NH as we can (we are Valley Questers), northern VT
(our native and spiritual home) and much of Maine.

We prefer to have one or two boxing companions on our jaunts. When
boxing in central and eastern MA, great companions are available, but
we usually box by ourselves in CT, western MA, NH, VT and ME. If
boxing with us slow-moving, old fogies would interest any of you, let
us know. We are woods people which means we are pretty quiet in the
woods with the hopes of seeing more.

BTW, down here, my favorite finds are a couple of mystery boxes by
someone calling themselves the doppelgangers. Ever hear of them?

oldhounder
