weekend wanderings

From: Dave & Diane | Date: 2007-04-09 13:55:34 UTC
Dave was home on leave and the weather was nice so we decided to do a bit of maintenance on his Rome Point Series. We were on the path to put something in the Rome box when we ran across Warrior Woman and Brother Bear also out doing maintenance! In fact they got an earlier start and did most of our work for us! Wow, you guys rock! They had found the boxes out 'sunning' themselves on the stone walls and made sure each and every one of the was tucked in nice and snug.So, Great Job guys! A+.
We also got to finish the Pirates of the Caribbean Series and did maintenance on Island Sanctuary. It now has a new logbook for the season. For a box that's in a dry location it seems to pick up alot of moisture.?
We are planning on replanting Conanicut Canons as soon as we can be sure we can keep the site to ourselves. We will post when it's back in service.
Good luck to all you Spring'boxers,
Diane
