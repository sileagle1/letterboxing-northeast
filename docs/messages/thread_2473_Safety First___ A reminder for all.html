Safety First... A reminder for all

From: mainekokopellian | Date: 2005-10-17 17:16:58 UTC
The organizers of Frightnight@ the Fort want to remind everyone
attending our event or planning to box this Fall in Maine (and New
England) to be aware of the hunting seasons & wear appropriate bright
attire when boxing in the woods.

We recently became aware that some of the parks around Southern Maine
may have some limited access to hunters. One such place is the Town
Farm Forest in Kittery.It allows access to hunters in some parts.
People just should take precautions to have necessary outerware,just
in case!!

WE want everyone to be smart & safe during this Fall boxing season!
