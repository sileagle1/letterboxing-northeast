On Sunday Grumpy and I found #500! We had to do the "woo hoo" dance
without Mona as the park we were in didn't allow dogs. She did the
dance in true doggie fashion when we told her when we got home.
We just wanted to thank everyone for planting all those wonderful
boxes. To Sunshine and Swamp Yankee, thank you for sharing your
hobby with us and for letting us tag along on occasion. To Toad dog
for not eating Mona the first time he met her. We love to bike ride
in the summer which makes letterboxing a special challenge as it's
hard to do both, so to those of you who plant with bike riders in
mind we send a heartfelt thank you. Sunshine you were right when you
said we wouldn't be satisfied with only 500, 1000 here we come!!!
Again, thank you all,
Hez, Grumpy and Mona
F503, P17, E62
