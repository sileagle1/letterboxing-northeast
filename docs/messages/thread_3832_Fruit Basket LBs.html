Fruit Basket LBs

From: Bell Lady | Date: 2008-06-25 19:54:08 UTC
I checked Fruit Basket today and had to make a few changes in the
clues since my clues were old and some non-existent. All 5 boxes in
the series are alive and well. The trail between the 2nd box and the
road is very overgrown but you can get through. The 3rd box appeared
to be missing but we found it beside the trail a little farther on.
Looks like some hiker saw it (maybe it wasn't concealed well), looked
at it and threw it into the bushes. I feel fortunate to have found
it. This box has been hidden in a new location. Everything else seem
to be in good shape. Be sure to check the revised clues before hunting
for these.
This is one of my earliest series and I'm delighted that they have
survived so well. Thanks to all finders for careful re-concealing.
