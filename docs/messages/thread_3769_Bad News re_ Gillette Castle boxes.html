Bad News re. Gillette Castle boxes

From: rospa10 | Date: 2008-03-30 21:46:56 UTC
I cut short my weekly visit with my dad today so I could get lots of
the boxes at Gillette that were planted in this past year. A number
have been taken--I know for sure as the rocks used to cover them were
still in place. Here are the affected series:
"Defiant Mona" (box 1 and 3) I could not find 2.
"Snooping Around Gillette Castle"
"Trail Signs in Stone"--I only looked for first box which was
gone. Was too discouraged and mad to check the others.

I could not find "Restored and Home". However, it could be there as
I'm not good with finding stone pile/stone wall boxes.

I did find "Hadlyme Country Store".
Too bad we couldn't plant a box and keep tabs by mounting one of
those motion detector cameras nearby so we could get a visual i.d. of
the creep. Perhaps we should plant a box in the park just for this
rotton person with some fresh Mona "deposits"
>^..^<
