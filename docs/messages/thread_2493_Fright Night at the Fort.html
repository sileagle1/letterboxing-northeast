Fright Night at the Fort

From: kathiekelleher | Date: 2005-10-23 18:39:43 UTC
I would like to thank everyone who worked so hard on Fright Night. From finding out
Boch's Circus was in town right through to the costume contest, fun was the key to the
day! Fort McClary was a great spot, loaded with history. I've lived a 1/2 hour away for over
20 years and have never been there. With all the stone walls and granite blocks it was the
ideal spot to play hide and seek!

I brought along several of my family members (new boxers) and they had a great day as
well. The people were friendly, the food was delicious and the event boxes were clever.
There are some pretty talented up and coming carvers out there!

So to Maine Kopellian, Oceanwytch, Cooties, The End and all involved- a big thanks for a
job well done!!!!

Take care, Orion
