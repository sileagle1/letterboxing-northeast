orion reminded me about something. EGGS! this weekend i will be
traveling to connecticut from new hampshire so i can find some
easter eggs. i plan on leaving at 4:30 in the morning. here's why!
saturday is my sister's baby shower in conway, NH and i had offered
to pick up my grandmother who lives in baltic, CT i fugured that i
could drive down to CT find those eggs, maybe some others boxes,
grab my grandmother, drive all the way north to conway, and then do
some more boxing there after the party. oh the baby shower starts at
1. i think i have plenty of time.

and then, sunday is Ice Scream Sunday! this will be my second event
and i want to leave extra early in the morning. I have not found a
single box in MA yet! i have found 20 in CT, 15 in RI, 11 in NY. All
these boxes were found "AFTER" driving through MA. Go figure. i dont
know how i managed to do that, but things are going to change. Bring
on the crazy weekend.

living free or dying tryin'
