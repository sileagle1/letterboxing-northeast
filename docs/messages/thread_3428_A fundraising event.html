Fellow boxers;

This May I will be walking the Boston Avon Walk for Breast Cancer.
I'm doing this because March 1st marked the 1 year anniversary of my
diagnosis of breast cancer. (Treatment is over, and I'm clear of
cancer!)

To help raise money for the walk I am hosting a fundraising party in
Franklin, MA. I've decided to have an "event" box, as well as some
of my postals that are now home, at the party. This could be a
perfect time to start your spring exchnages as well as dance off some
off those winter pounds (I know I need to) before we all gear up for
a season full of letterboxing.

This is an important cause to me, for obvious reasons. It would mean
the world to me to have some of my boxing neighbors join us for a
good time for a good cause.

DETAILS:
Date: March 30th
Time: 6:00 PM
Location: Elk's Lodge Franklin, MA
Donation: $25 single $40 couples (tax deductible)
Dinner Buffet, DJ, Door prizes, raffles, and letterboxes
Contact: Lisa at familyfrommass@ aol.com

Please contact me if you think you can come. If you want to help the
cause, but can't make it to the party, you can make a donation to me
