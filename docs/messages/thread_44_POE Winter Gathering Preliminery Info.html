POE Winter Gathering Preliminery Info

From: Warrior Woman | Date: 2003-12-11 03:55:13 UTC
Here's where we are so far:

Jim Harvey closes the farm to the public on December 24.

He has a few trips planned for January. I am hoping
to nail him down for some weekend in January. It
could possibly be in February, though. I don't know
yet. He's looking as his schedule.

As noted in my previous post to the LBNE group, the only
sanitary facilities is one portolet. For this event,
he'll need to arrange for the parking lot to be
plowed, a second portolet for the day, and I need to
find a few conference tables.

Since his barn is actually a country gift barn, he
will have someone to come in to man the register if
anyone wishes to make purchases. He has some really
cool stuff. Trust me on this one.

The space inside is rather crowded, so I am thinking
that we will have the food inside where it is warm,
and place the conference tables just outside the
door for stamp exchanges.

There are a LOT of letterboxes in the nearby area, including the one
at the farm itself. I will pull that letterbox out of its hiding
place for the event, since the place where it is hidden cannot
withstand the heavy traffic it would endure for an event.

If anyone has plans of placing "event only" boxes, please understand
that you will not be able to plant them on the farm itself, nor in
the barn. The barn is filled with a lot of fragile items. We want
to respect the space. If you wish to do an "event only" box, please
have them on your person, like a traveler, or out in the open, on the
exchange table. Or plant one in the many nearby forest and trail
areas.

Brian of Team Green Dragon has already volunteered
to carve the Event stamp.

(Thank you, Brian)

I'll let you all know the date just as soon as *I* know
the date!

Thanks for your patience.

Warrior Woman
