THE CELTIC MINIATURE SERIES

From: Northstar | Date: 2003-12-25 06:05:19 UTC
Just want to say that i visited the "celtic miniature" series in New
Haven today and on the whole, the series is in an advanced state of
disrepair. In box #1, the plastic zip bag is broken and the logbook
was quite damp. The inkpad has also fallen apart. It should be
brought in to dry for a week or so and re-bagged. This would mainly
be to avoid the kind of damage that box #2 sustained. The logbook for
box #2 is pretty much a goner. The metal rings rusted and corroded
the logbook. In addition, the book was pretty wet to begin with, and
most of the stamped-in images have washed away leaving the pages
stuck together. Also, the pen didn't work, the stamp came away from
it's mount and the cover on the ink pad has somehow managed to become
sealed shut. Needless to say, I pulled this box for some serious
repair and will try to get it back out in a timely fashion. Box #3
seemed to be in the best condition, aside from me having to wade
through waist-high prickers to reach it. Still, it could yet benefit
from something as simple as a new bag or two. People have been
stamping into this logbook from both sides despite a "this end up"
marker. Oh well. Upon the return of box #2, I will probably pull the
first box for those suggested repairs unless, of course, the owner of
the series beats me to it. Is there still an owner? --NORTHSTAR
