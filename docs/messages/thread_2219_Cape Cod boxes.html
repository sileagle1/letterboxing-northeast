Cape Cod boxes

From: btoesatbarker | Date: 2005-07-09 01:21:35 UTC
Hi all, just returned from the Cape and wanted to report about the
boxes I found and did not find. All the found boxes were in fine
shape, even though some were quite exposed. All were recovered better
when we left. Here is the list:

CCBL West Box #4 Falmouth Commodors
Woods Hole
CCBL West Box #2 Cotuit Kettleers
The Empty Tomb (Very creepy!!)
Flaming Dragons
Old Iron Sides (Mermaid Mom - thanks for being so understanding)
Bray Farm Letterbox
Toy Box #4: Scrabble
Bass Hole Letterbox
Unless Letterbox
Le Pew Lebberbox
Shade Tail Letterbox
Indian Grounds
AJ's Box

Could not find:

The Sandy Neck Sand Dollar - I believe we were in the right spot, but
we could not find more than two trees heading towards the water from
the garbage can, and did not find the box near either one. Anyone
else have problems with this one over the 4th?

Thanks to all who planted these wonderful boxes for us to find. It
was wonderful having so many to choose from. I decided on very short
ones and drive-bys since I was with my daughter Callie, and she is
not a good walker. Good thing, since she twisted her ankle on the
very first box and hobbled the rest of the day!

Queen B
