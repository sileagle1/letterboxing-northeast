CA 'boxers trip to the Cape

From: doublesaj12 | Date: 2006-09-12 23:23:48 UTC
Thanks to the wonderful 'boxers in the Cape area for meeting with us
left-coasters for an exchange last week. CapeCodLetterboxer,
Archimedes Screw, CapeCodLightningBug, Mumma & Bunny Boy, Kylie (I'm
forgetting the names of the adorable little ones. . .) and
AckLetterboxer were wonderful to take time out of their day to have
some fun. A special thank-you to ackletterboxer for driving us all
the way out to Great Point to see a fabulous sunset!

We were able to find the following boxes on Nantucket:
Aquarium Adventure
Endeavor
Water Pump April
Winter Park
My Family's Farm all by Camp Girls
New North Cemetery
A Whale's Tale
Miacomet Pond
Nantucket Sea Scallop
Got Wood? all by ackletterboxer
Nantucket Lightship Basket by Archimedes Screw
Great Point Lighthouse by LoneMassWolf
The Black Cat by Lycos Charisma

And the following boxes on the cape and in NH and VT:
St. Francis Xavier
CCBl West Box #1 by CC LBer
Crafter's Landing by Cuddlebug and eyeBob
Mystery Read
Coffin Lid
A Very Gorey Letterbox
Peace Day all by A. Screw
Leaf Peeper
Rocky's Box by team Leafpeeper
Mom's Rest Stop
All in a Row by Rubaduc
Sugar Bush Cheese Block
Slice of Friendship by Mark & Sue
Birthplace of VT by Princess Lea (from our neck of the woods in No.
Calif.)
We had a ball! If you are out west, let us know and we'll try to
show YOU a good time too!

~~Doublesaj & Old Blue~~
Davis, CA
