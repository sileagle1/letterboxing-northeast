Where's Bullwinkle?? In my stamp book!!

From: mepanj | Date: 2004-06-06 22:57:00 UTC
Over the course of several months, lots of miles (usually in cirles),
countless bumps, scraps and bruises, several strange stares from
neighboring people (as well as a friendly officer of the law),and
lots of blood lost to the swarming mosquitos, I and the rest of the
group Mepanj as well as Follow the Son can now gaze upon Bullwinkle
in all his glory as he resides safe and sound in our log books. What
an amazing adventure, both for the hunt and the carving.
The moral of this story.....never give up and look both ways!

Thanks Isoscoles! We won's soon forget this one!
