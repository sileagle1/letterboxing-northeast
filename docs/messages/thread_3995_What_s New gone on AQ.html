From: uneksia | Date: 2009-03-26 16:24:10 UTC
in the top right had corner of your page, right under where it says "my page
you will find a button that says "add widgets". a widget is basically the
box that has a specific function, such as "newest traditional letterboxes".

if you click on the "add widgets" it will bring you to a page that you can
add which widget you want. to delete the widgets you don't want you would go
to "my page" and click on the x for that particular widget.

hope this helps.
