Fall Planning...

From: dthreedvm01 | Date: 2009-02-05 05:24:51 UTC
As seen on AQ, we are organizing a letterboxing event at scenic Dennis
Hill State Park in Norfolk, CT, scheduled for Saturday, October 17th,
starting at 10 a.m. and going until whenever. The park closes at
sunset, and overnight camping is not allowed there. More details are
available on the event listing on Atlas Quest, but in short:

The pavilion at the top of the hill has lots of room, picnic tables, a
working fireplace, and an amazing view, which will hopefully be near-
peak foliage color.

There will be several plants on the trails at the park, some of which
may be event-only.

Table boxes are welcome, and we are accepting raffle donations. Please
contact us if you are interested in planting trailside letterboxes, as
there are already a few in the works.

No parking fee!

Please bring a lunch or snack pot luck item to share.

The park could serve as an excellent starting point for exploration of
other NW CT parks, forests, and (of course) letterboxes.

There will be a birthday cake honoring a few young (under 5 y.o.)
letterboxers with October birthdays, as well as age-appropriate themed
boxes, too.

Hope some of you can make it!

Jonah's Whalers - with help from many other CT 'boxers!
Canton, CT
