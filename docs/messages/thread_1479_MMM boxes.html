From: btoesatbarker | Date: 2004-10-21 21:16:10 UTC
If any of you were out in the woods today in the Mansfield area, you
may have heard a delighted scream as I finished getting all the
monsters today!!! The last box for me was To Serve Man by Chuck and
Molly. In finding all of the monster boxes and all of the other boxes
along the same trails that I had not gotten until now, it brings my
counts to 880 finds, 211 exchanges, 42 plants, and 9 event/traveling
event boxes! I am so excited, I had to tell everyone! Thanks to all
of the wonderful people who spent so much of their time and put so
much work into carving stamps, writing clues and placing boxes. It
sure made my month.
Queen B
