From: Nancy | Date: 2004-10-27 16:51:55 UTC
After 8 trips to Mansfield from RI and endless miles, sometimes
lost I finally got all the monsters yesterday. When I went that
first day and saw the the amazing and creative boxes I was hooked
and knew I had to find them all. Yesterday I had about 20 left and
was determined to do them all. I was doing ok but knew I'd have to
hurry to finish. I was doing Wolf Rock after 5 and dusk was
appoaching when I got the brilliant idea to take a shortcut, don't
know where I ended up, there was serious beaver activity there, but
needless to say I had to backtrack and follow the directions out.
Now its 5:45 and I have 1 box left at white oak, don't want to do
the hour drive back for 1 box so I flew up there, the directions say
15 mins., I can do that before dark right. Found the box and could
barely read the book when I actually groaned when I found out I had
to find another with compass readings no less. Well I found the spot
took out my compass and can barely see but I think I found the right
tree and I'm walking around and around it but can't find any
opening. Go back and start over, must have read it wrong, nope
brings me to that same tree, well I did find it that time and
hurried out of the woods as it was getting really dark, I'm glad
there was a full moon, which probably explains my dumb behavior of
going in strange woods after dark to start with.
I want to think everyone involved for the great times I had with
all these monsters this past month, it was really alot of fun. Now
if the Red Sox win the world series, the month will really be
perfect. Chuck get ready to retire that box, just wish I could have
gotten it first, no I'm not going back to Ct today.
Thanks Guys, Prairie Girl
