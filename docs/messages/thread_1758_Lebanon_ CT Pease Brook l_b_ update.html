Lebanon, CT Pease Brook l.b. update

From: rospa10 | Date: 2005-01-17 22:19:56 UTC
Yesterday I found this box which was placed by Drewclan in 3/2000.
The box is cracked but totally dry inside thanks to it hidie-hole in
the wall. Ink pad fine-stamp reminds me of Elton John song "Blue
Canoe". If you do go here, please wear orange regardless of the
season. There are discarded deer parts in one corner of the lot: at
lease 3 deer heads, a ribcage and other parts in garbage bags which
leads me to believe this is an area where hunters are illegally
shooting deer. I saw lots of spent shotgun shells along the first
part of the trail, unfortunately. Also, please note that the trail
which crosses the powerlines on the way back to your car has many
overgrown prickers along it. On a positive note: I have never ever
seen such a grand sycamore tree! Jay was sure right about there being
many a near champion tree in our N.E. forests. Re. replacement box:
it has to be the same size so as to fit its' spot. It is a small
flat rectangular box with tan lid (maybe Glad brand??). Jay
frequently uses this size box.
