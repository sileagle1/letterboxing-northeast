Last time we posted photos from a gathering we got the same error
message. What it essentially means is that the site gets so many hits
on it and the data transfer (amount of info) exceeds an hourly limit.
Check back in an hour or two once the server re-sets itself.

Option #2 - Post to the LBNE or LBNA photos section (i know there is
room on the big list section because we just had a moderators
discussion and combed through it, openning 7 megabytes of space for
struff like this!)

Option #3 - Shrink the pictures and/or decrease the # pixels on your
camera to make less "data" that needs to be transferred. I.e make
your 5x7 photo a 3x5.

-Steve and Heidi
