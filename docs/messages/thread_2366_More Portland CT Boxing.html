Hey folks..Heads up!

Portland is having a Brownstone Festival on September 17th. There will
be all sorts of activities going on all around town, including canoe
rides in the quarry, a square dance, historic tours of brownstone
buildings in town and so much more! If you get the Rivereast news,
keep on the lookout for informationin the Portland section!

I have agreed to plant a letterbox or two (or more?) in the riverfront
park...for which I have planned nothing at this point! But I guess
I'll have to get on that! Anyone interested in information on boxes as
I get them prepared, let me know! Anyone got a box they want to plant
for the day?

Unfortunately the town has voted to allow a scuba diving company to
lease our quarries and some acreage of the riverfront park to make a
camping area for divers to come and train....the almighty dollar at
work, God forbid we have a natural, undisturbed, non-income producing
place to enjoy in town! So, not sure how much longer we'll have free
and open access to the area...if you haven't gotten Cozy Cavy's HH
hostel or Big Pine in Portland yet, I'd plan to soon...may have to
retire them. Speaking of Cozy Cavy...the cavy it is placed in honor of
died this summer unexpectedly...poor thing!
