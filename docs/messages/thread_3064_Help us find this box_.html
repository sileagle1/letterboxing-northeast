Help us find this box!

From: cpascott | Date: 2006-07-12 16:12:30 UTC
Hey all ...

In early November 2005, Team Randalstik and I planted the "Wolf
Hollow" box at the Wolf Hollow Sanctuary in Ipswich, MA. The clues,
resident on my website at
http://home.comcast.net/~scott.e.hall/clues/Wolf_Hollow.html have
also been posted to LbNA and AQ.

To date, no one has recorded finding (or attempting to find) the box
on either site. Now, while this does not necessarily indicate that
no one has, in fact, sought the box, I'd like to make sure it gets
at least one visit! Who knows, maybe you could still be first
finder after 8 months.

So, here's the request -- somebody, anybody, head on over to Ipswich
and let us know what you think of the sanctuary and the box!

Many thanks!

CPAScott

Busy week doing some letterbox maintenance.

newly carved stamp. BUT, #2 went missing.

than a week or two.
