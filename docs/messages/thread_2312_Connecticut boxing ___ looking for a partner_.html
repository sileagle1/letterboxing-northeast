Hey all ...

Although I don't mind 'boxing alone, most times I'd rather do it with
someone else -- especially if I'm going to drive a distance to get a box.

The great thing about hooking up with other letterboxers (other than
not having to box alone) is that I get to meet and make the
acquaintence of some great people and perhaps even develop a few
friendships along the way.

So far, I've had the pleasure to 'box with Warrior Woman, Choi, Tony &
Veronica, someone named Sue from Central MA, and, at the Mansfield
Monster Mash, Northstar, and Scarab.

I'll be headed out with Music Woman on Friday, and hopefully
connecting with Team Green Dragon this weekend.

And I still can't get enough!

Of all the boxes I've found so far, all have been in Massachusetts,
Rhode Island, New Hampshire, or New York, with the exception of some
68 found as part of the Mansfield Monster Mash. However, most of
Connecticut is "untapped" for me.

With so many boxes in that state, I'd like to go after some this
season, but only really want to if someone will come along with me.
You could already live in Connecticut and we could meet up for a full
day of 'boxing, or you could join me on an overnight with the camping
gear for a weekend of 'boxing.

At the moment I don't have any particular part of the state I'm more
interested in that others, so I'm open if you are. I was thinking of
trying to partner up with someone to head out in mid-September.

If anyone's interesting on planning a 'boxing day (or weekend) with
me, send me an e-mail off-list.

Many thanks!

CPAScott
