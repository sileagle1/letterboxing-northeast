Solstice Boxing Brunch

From: Laura and Chuck Lubelczyk | Date: 2006-05-22 14:56:26 UTC
Please join us at Hattieâ€™s Restaurant for boxing and exchanges on
Saturday, June 10th from 10 am to 2 pm rain or shine. This celebration
of the upcoming solstice and summer fun will include event-only boxes
carved by local letterboxers, a temporary hitchhiker hostel, and a few
traveling event stamps as well.
You may bring a picnic lunch or purchase breakfast or lunch at the
restaurant which is open from 8am to 2 pm (be sure to save room for
delicious homemade desserts). This is a beautiful coastal area to
explore and the beach is directly across the street. Bring sunscreen to
protect against those sunny skies, warm clothes to take the chill off
the seabreeze, binoculars since you'll be in one of the best loved
birdwatching spots, and of course, ink and stamps for exchanging. Most
importantly, bring your friends and family so you can meet fellow
boxers, new and experienced alike and celebrate the upcoming solstice in
true letterboxing style.

Organizers: Hattie (listed as Splish Splash on Atlas Quest), Nautilus,
and Oceanwytch with help from others on the Crazyboxingbuddies yahoo group.
Help with all aspects of planning including decorating, carving and
planting stamps, setup and cleanup, photographing the event, and raffle
donations are all welcome.
RSVPs would be appreciated.
There is nearby camping on route 9 at Beachwood Resort for adventurous
boxers from out of town. Nautilus and Culex may also have some space
available in their house for visitors.
Don't forget to bring clues with you to other nearby boxes such and
Phyto's Bird Walk and Isoceles' Higher Education.
