CT:Interest in Mini Gathering June 5th?

From: Steve and Heidi | Date: 2004-04-29 04:38:32 UTC
Last year we disorganizingly organized a mini-gathering at Montville
High School for the Relay fo Life (American Cancer Society benefit
event). This year the locale has changed to Norwich Free Academy.
We're considering breaking out the event stamp from last year and/or
carving a 2004 event stamp. Looking to find out if people are
interested in meeting up for this again. Here's what we had last
year:

Lots of friends, some exchanges, an event stamp, tons of HH, visits
by some rare travellers,several boxes in the surrounding town.

This year: The Relay starts at 2pm rather than 11am (long story but
its due to SAT scheduling). We could certainly work something out
for say noon on the green in Norwich and have a little get together.
There's SEVERAL boxes close-by and I might be persuaded into placing
a couple day-of boxes in Mohegan Park if I can get some help with
carving.

-Steve and Heidi

Note: This is all preliminary and brainstorming. Feel free to comment
on list or via prvate e-mail.
