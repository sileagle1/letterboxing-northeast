Events

From: oldhounder | Date: 2005-10-23 18:34:45 UTC
Siamese and I are relative newcomers to the letterboxing world and are
quite addicted.

We appreciate the time, talent, good spirit, and kind-heartedness we
have found within the community. These qualities show at their peak in
the gatherings and events that we have attended. The apparent effort
that has gone into these is tremendous and we owe the folks that have
put these events on a debt of gratitude.

It would be too easy to leave someone out in listing those for whom we
have praise because events obviously are not the work of just of few.
We appreciate them all, the seen and those in the background who give
of their time and share the load.

So far we have attended Frightnight at the Fort in Kittery, the
Belfast event, and the Pink Flamingo Caper in Leominster. They were
all wonderful. Each was the best for different reasons.

Most of all, I like the contact with the people and am awed by the
talent of carvers, organizers, and planters. It's Swonderful,
Mahvalous what you do for us.

oldhounder
