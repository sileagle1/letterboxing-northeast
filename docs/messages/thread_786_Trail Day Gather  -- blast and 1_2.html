Trail Day Gather -- blast and 1/2

From: Northstar | Date: 2004-06-06 16:42:41 UTC
Fun was had by all yesterday as the weather was not all it was
cracked up to be. All who went the distance into this morning were
treated to a quartet of event stamps comemmerating each stage of the
evening. Then it was off to the nighttime letterboxing,("Things that
go bump in the Night") another five oh Alan's treats hidden off in
the dark of night. Flshlights in hand, we all braved the endless
darkness to collect the images. After returning from our adventure,
we were pleasantly surprised to find that Erik the Ram had stopped by
to say hello. Then, just when you thought it was all over, there was
a rustle in the bushes and who should sail in at the very last
minute, but Wanda and Pete!! Fresh off a plane from Seattle, they set
up camp and collected the images into their books before going to
bed. Stories by the campfire, fresh roasted smores and lots of
exchanges filled in the rest of the day and night- and at the end, no
one was sorry they had braved the trip. It was an excellent gather
and great fun for everyone. I would do it all again in a new york
minute. Thanks Alan, you're the best. ---Northstar
