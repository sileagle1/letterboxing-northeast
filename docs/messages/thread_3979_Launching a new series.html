Launching a new series

From: AC | Date: 2009-02-12 04:48:34 UTC
Hi All,



I just wanted to announce the launching of a new series. Newburgh, NY is having year long party, in
celebration of the 400th Anniversary of Henrik Hudson's and Samuel de Champlain's sails up river,
the 300th anniversary of the settling of Newburgh and the 200th anniversary of Fulton's steamboat
trip from NYC to Albany (2 years after the fact), the Palatine Shop at 87 Liberty Street is issuing
a limited edition set of commemorative postcards throughout 2009. In conjunction with the
postcards, I will be launching a new letterbox for each one. Some indoors, some outdoors, some easy
some challenging.. Box #1 C. K. Chatterton is in place. Boxes 2 - 4 will be placed within the next
day or so and subsequent boxes will be placed within a few days of the launch of each new card. Pop
on over to Atlas Quest and check them out.
