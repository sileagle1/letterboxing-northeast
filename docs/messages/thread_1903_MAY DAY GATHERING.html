MAY DAY GATHERING

From: MommaFox | Date: 2005-03-28 12:21:58 UTC
I am so sorry to have caused such a comotion on the list with everyone! I just want to clear up a few things.

NO, I didn't cancel because of the April Drive. I realize that people can't always make a Sunday Gathering and some people don't care for the drives so this probably wouldn't affect the Gathering.

NO, there is nothing wrong with anyone in my family, everyone is fine and healthy.

Help with the event is not even an issue, I have had many, many offers of help from several people. Thank You to all who offered!!

My husband feels that we are overly busy and that now that I work full time I might not have the time to organize this, I don't agree! I would never plan something without the time to do so. He also does not have as great an interest in Letterboxing as I do (please don't tell him I told you !)

I do have some people helping me and I will rethink my decision to hold the gathering. I was looking forward to seeing many of you again and meeting new people just like all of you were.

The plans I had so far really made for an easy time of it gathering wise (does this make sense!) I don't want to say too much and give it away just in case we decide to hold it. There will be a bit of mystery involved. I know some of you were concerned where is was being held (part of the mystery) I can tell you it will be in the general area of last year.

I will decide what to do in the next few days and get back to everyone next week after my weekend trip to Washington DC to see my grandson.

In the meantime, PLEASE be nice or I might have to cancel for good if I hear you were all mean to each other!!
