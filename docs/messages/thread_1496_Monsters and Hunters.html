Monsters and Hunters

From: MayEve | Date: 2004-10-25 01:14:59 UTC
Hi All,
I know this subject has been brought up before, but being as next
weekend will be the last for "Mashing", just a reminder to be safe
and well prepared out on the trails. Mom of 3 and I hit the trails
very early this Saturday morning, and after being treated to the
company of three beautiful deer out behind Lions Memorial Park, we
met up with <drumroll> the hunters! No, we did not have on our
brilliant neon orange attire <tsk tsk>, not thinking we were in open
territory. The hunters explained that they were not hunting deer,
but pheasant! As we were walking the same trail, they (and their
assorted muddy canine companions) assured us that they'd be on the
lookout for "boxers" in the woods and thanked us for the heads up.
They did encourage us to wear orange, and asked that we pass their
plea on to fellow boxers because although they hunted cautiously,
they told us many do not. We met up with "the hunters" several
times throughout the morning, and what might have been an
unfortunate experience turned into an enlightening and educational
one. So, as the final days of Mashing fast approach us, please be
aware that more than Monsters lurk in the Mansfield woods!
Now, let's BOX!! MayEve
