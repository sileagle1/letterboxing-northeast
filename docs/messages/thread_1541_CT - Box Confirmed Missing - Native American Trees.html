CT - Box Confirmed Missing - Native American Trees and Traditions Series

From: T Prindle | Date: 2004-11-01 22:42:17 UTC
Thank you to the boxer that gave me the heads up that the Tamarack
box (#2) of my Native American Trees and Traditons is missing. I
went to look for it this afternoon, and indeed, it was not in it's
spot... I found some of it's contents dragged and chewed across the
grove of spruces at the top of the hill... this is what it looks
like now:

http://nativetech.org/lb/tamarackbox.jpg

I searched and searched for the actual stamp so I could replace the
box... but only found the bags, box, stamp pad and tiny chewed pages
of the log book. The animal apparantly took the whole bag/box up the
hill from the boulder at 200 degrees, straight into the grove,
chewing parts along the way. I also found a pair of scissors and an
old milk jug severly chewed on, so this is a favorite spot of this
animal to bring found items (I would say a coyote from the size of
the scat in the area).

Please I beg all of you boxers new and old alike, please put
something HEAVY on top of the box. No plans to recarve this stamp,
it was truely one of a kind.

Rush Gatherer
The Dawnlanders
