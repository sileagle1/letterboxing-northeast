CT-Lebanon Fair Boxes

From: Sandy Leclerc | Date: 2005-08-10 20:49:33 UTC
Just a second posting regarding the boxes at the Lebanon Fair this coming
weekend. They will be available only during the fair which is being held
Friday, Saturday and Sunday. The clues will be posted either Thursday eveing
or Friday afternoon, depending on the placement of the boxes. If you have
any questions, just ask! The fee to enter the fair is $6.00, $5.00 for
seniors and kids under twelve are free. There will be at least 2 tiger &
lion shows each day, along with the normal country fair goings-on. Hope to
see you there.

Lebanon Fair Boxes

From: Steve and Heidi | Date: 2008-08-12 01:05:12 UTC
Just a thanks to Queen B for planting the Lebanon Fair letterboxes.
After almost 2 1/2 years of very rare letterboxing, we actually went
to the fair and found this little series! Baby steps!

Unfortunately, we were there while the horseshoe tournament was going
on when we tried to get the ''Ringer'' box, so we missed that
one.....but 2 out of 3 isn't a bad day either!

Garrett's almost 3 now and keeps asking to go hiking in the woods, so
hopefully the cooler autumn weather will draw us back the the hobby we
love so much. It's taken a real back seat to the game of life the past
couple of years, but I think we're going to make an effort to get back
into it a little bit......

Again, Thanks!

Steve and Heidi (And Garrett)
