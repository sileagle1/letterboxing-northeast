I'm not sure about clues - other than printing some in the area for them and making a booklet. However, another item you can get is a book entitled "The Letterboxer's Companion" by Randy Hall. I got my copy at Barnes and Noble. It describes almost everything you need to know about letterboxing. It's a handy tool that gets into the basics and beyond in letterboxing. I paid $14.95 for my copy.

Thanks and Enjoy!
