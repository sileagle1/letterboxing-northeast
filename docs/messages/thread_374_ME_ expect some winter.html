Just a heads up to you folks "from away"...
Southern Maine is currently under a "winter storm watch" so that includes the York
County area. They are due for 3-5 inches Tuesday night through Wednesday and
another storm that will carry over from Thursday night to Friday during the day.

I'm not sure how this set of storms will impact your driving and travel plans for the
letterboxing at Laudholm event, but I do hope you all will use caution. Winter is FAR
from over in Maine.

For more info on local weather see:http://www.crh.noaa.gov/forecasts/
MEZ023.php?warncounty=MEC023&city=Wells
