Program on LB at our local library

From: SawnSJ@aol.com | Date: 2005-01-17 15:50:27 UTC
Tom and I are doing an Introduction to Letterboxing at the Enfield Public
Library tomorrow night at 7:00 p.m. Late last year we worked with one of the
librarians to plant a box in the library and got her intrigued so she asked us
to do the program.

We're going to talk a little about the history of LBing, what you need to
get started, talk about etiquette, how to use a compass, and how to access
clues on the computer. Kristen, the librarian, carves stamps for a hobby so
she's going to do a demo. If we have time, we'll do a "field trip" to the
library's letterbox!

Thoughts on anything else that we should include?
