For those of you that may not realize, there has been a huge effort
underway for many weeks, spearheaded by Pine Tree, to revive and
revitalize Carolyn Stearn's [Leader of the Pack] letterboxes. Carolyn
was instrumental in introducing letterboxing to the Mansfield area in
the late 1990's and early 2000, thus influencing and encouraging
planting in other parts of the state.

Anyone who has any information of ANY of her boxes, please contact
Pine Tree on this list.

Kudos to Pine Tree and her crew for their efforts in their maintenance
project to preserve these early CT boxes from one of our state's
pioneers.

Thanks to you all!!!
